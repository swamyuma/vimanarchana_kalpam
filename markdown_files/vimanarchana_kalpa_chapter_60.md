# Chapter 60: Iconography and Consecration of Mānuṣa-Vāsudeva, Daivika-Vāsudeva, and Pictorial Images (Mānuṣa-vāsudevādi-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules for the Dimensions, Iconography, and Consecration of Mānuṣa-Vāsudeva, Daivika-Vāsudeva, and Painted Deities (Ālekhya-devāḥ)

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ षष्टितमः पटलः**
> **मानुष-वासुदेवादि-विधिः -**
> ***atha ṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***mānuṣa-vāsudevādi-vidhiḥ -***
> 
> **(मानुषवासुदेवस्य लक्षणम्)**
> अथ मानुषवासुदेवम् आयताश्रे विमाने मध्यम-दशतालमितं द्विभुजं शङ्खचक्रधरं; दक्षिणे रुक्मिणीदेवीं; तद्-दक्षिणे हल-मुसलधरं बलभद्रं; तद्-दक्षिणे प्रद्युम्नं द्विभुजं, क्षुरिकाधर-दक्षिणहस्तं, कट्यवलम्बित-वामहस्तं; तद्-दक्षिणे विरिञ्चिं चतुर्बाहुं चतुराननम्; देवस्य वामपार्श्वे अनिरुद्धं द्विभुजं खड्ग-खेटकधरं; तद्-वामे साम्बं द्विभुजं दान-दक्षिणकरं; तद्-वामे गरुडं च कारयेत्।
> ***(mānuṣavāsudevasya laxaṇam)***
> *atha mānuṣavāsudevam āyatāśre vimāne madhyama-daśatālamitaṃ dvibhujaṃ śaṅkhacakradharaṃ; daxiṇe rukmiṇīdevīṃ; tad-daxiṇe hala-musaladharaṃ balabhadraṃ; tad-daxiṇe pradyumnaṃ dvibhujaṃ, xurikādhara-daxiṇahastaṃ, kaṭyavalambita-vāmahastaṃ; tad-daxiṇe viriñciṃ caturbāhuṃ caturānanam; devasya vāmapārśve aniruddhaṃ dvibhujaṃ khaḍga-kheṭakadharaṃ; tad-vāme sāmbaṃ dvibhujaṃ dāna-daxiṇakaraṃ; tad-vāme garuḍaṃ ca kārayet.*
> 
> तेषां वर्ण-अम्बर-आभरणानि भृगूक्तविधानेन कारयेत्। देवान् एतान् स्थितान् एव संस्थाप्य, तद्रूपं कौतुकं कारयेत्। अथवा विष्णुं चतुर्भुजम् एवं संस्थापयेत् ॥
> *teṣāṃ varṇa-ambara-ābharaṇāni bhṛgūktavidhānena kārayet. devān etān sthitān eva saṃsthāpya, tadrūpaṃ kautukaṃ kārayet. athavā viṣṇuṃ caturbhujam evaṃ saṃsthāpayet.*
> 
> **(दैविकवासुदेवस्य लक्षणम्)**
> दैविकं वासुदेवं सिंहासने समासीनं चतुर्भुजं शङ्खचक्रधरं श्री-भूमिभ्यां सहितं; तथा दक्षिणवामयोः पूर्वोक्तान् देवान् बलभद्रस्य दक्षिणे देवीं रेवतीं, प्रद्युम्नस्य रोहिणीं, अनिरुद्धस्य उषां, साम्बस्य इन्दुकरीं च सहैव स्थापयेत्। एतेषां कौतुकबिम्बानि पूर्ववत् कारयेत् ॥
> ***(daivikavāsudevasya laxaṇam)***
> *daivikaṃ vāsudevaṃ siṃhāsane samāsīnaṃ caturbhujaṃ śaṅkhacakradharaṃ śrī-bhūmibhyāṃ sahitaṃ; tathā daxiṇavāmayoh pūrvoktān devān balabhadrasya daxiṇe devīṃ revatīṃ, pradyumnasya rohiṇīṃ, aniruddhasya uṣāṃ, sāmbasya indukarīṃ ca sahāiva sthāpayet. eteṣāṃ kautukabimbāni pūrvavat kārayet.*
> 
> **(वासुदेवप्रतिष्ठा)**
> एतेषां प्रतिष्ठाविशेषं वक्ष्ये — पूर्वोक्त-यागशाला-मध्ये शय्यावेदिं परितः पञ्चाग्नीन् पौण्डरीकाग्निं च कृत्वा; अन्वाहार्यस्य दक्षिणे च आवसथ्यस्य उत्तरे च औपासनाग्निकुण्डे कृत्वा; वासुदेवयोः सभ्ये, बलभद्रस्य आहवनीये, प्रद्युम्नस्य अन्वाहार्ये, अनिरुद्धस्य गार्हपत्ये, साम्बस्य आवसथ्ये, दक्षिणोत्तरयोः औपासनाग्निकुण्डयोश्च हौत्रं प्रशंस्य; 'भद्रेशं पुण्यमूर्तिं वासुदेवं पुण्यात्मकम्' इति वैदिकवासुदेवस्य; देव्योः मुन्योः च पूर्ववत्।
> ***(vāsudevapratiṣṭhā)***
> *eteṣāṃ pratiṣṭhāviśeṣaṃ vakṣye — pūrvokta-yāgāśālā-madhye śayyāvediṃ paritaḥ pañcāgnīn pouṇḍarīkāgniṃ ca kṛtvā; anvāhāryasya daxiṇe ca āvasathyasya uttare ca aupāsanāgnikuṇḍe kṛtvā; vāsudevayoh sabhye, balabhadrasya āhavanīye, pradyumnasya anvāhārye, aniruddhasya gārhapatye, sāmbasya āvasathye, daxiṇottarayoh aupāsanāgnikuṇḍayōśca houtraṃ praśaṃsya; 'bhadreśaṃ puṇyamūrtiṃ vāsudevaṃ puṇyātmakam' iti vaidicavāsudevasya; devyoh munyoh ca pūrvavat.*
> 
> 'बलिनं हलधरं नागराजं सङ्कर्षणम्' इति बलभद्रस्य; 'रोहिणीपतिं प्रद्युम्नं वीरं बलशासकम्' (मुद्रितपाठे बलशासनं) इति प्रद्युम्नस्य; 'पुरुषं सत्यम् अच्युतम् अनिरुद्धम्' इति अनिरुद्धस्य; 'साम्बं जाम्बवतीसुतं भद्रं सुन्दरम्' इति साम्बस्य; पितामह-गरुडयोश्च पूर्ववत् मूर्तिमन्त्रैः आवाहनादीनि कृत्वा; सभ्याग्नौ वासुदेवयोः पुरुषसूक्तम्, आहवनीये विष्णुसूक्तम्, अन्वाहार्ये वैष्णवं, गार्हपत्ये विष्णुगायत्रीम्, आवसथ्ये च एकाक्षरादिसूक्तं च प्रत्येकं दशकृत्वः; ब्रह्मणो ब्राह्मं, गरुडस्य गारुडं तथा हुत्वा; अन्यत् सर्वं पूर्ववत् आचरेत् ॥
> *'balinaṃ haladharaṃ nāgarājaṃ saṅkarṣaṇam' iti balabhadrasya; 'rohiṇīpatiṃ pradyumnaṃ vīraṃ balaśāsakam' iti pradyumnasya; 'puruṣaṃ satyam acyutam aniruddham' iti aniruddhasya; 'sāmbaṃ jāmbavatīsutaṃ bhadraṃ sundaram' iti sāmbasya; pitāmaha-garuḍayōśca pūrvavat mūrtimantraiḥ āvāhanādīni kṛtvā; sabhyāgnou vāsudevayoh puruṣasūktam, āhavanīye viṣṇusūktam, anvāhārye vaiṣṇavaṃ, gārhapatye viṣṇugāyatrīm, āvasathye ca ekākṣarādisūktaṃ ca pratyekaṃ daśakṛtvah; brahmaṇo brāhmaṃ, garuḍasya gāruḍaṃ tathā hutvā; anyat sarvaṃ pūrvavat ācaret.*
> 
> **(आलेख्यदेवानां विधिः)**
> अथ आलेख्यदेवानां स्थापनविधिं वक्ष्ये — द्विविधम् आलेख्यं भवति, पटे भित्तौ च। तेषां मानाङ्गुलेन समाङ्गुलात् द्व्यङ्गुलमानेषु इष्टमानं विनिश्चित्य; पश्चात् तदुक्त-ताल-विभागेन साङ्गोपाङ्गं तत्-तत्-वर्ण-अम्बर-आभरण-आद्यैर्युक्तं, नयनानन्दं, समालिख्य, शुभे नक्षत्रे स्थापनं कारयेत् ॥
> ***(ālekhyadevānāṃ vidhiḥ)***
> *atha ālekhyadevānāṃ sthāpanavidhiṃ vakṣye — dvividham ālekhyaṃ bhavati, paṭe bhittou ca. teṣāṃ mānāṅgulena samāṅgulāt dvyāṅgulamāneṣu iṣṭamānaṃ viniścitya; paścāt tadukta-tāla-vibhāgena sāṅgōpāṅgaṃ tat-tat-varṇa-ambara-ābharaṇa-ādyairyuktaṃ, nayanānandaṃ, samāliṣya, śubhe naxatre sthāpanaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> **(आलेख्यदेवानां प्रतिष्ठा)**
> तद्दिनात् पूर्वम् अङ्कुरान् अर्पयित्वा; सम्भारान् आहृत्य; पूर्वेद्युः एव पूर्वाह्णे तत्-तद्-देवाभिमुखे आहवनीयाग्निकुण्डं च कृत्वा; आधारान्ते वास्तुहोम-अङ्गहोमौ हुत्वा; पूर्ववद् अक्षिमोचनं (मुद्रितपाठे अक्षिमोधनं) कृत्वा; अग्निं सुसंरक्ष्य; बेरशुद्ध्यर्थं उत्पूतराधावैः (तीर्थोदकैः) पवमानाद्यैः समभ्युक्ष्य; पुण्याहं वाचयित्वा; तद्-रात्रौ अग्निं प्रज्वाल्य परिषिच्य; वैष्णवं व्याहृत्यन्तं हुत्वा; तत्-तद्-देवाभिमुखे धान्योपरि कुम्भं सन्यस्य; तत्-तद्रूपं मनसा ध्यात्वा कुम्भेऽम्भसि समावाह्य; पूर्ववत् समकलशैः तत्-तत्-कुम्भं बिम्बं च अभ्युक्ष्य; व्रीहिभिः स्थण्डिलं कृत्वा; वस्त्राणि आस्तीर्य; प्रतिसरबन्धनं शयनं च समन्त्रकं कृत्वा; अग्निं परिस्तीर्य; हौत्रं प्रशंस्य; तत्-तन्-मूर्तिमन्त्रैः आवाहनादीनि कृत्वा; तत्-तत्-मन्त्रैः अष्टाधिकशतम् आवर्त्य हुत्वा; रात्रिशेषं व्यपोह्य; प्रभाते स्नात्वा देवान् प्रणम्य; मुहूर्ते प्राप्ते तत्-तत्-मन्त्रेण स्थापनं कृत्वा; तत्-तद्-हृदये तत्-तद्-बीजाक्षरं न्यस्य; तत्-तत्-मन्त्रेण तत्-तत्-कुम्भोदकेन आवाह्य; पुण्याहान्ते अभिमुखे देशे (धान्यपीठे) देवम् अभ्यर्च्य, हविः निवेदयेत् ॥
> ***(ālekhyadevānāṃ pratiṣṭhā)***
> *taddināt pūrvam aṅkurān arpayitvā; sambhārān āhṛtya; pūrvedyuh eva pūrvāhṇe tat-tad-devābhimuxe āhavanīyāgnikuṇḍaṃ ca kṛtvā; ādhārānte vāstuhoma-aṅgahomou hutvā; pūrvavad aximocanaṃ kṛtvā; agniṃ susaṃraxya; beraśuddhyarthaṃ utpūtarādhāvaih pavamānādyaih samabhyuxya; puṇyāhaṃ vācayitvā; tad-rātrou agniṃ prajvālya pariṣicya; vaiṣṇavaṃ vyāhṛtyantaṃ hutvā; tat-tad-devābhimuxe dhānyōpari kumbhaṃ sanyasya; tat-tadrūpaṃ manasā dhyātvā kumbhe'mbhasi samāvāhya; pūrvavat samakalaśaih tat-tat-kumbhaṃ bimbaṃ ca abhyuxya; vrīhibhiḥ sthaṇḍilaṃ kṛtvā; vastrāṇi āstīrya; pratisarabandhanaṃ śayanaṃ ca samantrakam kṛtvā; agniṃ paristīrya; houtraṃ praśaṃsya; tat-tan-mūrtimantraih āvāhanādīni kṛtvā; tat-tat-mantraih aṣṭādhikaśatam āvartya hutvā; rātriśeṣaṃ vyapōhya; prabhāte snātvā devān praṇamya; muhūrte prāpte tat-tat-mantreṇa sthāpanaṃ kṛtvā; tat-tad-hṛdaye tat-tad-bījāxaraṃ nyasya; tat-tat-mantreṇa tat-tat-kumbhodeakena āvāhya; puṇyāhānte abhimuxe deaśe (dhānyapīṭhe) devam abhyarcya, haviḥ nivedayet.*
> 
> आचार्यादिभ्यो दक्षिणां दद्यात्। नित्यार्चनम् आचरेद् इति आह मरीचिः ॥
> *ācāryādibhyo daxiṇāṃ dadyāt. nityārcanam ācared iti āha marīciḥ.*
> 
> कर्षणादि-प्रतिष्ठान्तं, प्रतिष्ठादि-अर्चनान्तम्, अर्चनादि-स्नपनान्तं, स्नपनादि-उत्सवान्तं प्रकरणं समाप्तम् ॥
> *karṣaṇādi-pratiṣṭhāntaṃ, pratiṣṭhādi-arcanāntam, arcanādi-snapanāntam, snapanādi-utsavāntam prakaraṇaṃ samāptam.*

#### English Translation
*   **Iconography of Mānuṣa-Vāsudeva:** Now, the description of Mānuṣa-Vāsudeva (human Vāsudeva): in the temple with a rectangular structure (*āyatāśra*), He is made in the *madhyama-daśatāla* (medium ten-span, 120 finger-units) system, has two arms, holds the conch and disc. On His right is Goddess Rukmiṇī. Further to His right is Balarāma (*Balabhadra*) holding a plow and pestle. Further to His right is the two-armed Pradyumna, holding a small sword (*xurikā*) in His right hand, His left hand resting on His hip. Further to His right is the four-armed, four-faced Brahmā (*Viriñci*). On the left side of Vāsudeva is the two-armed Aniruddha, holding a sword and shield. Further to His left is the two-armed Sāmba, with His right hand in the gesture of giving (*dāna-daxiṇa-kara*). Further to His left is Garuḍa. Their complexions, garments, and ornaments should be made according to the rules of Bhṛgu. Having established these deities in a standing posture, make the processional icon (*kautuka*) in the same form, or establish the four-armed Viṣṇu.

*   **Iconography of Daivika-Vāsudeva:** Daivika-Vāsudeva (divine Vāsudeva) is seated on a throne, has four arms, holds the conch and disc, and is accompanied by Goddesses Śrī and Bhū. On His right and left sides, the previously mentioned deities are placed: beside Balarāma is Goddess Revatī, beside Pradyumna is Rohiṇī, beside Aniruddha is Uṣā, and beside Sāmba is Indukarī, all established together. The processional icons of these deities should be made as before.

*   **Consecration of Vāsudeva:** I shall explain the specific consecration of these deities: in the middle of the sacrificial hall, set up the bedding altar and the five fires around it, and also the Pauṇḍarīka fire. To the right of the Anvāhāryā fire and to the left of the Āvasathya fire, set up two Aupāsana fire-pits. Offer the chanter's praise at the primary Sabhya fire for Vāsudeva, Āhavanīya for Balarāma, Anvāhāryā for Pradyumna, Gārhapatya for Aniruddha, Āvasathya for Sāmba, and at the two Aupāsana fire-pits: invoke Vāsudeva reciting "Bhadreśaṃ puṇyamūrtiṃ vāsudevaṃ puṇyātmakam", and invoke the Goddesses and sages as before. Invoke Balarāma reciting "Balinaṃ haladharaṃ nāgarājaṃ saṅkarṣaṇam". Invoke Pradyumna reciting "Rohiṇīpatiṃ pradyumnaṃ vīraṃ balaśāsanam". Invoke Aniruddha reciting "Puruṣaṃ satyam acyutam aniruddham". Invoke Sāmba reciting "Sāmbaṃ jāmbavatīsutaṃ bhadraṃ sundaram". Invoke Brahmā and Garuḍa as before. Offer fire oblations: in the Sabhya fire for the Vāsudeva deities with the Puruṣa-sūkta, in the Āhavanīya with the Viṣṇu-sūkta, in the Anvāhāryā with the Vaiṣṇava-mantra, in the Gārhapatya with the Viṣणu-gāyatrī, and in the Āvasathya with the Ekākṣara-sūkta, ten times each. For Brahmā, offer with the Brāhma-mantra, and for Garuḍa with the Gāruḍa-mantra. Perform all other rites as before.

*   **Rules for Pictorial Images (*Ālekhya-devāḥ*):** I shall now explain the rules for installing painted deities (*ālekhya-devāḥ*): painting is of two types—on cloth (*paṭa*) or on a wall (*bhitti*). Using the patron's finger units, select an appropriate height from one finger-unit to two finger-units, and divide it into the specified tala proportions. The painting should be complete with all limbs, complexions, garments, and ornaments, pleasing to the eyes (*nayanananda*), and should be installed under an auspicious star.

*   **Consecration of Painted Deities:** On the day before the consecration, offer sprouts, gather the materials, set up the Āhavanīya fire-pit in front of the deity in the morning, offer the Vāstu-homa and Aṅga-homa, perform the eye-opening (*axi-mocana*) as before, and protect the fire. To purify the image, sprinkle it with purified water while reciting the Pavamāna and other hymns, perform the *puṇyāha* purification, kindle the fire at night, perform the *pariṣecana* circumambulation, and offer oblations with the Vyāhṛti and Vaiṣṇava mantras. Place a water-pot on grain in front of each deity, meditate on their form in the mind, and invoke them into the water of the pot. Sprinkle each pot and image with the main water-pots, build a grain pedestal, spread garments, tie the protective thread (*pratisara-bandhana*), place them on the bedding altar with mantras, circumambulate the fire, perform the chanter's praise, and invoke the deities with their specific form-mantras. Offer one hundred and eight oblations with their specific mantras. Keep vigil for the rest of the night. In the morning, bathe, bow to the deities, and at the auspicious moment, perform the installation. In the heart of the deity, place the specific seed-syllables (*bījākṣara*). Invoke the deity with the water of the respective pot while reciting their specific mantras. At the end of the purification, worship the deity on the grain pedestal (*dhānya-pīṭha*) and offer cooked food (*havis*). Give donations to the priest and others. Perform the daily worship—so says Marīci.

*   **Conclusion of the Section:** The section covering the rites from plowing to consecration, from consecration to worship, from worship to bathing, and from bathing to festivals, is completed.
