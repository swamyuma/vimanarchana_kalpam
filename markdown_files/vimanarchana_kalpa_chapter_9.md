# Vimanarchana Kalpa of Sage Marīci (Vaikhānasa Āgama)
## Chapter 9: Ekabhūmi-Vidhāna-Vidhiḥ (Rules for Single-Tier Temples)

This document contains the Sanskrit text, IAST transliteration, and English translation of the 9th Chapter (*Navamaḥ Paṭalaḥ*) of the **Vimānārcanākalpa** (attributed to Sage Marīci, representing the Vaikhānasa school of Vaishnavism).

This chapter details the architectural and iconographic guidelines for single-tiered temples (*ekabhūmi-vimāna*). It defines the five heights (Śāntika, Pauṣṭika, Jayada, Adbhuta, and Sārvakāmika) under the sixfold (*ṣaḍ-varga*) and fourfold (*catur-varga*) divisions, the dimensions of the inner sanctum (*garbhagṛha*), the front porch (*mukha-maṇṭapa*), the half-hall (*ardha-maṇṭapa*), columns and pilasters (*pāda*), brackets (*bodhikā*), doorframes (*dvāra*), door leaves (*kavāṭa*), entablature (*prastara*), neck (*grīvā*), dome (*śikhara*), dormer windows (*nāsikā*), and the finial (*sthūpikā*). It also includes mathematical/astrological checking formulas (*āyādi-parīkṣā*) and the specific placement of subsidiary deities on the walls and roof.

---

### Section 1: Proportions of Single-Tier Vimāna

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ नवमः पटलः**
> **एकतलविमाने शान्तिक-लक्षणम्**
> ***atha navamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***ekatalavimāne śāntikalakṣaṇam***
> 
> अथ एकभूमिं वक्ष्ये - अधिष्ठान-पाद-प्रस्तर-ग्रीवा-शिखर-स्तूपिकाः च इति षड्वर्गयुक्ता एकभूमिः; अथवा चतुर्वर्गयुता स्यात्। शान्तिकोत्सेधं षडंशं कृत्वा, एकांशम् अधिष्ठानं, द्व्यंशं पादतुङ्गं, द्व्यंशं शिखरम्, एकांशं स्थूपिकोदयम्; एवं चतुर्वर्गयुतम्; अथवा शिखरक्रियां लुपाविधानेन कारयेत्।
> *atha ekabhūmiṃ vakṣye - adhiṣṭhāna-pāda-prastara-grīvā-śikhara-sthūpikāś ca iti ṣaḍvargayuktā ekabhūmiḥ; athavā caturvargayutā syāt. śāntikotsedhaṃ ṣaḍaṃśaṃ kṛtvā, ekāṃśam adhiṣṭhānaṃ, dvyaṃśaṃ pādatuṅgaṃ, dvyaṃśaṃ śikharama ekāṃśaṃ sthūpikodayam; evaṃ caturvargayutam; athavā śikharakriyāṃ lupāvidhānena kārayet.*
> 
> शान्तिकोत्सेधम् अष्टभागं कृत्वा - एकांशम् अधिष्ठानं, [पादं वा, तदुपरि प्रस्तरम् एकांशं, पादोन-त्र्यंशं शिखरं, एकांशं स्थूपिकोदयं स्यात्] तद्विगुणं स्तम्भायामं, तदुपरि प्रस्तरम् एकांशं, कण्ठम् एकांशं, द्व्यंशं शिखरं, एकांशं स्थूपिकोदयं स्यात्।
> *śāntikotsedham aṣṭabhāgaṃ kṛtvā - ekāṃśam adhiṣṭhānaṃ, [pādaṃ vā, tadupari prastaramekaṃśaṃ, pādonatryaṃśaṃ śikharaṃ, ekāṃśaṃ sthūpikodayaṃ syāt] tadviguṇaṃ stambhāyāmaṃ, tadupari prastaramekaṃśaṃ, kaṇṭhamekāṃśaṃ, dvyaṃśaṃ śikharama ekāṃśaṃ sthūpikodayaṃ syāt.*
> 
> **पौष्टिक-लक्षणम्**
> पौष्टिकोत्सेधं नवभागं कृत्वा - एकांशम् अधिष्ठानं, सपाद-द्व्यंशं पादायामं, प्रस्तरं पादोन-अंशं, सपाद-अंशं कण्ठं, पादोन-त्र्यंशं शिखरं, एकांशं स्थूपिका च स्यात्।
> ***pauṣṭikalakṣaṇam***
> *pauṣṭikotsedhaṃ navabhāgaṃ kṛtvā - ekāṃśam adhiṣṭhānaṃ, sapāda-dvyaṃśaṃ pādatuṅgaṃ, prastaraṃ pādona-aṃśaṃ, sapāda-aṃśaṃ kaṇṭhaṃ, pādona-tryaṃśaṃ śikharaṃ, ekāṃśā sthūpikā ca syāt.*
> 
> **जयद-लक्षणम्**
> जयदोत्सेधं दशांशं कृत्वा - एकांशम् अधिष्ठानं, द्व्यर्धांशं पादतुङ्गं, एकांशं प्रस्तरं, सार्ध-एकांशं कण्ठं, त्र्यंशं शिखरं, एकांशं स्थूपितुङ्गं स्यात्।
> ***jayadalakṣaṇam***
> *jayadotsedhaṃ daśāṃśaṃ kṛtvā - ekāṃśam adhiṣṭhānaṃ, dvyardhāṃśaṃ pādatuṅgaṃ, ekāṃśaṃ prastaraṃ, sārdha-ekāṃśaṃ kaṇṭhaṃ, tryaṃśaṃ śikharaṃ, ekāṃśaṃ sthūpituṅgaṃ syāt.*
> 
> **अद्भुत-लक्षणम्**
> अद्भुतोत्सेधम् एकादशांशं कृत्वा - सपाद-अंशम् अधिष्ठानं, तद्विगुणं स्तम्भायामं, तदुपरि प्रस्तरम् एकांशं, सार्ध-अंशं कण्ठं, त्र्यंशं शिखरं, सपाद-अंशं स्थूपिकोदयं स्यात्।
> ***adbhutalakṣaṇam***
> *adbhutotsedham ekādaśāṃśaṃ kṛtvā - sapāda-aṃśam adhiṣṭhānaṃ, tadviguṇaṃ stambhāyāmaṃ, tadupari prastaramekaṃśaṃ, sārdha-aṃṃśaṃ kaṇṭhaṃ, tryaṃśaṃ śikharaṃ, sapāda-aṃśaṃ sthūpikodayaṃ syāt.*
> 
> **सार्वकामिक-लक्षणम्**
> द्वादशांशं कृत्वा - सपाद-अंशम् अधिष्ठानं, द्विगुणं स्तम्भायामं, सपाद-अंशं प्रस्तरं, सपाद-अंशं कण्ठं, [पादं वा, तदुपरि प्रस्तरम् एकांशं, एकांशं कण्ठं, पादोन-त्र्यंशं शिखरं, एकांशं स्थूपिकोदयं स्यात्]; अद्भुतोत्सेधम् अपि एवं योजयेत् इति केचित्।
> अथवा शान्तिक-पौष्टिक-जयदानां उत्सेधं क्रमेण सम-षट्-पञ्च-भागं कृत्वा, तत्तद् एकांशं हित्वा, समारोपयेत् इति एके।
> ***sārvakāmikalakṣaṇam***
> *dvādaśāṃśaṃ kṛtvā - sapāda-aṃśam adhiṣṭhānaṃ, dviguṇaṃ stambhāyāmaṃ, sapāda-aṃśaṃ prastaraṃ, sapāda-aṃśaṃ kaṇṭhaṃ, [pādaṃ vā, tadupari prastaramekaṃśaṃ, ekāṃśaṃ kaṇṭhaṃ, pādonatryaṃśaṃ śikharaṃ, ekāṃśaṃ sthūpikodayaṃ syāt]; adbhutotsedham apyevaṃ yojayet iti kecit.*
> *athavā śāntika-pauṣṭika-jayadānāṃ utsedhaṃ krameṇa sama-ṣaṭ-pañcabhāgaṃ kṛtvā, tattadekaṃśaṃ hitvā, samāropeyet iti eke.*

#### English Translation
*   **Proportions of Single-Tier Vimāna:**
    *   The single-tiered temple (*ekabhūmi*) consists of six structural divisions (*ṣaḍ-varga*): basement (*adhiṣṭhāna*), pillars/walls (*pāda*), entablature (*prastara*), neck (*grīvā*), dome (*śikhara*), and finial (*sthūpikā*). Alternatively, it can be designed with four divisions (*catur-varga*).
    *   **Śāntika Proportion (Peaceful - 1.0 height-to-width ratio):**
        *   Under the 6-part division: divide the height into 6 parts: 1 part for basement, 2 parts for pillars, 2 parts for dome, and 1 part for finial. Alternatively, build the dome using rafter construction (*lupā-vidhāna*).
        *   Under the 8-part division: divide the height into 8 parts: 1 part for basement, 3 parts for pillar height (or twice the pillar width), 1 part for entablature, 1 part for neck, 2 parts for dome, and 1 part for finial.
    *   **Pauṣṭika Proportion (Nourishing - 1.25 ratio):**
        *   Divide the height into 9 parts: 1 part for basement, 2.25 parts for pillar height, 0.75 parts for entablature, 1.25 parts for neck, 2.75 parts for dome, and 1 part for finial.
    *   **Jayada Proportion (Victory-giving - 1.5 ratio):**
        *   Divide the height into 10 parts: 1 part for basement, 2.5 parts for pillar height, 1 part for entablature, 1.5 parts for neck, 3 parts for dome, and 1 part for finial.
    *   **Adbhuta Proportion (Miraculous - 1.75 ratio):**
        *   Divide the height into 11 parts: 1.25 parts for basement, 4 parts for pillar height (twice the pillar width), 1 part for entablature, 1.5 parts for neck, 3 parts for dome, and 1.25 parts for finial.
    *   **Sārvakāmika Proportion (Desire-fulfilling - 2.0 ratio):**
        *   Divide the height into 12 parts: 1.25 parts for basement, 4 parts for pillar height, 1.25 parts for entablature, 1.25 parts for neck, 3.25 parts for dome, and 1 part for finial. Some say one should apply the Adbhuta height proportions in this case.
        *   Alternatively, divide the heights of the Śāntika, Pauṣṭika, and Jayada styles into 6, 6, and 5 parts respectively, omit one part from each, and mount the structure.

### Section 2: Sanctum and Portico Layout

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **गर्भगृहलक्षणम्**
> विमानविस्तारं त्रि-चतुः-पञ्च-सप्त-नव-एकादशभागं कृत्वा यथाक्रमेण एक-द्वि-त्रि-चतुः-पञ्च-षडंशं गर्भगृहविस्तारं स्यात्। विमानविस्तारसमं त्रिपादम् अर्धं वा मुखमण्टपं; तदन्तरं त्रयोदशभागं कृत्वा तदेकांश-अधिकेन आयामं सप्ताविंशतिभेदं स्यात्। त्रित्रिसङ्ख्याभिः च उत्तम-मध्यम-अधम-भेदैः नवधा भवति। उत्तमानां विपुलं विमानविस्तारसमं सान्तरालं, तस्य वेशनं द्विदण्डं दण्डार्धं पादं वा तदायामं। हस्तेन पादम् अर्धं त्रिपादम् एकहस्तादि सपाद-पञ्चहस्तान्तं सभित्तिकं द्वारं जालकं वा कुर्यात्। मध्ये नीचानाम् अन्तरालं विना एकदण्डान्तं पाददण्ड-विवृद्ध्या नवभेदैः प्रवेशं कारयेत्; अन्येषां सर्वेषाम् अन्तरा विना एतैः निर्गमं कारयेत्।
> ***garbhagṛhalakṣaṇam***
> *vimānavistāraṃ tri-catuḥ-pañca-sapt-nav-aikādaśabhāgaṃ kṛtvā yathākrameṇa eka-dvi-tri-catuḥ-pañca-ṣaḍaṃśaṃ garbhagṛhavistāraṃ syāt. vimānavistārasamaṃ tripādaṃ ardhaṃ vā mukhamanṭapaṃ. tadantaraṃ trayodaśabhāgaṃ kṛtvā tadeka-aṃśādhikena āyāmaṃ saptaviṃśatibhedaṃ syāt. tritrisaṅkhyābiḥ ca uttama-madhyama-adhamabhedaiḥ navadhā bhavati. uttamānāṃ vipulaṃ vimānavistārasamaṃ sāntarālaṃ, tasya veśanaṃ dvidaṇḍaṃ daṇḍārdhaṃ pādaṃ vā tadāyāmaṃ. hastena pādam ardhaṃ tripādam ekahastādi sapāda-pañcahastāntaṃ sabhittikaṃ dvāraṃ jālakaṃ vā kuryāt. madhye nīcānām antarālaṃ vinā ekadaṇḍāntaṃ pādadaṇḍavivṛdhyā navabhedaiḥ praveśaṃ kārayet, anyeṣāṃ sarveṣām antarā vinā etaiḥ nirgamaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> **मुखमण्टपलक्षणम्**
> अथवा मुखमण्टपं कृत्वा तत्प्रमुखे तदायामेन पादाधिकम् अर्ध-अधिकं त्रिपाद-अधिकं द्विगुणं वा विस्तारं, विमानात् पाद-अर्धं त्रिपाद-अधिकं विस्तारायामयुतम् अर्धमण्डपं कुर्यात्। एतन्मध्ये भद्रतोरणाद्यैः युक्तम् अयुक्तं वा त्रिवर्गाढ्यं कुर्यात्।
> ***mukhamantapalakṣaṇam***
> *athavā mukhamanṭapaṃ kṛtvā tatpramukhe tadāyāmena pādādhikam ardhādhikaṃ tripādādhikaṃ dviguṇaṃ vā vistāraṃ, vimānāt pādārdhaṃ tripādādhikaṃ vistārāyāmayutum ardhamaṇḍapaṃ kuryāt. etanmadhye bhadratoraṇādyaiḥ yuktam ayuktaṃ vā trivargāḍhyaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> एतद्विमानस्य प्रमुखं विना पार्श्वत्रये मध्यभद्रं विमान-विपुल-त्रिभागैकं चतुर्भागं पञ्चभागं त्रिभागं वा; तन्निर्गमदण्डम् अध्यर्धं द्विदण्डं वा (मध्यं द्विदण्डम् अध्यर्ध-द्विदण्डं त्रिदण्डं वा) पादं तस्यावसाने कुर्यात्। तोरणानाम् उत्सेधं स्थलाद् उपरि उत्तराद् अधस्तात् द्वि-चतुर्भागात् त्रिभागं त्रिभागात् द्विभागं वा, तोरणपादोत्सेधं अर्धं त्रिभागैकं वा; अथवा दण्डेन त्रि-चतुः-पञ्च-दण्ड-विस्तारं तस्य पादबाह्यं तत्पादवशेन तदङ्गानि मानयेत्।
> *etadvimānasya pramukhaṃ vinā pārśvatraye madhyabhadraṃ vimāna-vipula-tribhāgaikaṃ caturbhāgaṃ pañcabhāgaṃ tribhāgaṃ vā; tannirgamadaṇḍam adhyardhaṃ dvidaṇḍaṃ vā (madhyaṃ dvidaṇḍam adhyardha-dvidaṇḍaṃ tridaṇḍaṃ vā) pādaṃ tasyāvasāne kuryāt. toraṇānām utsedhaṃ sthalād upari uttarād adhastāt dvi-caturbhāgāt tribhāgaṃ tribhāgaṃ vā, toraṇapādotsedhaṃ ardhaṃ tribhāgaikaṃ vā; athavā daṇḍena tri-catuḥ-pañcadaṇḍavistāraṃ tasya pādabāhyaṃ tatpādavaśena tadaṅgāni mānayet.*

#### English Translation
*   **Dimensions of the Sanctum (*Garbhagṛha-lakṣaṇam*):**
    *   Divide the temple width (*vimāna-vistāra*) into 3, 4, 5, 7, 9, or 11 parts. Accordingly, the width of the inner sanctum (*garbhagṛha*) should be 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 parts respectively.
    *   The front porch (*mukha-maṇṭapa*) should be equal to, three-quarters, or half of the temple width.
    *   Dividing its interior into 13 parts, the length (*āyāma*) increases by 1 part at a time, offering 27 varieties of dimensions. Grouped by threes into superior, middling, and inferior categories, they yield nine options.
    *   For the superior types, the width is equal to the temple width including the vestibule (*sāntarāla*). Its recession (*veśana*) is 2, 1.5, or 1 module.
    *   In cubits, make the doorway (*dvāra*) or lattice window (*jālaka*) including the doorframe/walls measure from 1.25, 1.5, 1.75, or 2 cubits up to 5.25 cubits.
    *   In the middle of minor shrines, omit the vestibule and make the projection/recession range up to 1 module, with nine options increasing by quarter-modules. For all other shrines, make the projection without the vestibule.

*   **Front Porch and Half-Hall Layout (*Mukha-maṇṭapa-lakṣaṇam*):**
    *   Or, having constructed the front porch, make a half-hall (*ardha-maṇṭapa*) in front of it measuring 1.25, 1.5, 1.75, or 2 times the porch length in width, and 1.5 or 1.75 times the temple width in length. In its center, design an ornate projection-archway (*bhadra-toraṇa*) or leave it plain, adorned with three classical layers of moldings.
    *   Except for the front face of the temple, on the remaining three sides, place a central projection (*madhyabhadra*) measuring 1/3, 1/4, or 1/5 of the temple width. Its projection depth (*nirgama*) should be 1.5, 2, or 2.5 modules.
    *   The height of the archways (*toraṇa*) above the floor level and below the lintel (*uttara*) should be 2/4, 1/3, or 2/3 of the height. The height of the archway pillars should be half or one-third of the total height.
    *   Alternatively, in modules, make the archway width 3, 4, or 5 modules from the outer face of the pillars, and determine all its components based on this module.

### Section 3: Pillars, Brackets, and Doors

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **पाद-बोधिक-द्वार-लक्षणम्**
> तोरण-अन्तर-गाढ-प्रवेशानं दण्डं, त्रिपादम् अर्धं, पादं वा; शेषं झष-उत्सेधम् उत्तर-अन्तं पञ्च-वक्त्रैः युक्तं पार्श्वयोः मकरस्य मध्ये पूरितं गाढ-युक्तं नाना-लता-पत्र-जाति-विचित्रितं सर्व-अलङ्कारैः यथा-शोभं युक्त्या कारयेत्। विमानस्य अधिष्ठानाद् उपरि उत्तर-अन्तं पादतुङ्गं, तत्र रस-मुनि-वसु-नव-दश-भागं कृत्वा; तद् एकांशं वेदिकोत्सेधं, तद्-तुङ्गं चतुर्धा कृत्वा अर्ध-अंश-कम्पं, द्व्यंशं कण्ठं कर्णम् अर्ध-अंश-कम्पं, पद्मम् अर्ध-अंशं, कम्पम् एवं प्रत्यङ्गेषु योजयेत्।
> ***pādabodhikādvāralakṣaṇam***
> *toraṇāntara-gāḍhapraveśanaṃ daṇḍaṃ, tripādaṃ ardhaṃ, pādaṃ vā; śeṣaṃ jhaṣotsedham uttarāntaṃ pañcavaktrairyuktaṃ pārśvayor makarasya madhyepūritaṃ gāḍhayuktaṃ nānālātāpatrajātivicitritaṃ sarvālaṅkāraiḥ yathāśobhaṃ yuktyā kārayet. vimānasyādhiṣṭhānād uparyuttāntaṃ pādatuṅgaṃ, tatra rasa-muni-vasu-nava-daśabhāgaṃ kṛtvā, tadeka-aṃśā-vedikotsedhaṃ, tattuṅgaṃ caturdhā kṛtvā ardha-aṃśakampaṃ, dvyaṃśakaṇṭhaṃ karṇam ardha-aṃśakampaṃ padmam ardha-aṃśaṃ, kampam evaṃ, evaṃ pratyaṅgeṣu yojayet.*
> 
> अथवा रसांशं कृत्वा, एकांश-कम्पं, द्व्यंशं कण्ठं (कर्णं), शेषम् उपरिबन्धं स्यात्। paṭṭikā-अंशेषु तुङ्ग-अर्ध-गलं, शेषं त्रिधा कृत्वा उपरिबन्धं पूर्ववत् कारयेत्; अथवा चतुर्धा कृत्वा एकांशेन गलाद् अधस्तात् कम्पं कारयेत् इति केचित्। पादोत्सेधं सम-अष्ट-नव-दश-भागं कृत्वा एकांशं पाद-विपुलं; अथवा अधिष्ठानोच्चं त्रि-चतुष्-पञ्च-भागं कृत्वा एकांशं विमान-निर्देश-हस्त-अङ्गुलिभिः वा पाद-विष्कम्भं स्यात्। चतुष्-पञ्च-षडंशं कृत्वा एकांश-हीनं भित्तिपाद-विपुलं, तत्र रस-मुनि-वसु-नन्द-धा विभज्य एकांशोनम् अग्रविस्तारं तद् दण्डसंज्ञं, तेन सर्वाङ्ग-प्रमाण-निर्णय-कारणं स्यात्। पादाः चतुरश्राः अष्टाश्राः षोडशाश्राः वृत्ताः इति मिश्रम् अमिश्रं वा कारयेत्। सम-युग्मं श्रेष्ठं, विषम-युग्मं निषिद्धम्। एक-त्रि-पञ्च-सप्त-नव-आदि-युग्म-भक्ति-वशेन संयोजयति। पादमूले अर्ध-दण्डेन पद्मं, तदुपरि कर्णे कण्ठे चतुरश्रं, शेषम् अष्टाश्रं षोडशाश्रं वृत्तं वा भित्तिपादं; पादात् पादान्तरं भित्तिप्रवेशं पाददण्डं स्यात्। दारु-शिला-स्तम्भेषु मूले अर्ध-दण्डेन पद्मं, तदुपरि कण्ठे (कर्णे) चतुरश्रं, शेषम् अष्टाश्रं षोडशाश्रं वृत्तं वा भित्तिपादं; पादात् पादान्तरं भित्तिप्रवेशं पाददण्डं स्यात्। दारु-शिला-स्तम्भेषु मूल-मध्य-अग्रेषु चतुरश्रं, अष्टाश्रं, षोडशाश्रं, वृत्तं वा सर्वत्र सर्वसमं नयेत्। पद्मोपरि सिंह-व्याल-भूत-नागादि कल्पयेत् इति केचित्।
> *athavā rasāṃśaṃ kṛtvā, ekāṃśakampaṃ, dvyaṃśaṃ kaṇṭhaṃ (karṇaṃ) śeṣam uparibandhaṃ syāt. paṭṭikāṃśeṣu tuṅgārdhagalaṃ, śeṣaṃ tridhā kṛtvā uparibandhaṃ pūrvavat kārayet; athavā caturdhā kṛtvā ekāṃśena galādadhastāt kampaṃ kārayet iti kecit. pādotsedhaṃ sama-aṣṭa-nava-daśabhāgaṃ kṛtvā ekāṃśa-pāda-vipulaṃ; athavā adhiṣṭhānoccaṃ tri-catuḥ-pañcabhāgaṃ kṛtvā ekāṃśaṃ vimānānirdeśa-hasta-aṅgulibhir vā pādaviṣkambhaṃ syāt. catuṣ-pañca-ṣaḍaṃśaṃ kṛtvā ekāṃśahīnaṃ bhittipāda-vipulaṃ, tatra rasa-muni-vasu-nandadhā vibhajya ekāṃśonam agravistāraṃ tad-daṇḍasaṃjñaṃ, tena sarvāṅgapramāṇanirṇayakāraṇaṃ syāt. pādāś-caturaśrā aṣṭāśrā ṣoḍaśāśrā vṛttā iti miśram amiśraṃ vā kārayet. samayugmaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ viṣamayugmaṃ niṣiddham. eka-tri-pañca-sama-nanda-bhaktivaśena saṃyojayati. pādamūle ardha-daṇḍena padmaṃ, tadupari karṇe kaṇṭhe caturaśraṃ, śeṣam aṣṭāśraṃ, ṣoḍaśāśraṃ, vṛttaṃ vā, bhittipādaṃ. pādāt pādāntaraṃ bhittipraveśaṃ pādadaṇḍaṃ syāt. dāru-śilā-stambheṣu mūle ardha-daṇḍena padmaṃ, tadupari kaṇṭhe (karṇe) caturaśraṃ, śeṣam aṣṭāśraṃ ṣoḍaśāśraṃ vṛttaṃ vā bhittipādaṃ, pādāt pādāntaraṃ bhittipraveśaṃ pādadaṇḍaṃ syāt. dāru-śilā-stambheṣu mūlamadhyāgreṣu caturaśraṃ, aṣṭāśraṃ, ṣoḍaśāśraṃ, vṛttaṃ vā, sarvatra sarvasamaṃ nayet. padmopari siṃha-vyāla-bhūta-nāgādi kalpayet iti kecit.*
> 
> पादाग्रे बोधिका-वीरकाण्ड-फलकानाम् एकैकदण्डम् उत्सेधं त्रिपाददण्डं वा, फलाकयाः पादोनं; कुम्भोच्चं, तत्समं बोधिकोच्चं वा इति केचित्। फलकोत्सेध-अर्धं ताटितुङ्गं, धृगुतुङ्गं, तदर्धं, पाददण्डं वा। ताट्याः अधस्तात् एकदण्डेन मालास्थानं, वीरकाण्डानां विपुलं पादविपुलम् एव, पादस्य कर्णे कण्ठेन पादात् क्रमेण ताट्यूर्ध्वभागं विस्तारं, तत्कर्णेन कुम्भविस्तारं, तत्कण्ठेन फलकाविस्तारं, तत्समं पादाधिकं वा बोधिकाविस्तारं, नानाचित्रित-रङ्गादीनि कारयेत्। फलकोत्सेधं त्रि-चतुर्भागं कृत्वा एकांशेन वेत्रम् अधस्तात् पद्मं, शेषं युक्त्या कारयेत्। अथवा फलक-कुम्भ-ताटीणाम् उत्सेधं तत्तद्-विस्तार-त्रि-भाग-एक-भागेन कारयेत् इति एके।
> *pādāgre bodhikā-vīrakāṇḍa-phalakānām ekaikadaṇḍam utsedhaṃ tripādadaṇḍaṃ vā, phalakāyāḥ pādonaṃ; kumboccaṃ, tatsamaṃ bodhikoccam veti kecit. phalakotsedhārdhaṃ tāṭituṅgaṃ, dhṛgutuṅgaṃ, tadardhaṃ, pādadaṇḍaṃ vā. tāṭyā adhastāt ekadaṇḍena mālāsthānaṃ, vīrakāṇḍānāṃ vipulaṃ pādavipulam eva pādasyakarṇe kaṇṭhena pādāt krameṇa tāṭyūrdhvabhāgaṃ vistāraṃ, tatkarṇena kumbhavistāraṃ, tatkaṇṭhena phalakāvistāraṃ, tatsamaṃ pādādhikaṃ vā, bodhikāvistāraṃ nānācitrita-raṅgādīni kārayet. phalakotsedhaṃ tri-caturbhāgaṃ kṛtvā, ekāṃśena vetram adhastāt padmaṃ, śeṣaṃ yuktyā kārayet. athavā phalaka-kumbha-tāṭīnām utsedhaṃ tattadvistāra-tribhāga-ekabhāgena kārayet ityeke.*
> 
> अथ द्वारलक्षणं वक्ष्ये - पादायाम-द्वारोत्सेधं तदर्ध-विस्तारं, विस्तार-अधिक-मानं विमान-निर्देश-अङ्गुलने एक-अष्ट-अङ्गुल-अन्तं नवधा भवति। अथवा दण्डेन पाददण्डादि पादाधिकं द्विदण्डान्तं पाददण्ड-वृद्ध्या नवविधं स्यात्। हस्तेन पाद-सार्धपाद-अर्ध-भित्तिविस्तारं, समं वा बहल-द्वार-विपुलं; पादोत्सेधं सम-अष्ट-नव-दश-भागं कृत्वा, षट्-सप्त-अष्ट-नव-भागं शुद्धद्वारं, शेषांशं द्विधा कृत्वा एकांशं भुवङ्गम् एकांशं पतङ्गम्; अथवा भुवङ्गमानं त्रिभागैकं शेष-पतङ्गमानं स्यात्। तत्समं दक्षिण-उत्तर-स्तम्भ-निर्गमं, तद्-बहुलात् पादाधिकम् अर्ध-अधिकं पादोन-द्विगुणं द्विगुणं वा, तेषां विपुल-बहल-समां नाना-पुष्प-वल्लिकां कारयेत्।
> अथवा तन्महद् द्वारं चेत् षोडशांशं कृत्वा त्रयोदशांशं शुद्धद्वारोत्सेधं, समांशम् अष्टांशं वा द्वारविस्तारं, षोडशांशं द्विधा कृत्वा एकांशं भुवङ्गम् एकांशं पतङ्गं स्यात्, अन्यत् सर्वं पूर्ववत् कारयेत्।
> *atha dvāralakṣaṇaṃ vakṣye - pādāyāma-dvārotsedhaṃ tadardhavistāraṃ vistārādhikumānaṃ vimānānirdeśāṅgulena eka-aṣṭāṅgulāntaṃ novadhā bhavati. athavā daṇḍena pādadaṇḍādi pādādhikaṃ dvidaṇḍāntaṃ pādadaṇḍavṛdhyā navavidhaṃ syāt. hastena pāda-sārdhapāda-ardha-bhittivistāraṃ, samaṃ vā bahaladvāravipula-pādotsedhaṃ sama-aṣṭa-nava-daśabhāgaṃ kṛtvā, ṣaṭ-sapt-aṣṭ-navabhāgaṃ śuddhadvāraṃ, śeṣāṃśaṃ dvidhā kṛtvā ekāṃśaṃ bhuvaṅgam ekāṃśaṃ pataṅgam; athavā bhuvaṅgamānaṃ tribhāgaikaṃ śeṣapataṅgamānaṃ syāt. tatsamaṃ dakṣiṇottara-stambha-nirgamaṃ, tadbahulāt pādādhikaṃ ardhādhikaṃ pādona-dviguṇaṃ dviguṇaṃ vā, teṣāṃ vipula-bahalasamāṃ nānāpuṣpavallikāṃ kārayet.*
> *athavā tanmahad dvāraṃ cet ṣoḍaśāṃśaṃ kṛtvā trayodaśāṃśaṃ śuddhadvārotsedhaṃ samāṃśam aṣṭāṃśaṃ vā dvāravistāraṃ ṣoḍaśāṃśaṃ dvidhā kṛtvā ekāṃśaṃ bhuvaṅgam ekāṃśaṃ pataṅgaṃ syāt, anyat sarvaṃ pūrvavat kārayet.*
> 
> **कवाटस्यलक्षणम्**
> कवाट-विस्तार-आयामं द्वारसमं, तयोः बाहुल्यं द्वारस्तम्भ-बहुल-अर्धं तत्पादाधिकं पादहीनं वा, तयोः ऊर्ध्व-अधः-शिखायुक्तं शेषं युक्त्या एव कारयेत्। द्वारपट्टिकायामं शुद्धद्वारसमं, तस्य विस्तारं द्वारविस्ताराद् अष्टांशं, तस्यार्धं पादं वा बाहुल्यं वामकवाटान्वितं नानाचित्रैः विचित्रतम्, तद्दण्डं पार्श्वपट्टिकाग्रं द्वि-त्रि-उपेत-पञ्च-सप्त-नव-आदि-युग्म-यष्टि-समायुक्तं कारयेत्। शङ्कुश्चेत् पट्टिकायष्टीः लोहजाः एव कारयेत्, आयसैः पुष्पमुकुल-शङ्कुभिः दृढं कृत्वा, आयसैः पट्टैः चन्द्राद्यैः त्रुट्याकारैः आकारैः मुकुलाकारैः च शोभनार्थं रक्षार्थं च योजयेत्; तयोः ऊर्ध्व-अधस्-तन्मध्ये वलयैः भुवङ्ग-पतङ्गयोः मध्ये वलयद्वयं पुलकोपेते कारयेत्। द्वार-षडंश-स्थान-क्रमं - भुवङ्गस्थानक्रमं भुवङ्गं दक्षिणशीर्षम् उत्तरपादम् ऊर्ध्वाननं; द्वितीयं दक्षिणस्तम्भम् उत्तराभिमुखं; तृतीयम् उत्तरस्तम्भं दक्षिणाभिमुखं; चतुर्थं पतङ्गम् उत्तरशीर्षं दक्षिणपादम् अधोमुखं संस्थाप्य; paścāt पञ्चमं दक्षिणकवाटम् उत्तराभिमुखं, षष्ठम् उत्तकरकवाटं दक्षिणाभिमुखं तक्षणा संस्थाप्य; पश्चात् धातु-विधातृ-भुवङ्ग-पतङ्ग-पति-वरुणान् तत्तन्मन्त्रेण आचार्यः स्थापयेत्।
> ***kavāṭasvalakṣaṇam***
> *kavāṭavistārāyāmaṃ dvārasamaṃ, tayor bāhulyaṃ dvārastambha-bahu-ardhaṃ tatpādādhikaṃ pādahīnaṃ vā, tayor ūrdhva-adhaḥ-śikhāyuktaṃ śeṣaṃ yuktyaiva kārayet. dvārapaṭṭikāyāmaṃ śuddhadvārasamaṃ, tasyavistāraṃ dvāravistārād aṣṭāṃśaṃ tasyārdhaṃ pādaṃ vā, bāhulyaṃ vāmakavāṭānvitaṃ nānācitraiḥ vicitritam, taddaṇḍaṃ pārśvapaṭṭikāgraṃ dvityropeta-pañca-sapta-navādi-yugmayuṣṭi-samāyuktaṃ kārayet. śaṅkuś-cet paṭṭikāyaṣṭīḥ lohajā eva kārayet, āyasaiḥ puṣpamukula-śaṅkubhiḥ dṛḍhaṃ kṛtvā; āyasaiḥ paṭṭaiś candrādyaiḥ truṭyākāraiḥ ākāraiḥ mukulākāraiś ca śobhanārthaṃ rakṣārthaṃ ca yojayet; tayor ūrdhva-adhas-tanmadhye valayair bhuvaṅga-pataṅgayor madhye valayadvayaṃ pulakopetaṃ kārayet. dvāra-ṣaḍaṃśa-sthāna-kramaṃ: bhuvaṅgasthānakramaṃ bhuvaṅgaṃ dakṣiṇa-śīrṣam uttarapādaṃ ūrdhvānanam; dvitīyaṃ dakṣiṇastambham uttarābhimukham; tṛtīyam uttarastambhaṃ dakṣiṇābhimukham; caturthaṃ pataṅgam uttarā-śīrṣaṃ dakṣiṇapādaṃ adhomukham; saṃsthāpya paścāt pañcamaṃ dakṣiṇakavāṭam uttarābhimuhaṃ, ṣaṣṭham uttarakavāṭaṃ dakṣiṇābhimukhaṃ takṣaṇā saṃsthāpya; paścāt dhātu-vidhātṛ-bhuvaṅgapataṅgapati-varuṇān tattanmantreṇa ācāryaḥ sthāpayet.*
> *dvārastambhaṃ kavāṭārthaṃ śirīṣa-punnāga-nimbavakulārjuna-madhūka-panasa-asanān anyān śubhān puṃvṛkṣān vā vidhinā āhṛtya dāru dvidhā kṛtvā dakṣiṇāṃśam dakṣiṇastambha-kavāṭam, vāmāṃśam uttarastambham uttarakavāṭaṃ ca, tasyordhvabhāgam ūrdhvapaṭṭikāś ca kārayet. evam eva vṛkṣālābhe ca ekajātīya-śilābhiḥ stambhaṃ kavāṭaṃ kārayet. kavāṭapṛṣṭhe bandhanārthaṃ dārumaya-yantraṃ yantrikāś ca kṛtvā bāhyapārśve ca ayasā rakṣābandhanaparaṃ yantraṃ yantrikāṃ āyatavalayopetām ayasā kāñcanena vā valmīkāṃ kṛtvā arpayet.*
> 
> द्वारस्तम्भं कवाटार्थं शिरीष-पुन्नाग-निम्ब-वकुल-अर्जुन-मधूक-पनस-असनन् अन्यान् शुभान् पुंवृक्षान् वा विधिना आहृत्य दारु द्विधा कृत्वा, दक्षिणांशं दक्षिणस्तम्भ-कवाटं, वामांशम् उत्तरस्तम्भम् उत्तरकवाटं च, तस्य ऊर्ध्वभागम् ऊर्ध्वपट्टिकाश्च कारयेत्। एवम् एव वृक्षालाभे च एकजातीय-शिलाभिः स्तम्भं कवाटं च कारयेत्। कवाटपृष्ठे बन्धनार्थं दारुमय-यन्त्रं यन्त्रिकाश्च कृत्वा बाह्यपार्श्वे च अयसा रक्षाबन्धनपरं यन्त्रं यन्त्रिकां च आयत-वलयोपेताम् अयसा काञ्चनेन वा वल्मीकां कृत्वा अर्पयेत्।

*   **Pillar and Column Specifications (*Pāda-bodhikā-lakṣaṇam*):**
    *   Within the archways, place deep recessions of 1, 0.75, 0.5, or 0.25 module. Adorn the lintel with fish-shaped designs (*jhaṣa-utsedha*) with five faces, flanked by makaras, and decorated with diverse creepers, leaves, and overall auspicious ornaments.
    *   The height of the pillar (*pādatuṅga*) from the basement to the lintel is divided into 6, 7, 8, 9, or 10 parts:
        *   Assign 1 part for the pedestal (*vedikā*).
        *   If divided into 4 parts: 0.5 part is for the fillet (*kampa*), 2 parts for the neck (*kaṇṭha/karṇa*), 0.5 part for the fillet, and 1 part for the lotus (*padma*). Apply this proportion on all secondary pillars.
        *   Alternatively, divide it into 6 parts: 1 part for fillet, 2 parts for neck, and the remaining for the upper band.
    *   Pillar width (*pāda-vipula*): Make it 1 part out of 8, 9, or 10 parts of the pillar height. Or, make it 1 part out of 3, 4, or 5 parts of the basement height.
    *   The thickness of the wall pilaster (*bhitti-pāda*) should be 1 part less than the main pillar.
    *   The width of the pillar's top recess is called the *daṇḍa* (module), which determines all architectural proportions.
    *   Pillars can be square (*caturaśra*), octagonal (*aṣṭāśra*), sixteen-sided (*ṣoḍaśāśra*), or circular (*vṛtta*), made as composite or single styles. Even-sided pillars are auspicious; odd-sided are prohibited.
    *   Plinths and carvings on pillars: at the base of the pillar, carve a lotus (*padma*) measuring half a module, above it a neck recess, and let the rest of the pilaster be octagonal, sixteen-sided, or circular. The spacing between pillars should be one module.
    *   Above the lotus, carve lions, vyalas, bhutas, or nagas.
    *   At the top of the pillar, the brackets (*bodhikā*), block (*vīrakāṇḍa*), and abacus (*phalakā*) should be 1 module or 0.75 module high. Adorn the brackets with colorful patterns and carvings.

- **Doorway and Frame Design (*Dvāra-lakṣaṇam*):**
    *   The doorway height (*dvārotsedha*) should equal the pillar height, and its width should be half of the height. The width ranges in nine options based on standard digits, or in modules increasing by quarter-modules.
    *   The thickness of the wall next to the door should be 1.25, 1.5, or 1.75 cubits, or equal to the doorframe.
    *   Divide the doorframe height into 8, 9, or 10 parts: assign 6, 7, 8, or 9 parts for the clear opening (*śuddha-dvāra*). Of the remaining parts, divide them into two: 1 part for the threshold (*bhuvaṅga*) and 1 part for the lintel (*pataṅga*).
    *   The projection of the jambs should equal their thickness, decorated with various flower and creeper patterns.

- **Door Shutters and Hardware (*Kavāṭa-lakṣaṇam*):**
    *   Make the width and height of the door shutters (*kavāṭa*) equal to the door opening. Their thickness should be half the thickness of the doorframe.
    *   Fit them with iron pivots (*śikhā*) at the top and bottom.
    *   The panels should be decorated with carvings, and fitted with iron plates shaped like half-moons, flower buds, or protective brackets.
    *   Fit them with rings (*valaya*) in the middle, and two rings between the threshold and lintel.
    *   Sequential installation order of door parts:
        1. First, place the threshold (*bhuvaṅga*) with its head to the South and foot to the North, facing upwards.
        2. Second, place the South doorframe jamb facing North.
        3. Third, place the North doorframe jamb facing South.
        4. Fourth, place the lintel (*pataṅga*) with its head to the North and foot to the South, facing downwards.
        5. Fifth, hang the South door shutter facing North.
        6. Sixth, hang the North door shutter facing South.
        *   Having installed them, the Acharya should worship the guardian deities (Dhatri, Vidhatri, sill deity, lintel deity, Patipati, and Varuṇa) with their respective mantras.
    *   Wood selection: Procure wood from auspicious male trees like Shirisha, Punnaga, Nimba, Vakula, Arjuna, Madhuka, Panasa, or Asana. Carve the right side from the right piece and the left side from the left piece of wood. Alternatively, use a single type of stone to carve the doorframe and shutters.
    *   On the back of the door, fit a wooden latch-bar (*dārumaya-yantra*) and locks. On the outer side, attach iron safety bands and bolt rings plated with iron or gold.

    *   Cast it in gold, silver, or copper. If unable, build it of lime mortar (*sudhā*) and fit an iron needle (*āyasī-sūci*) at the tip. Minor variations of 1/4, 1/2, 3/4, or 1 digit, or minor fractions in module are not considered faults. One can adjust the dimensions by a quarter, half, three-quarters, or one cubit as appropriate.

#### English Translation
*   **Astrological and Mathematical Testing (*Āyādi-parīkṣā*):**
    *   Now, I shall describe the proportional testing (*āyādi-parīkṣā*):
        *   **Āya and Nakṣatra:** Multiply the height in cubits by 8. Divide it by 36 (Bhānu $\times$ 3); the remainders represent the Āya (income) and Nakṣatra (star).
        *   **Yoni and Vyaya:** Multiply the width by 9. Divide it by 8; the remainder represents the Yoni (source) and Vyaya (expenditure).
        *   **Vāra and Tithi:** Multiply the circumference by 9. Divide it by 30; the remainders represent the Vāra (solar day) and Tithi (lunar day).
    *   One should apply these tests to the dimensions in cubits of the temple, gateways (*gopura*), porticos (*maṇṭapa*), enclosures (*āvaraṇa*), and shrines; and in digits (*aṅgula*) for icons, and overall dimensions for temple chariots (*ratha*).
    *   Even if there is a minor defect (*alpa-doṣa*), if the overall virtues are high (*guṇa-adhika*), it should be accepted. One can adjust fractional dimensions using barley-corn (*yava*) units. Everything not detailed here should be done according to the rules of the scriptures of master builders (*śilpi-śāstra*).

*   **Plastering and Placement of Subsidiary Sculptures:**
    *   Plaster the temple with white lime (*sudhā-varṇa*) and paint it with decorative colors. Adorn it with carvings of vyalas, lions, and others.
    *   In the valabhi (frieze), place carvings of Vidyadharas, hamsas (swans), bhutas, and nagas. Place doorkeepers (*dvāra-pālaka*) at the doorways.
    *   **Subsidiary Deities on the Dome/Roof (*Śikhara*):**
        *   Inside the large dormers (*mahānāsī*): place **Puruṣa** in the East, **Nṛsiṃha** in the South, **Acyuta** in the West, and **Aniruddha** in the North.
        *   Alternatively: place the **Sudarṣana Cakra** in the East, **Dakṣiṇāmūrti** in the South, **Nṛsiṃha** in the West, and **Pitāmaha** (Brahmā) in the North.
    *   **Subsidiary Deities on the Wall/Pillar-band (*Pāda-varga*):**
        *   Between the archways: place **Satya** in the South, **Acyuta** in the West, **Aniruddha** in the North, and **Puruṣa** to the right of the East doorway.
        *   Alternatively: place **Nṛsiṃha** in the South, **Acyuta** in the West, and **Varāha** in the North.
    *   At the doorways of the South and other directions, place the respective directional deities, and to the right of the doorways, place other subsidiary deities in standing or sitting postures.
    *   In the front porch (*mukha-maṇṭapa*): place **Vighneśa** (Gaṇeśa) on the South outer wall, and **Vindhyavāsinī** (Durgā) on the North.
    *   At the corners (South-East, etc.) above the entablature and at the neck level, place the mounts of the deities: **Mṛgeśa** (lion/tiger) or **Khageśa** (Garuḍa). Everywhere else, depict the victorious pastimes and sports (*vijaya-krīḍā-ādīni*) of the Lord.
