# Chapter 68: Expiations for Festivals (Utsava-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules and Expiatory Rites for Omissions, Deficiencies, and Interruptions during Temple Festivals (Utsava)

#### Verse / Paragraph 1
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **अथ अष्टषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **(उत्सवप्रायश्चित्त-विधिः)**

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **atha aṣṭaṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ**
> **(utsavaprāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)**

**Translation (English):**
*   **Significance of Flag-Hoisting:** If a festival (*utsava*) is celebrated without performing the flag-hoisting ceremony (*dhvajārohaṇa*), the entire festival becomes fruitless and devoid of merit; therefore, one must perform the flag-hoisting ceremony with all effort and then conduct the festival.

#### Verse / Paragraph 2
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> अथ उत्सव-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिं वक्ष्ये —

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> atha utsava-prāyaścitta-vidhiṃ vakṣye —

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Festival due to Ignorance or Greed:** Having resolved the final bathing festival (*avabhṛtha*) to occur in the specified month and under the designated asterism, and having hoisted the flag, if one fails to perform the festival due to ignorance, greed for wealth, or any other reason, then in the second month, on that same day, having resolved the final bathing ceremony and having collected the sacred soil (*mṛt-saṅgrahaṇa*) for the holy water sprouts (*tīrtha-aṅkura*) prior to that day, one should begin the festival anew.

#### Verse / Paragraph 3
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजारोहण-हीने)**
> ध्वजारोहण-हीने च उत्सवे कृते स उत्सवः निष्फलो भवति, तस्मात् सर्वप्रयत्नेन ध्वजारोहणं कृत्वा उत्सवं कारयेत् ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvajārohaṇa-hīne)**
> dhvajārohaṇa-hīne ca utsave kṛte sa utsavaḥ niṣphalo bhavati, tasmāt sarvaprayatnena dhvajārohaṇaṃ kṛtva utsavaṃ kārayet ||

**Translation (English):**
*   **Expiation for Omitted Festival:** As expiation for this omission, in the lotus-fire (*abja-agni*), offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Dhvaja*, *Gāruḍa*, *Vaiṣvaksena*, *Saudarśana*, and *Utsava-adhidaivatya* (presiding deity of the festival) oblations, ending with the *vyāhṛtis*; then, worship the deity with Vaiṣṇava rites and conduct the festival.

#### Verse / Paragraph 4
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(उक्तमासे अज्ञानात् उत्सवाऽकरणे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> उक्तमासे च उक्तनक्षत्रे अवभृथं संकल्प्य ध्वजारोहणं कृत्वा, अज्ञानाद् अर्थलोभाद् वा येनकेनचिद् उत्सवं न कुर्यात्, द्वितीये मासे तद्दिने अवभृथं संकल्प्य तद्दिनात् पूर्वं तीर्थ-अङ्कुरार्थं मृत्संग्रहणं कृत्वा उत्सवम् आरभेत।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(uktāmāse ajñānāt utsavā'karaṇe prāyaścittam)**
> uktamāse ca uktanakṣatre avabhṛthaṃ saṅkalpya dhvajārohaṇaṃ kṛtva, ajñānād arthālobhād vā yenakenacid utsavaṃ na kuryāt, dvitīye māse taddine avabhṛthaṃ saṅkalpya taddināt pūrvaṃ tīrtha-aṅkurārthaṃ mṛtsaṅgrahaṇaṃ kṛtvā utsavam ārabheta.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Incorrect Timing of Final Bath:** If one performs the final bathing ceremony (*avabhṛtha*) in a manner that violates the correct asterism (*nakṣatra*), count of days (*saṅkhyā*), or chronological sequence (*krama*) of the festival, offer oblations to the festival's presiding deity (*utsava-adhidaivatya*), and to the presiding deities of the lunar day (*tithi*), week-day (*vāra*), and asterism (*nakṣatra*) twice, and then feed the Brahmins.

#### Verse / Paragraph 5
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> अस्य प्रायश्चित्तम् अब्जाग्नौ वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, ध्वजं, गरुडं, वैष्वक्सेनं, सौदर्शनं, उत्सवाधिदैवत्यं (मुद्रितपाठे मुत्सवाधिदैवत्यं) च व्याहृत्यन्तं हुत्वा, वैष्णवं संपूज्य उत्सवं कारयेत्। उत्सवस्य अवभृथ-नक्षत्र-संख्या-क्रम-विपर्यसेन (मुद्रितपाठे नक्षत्र इति स्थाने तक्षत्र) अवभृथं कुर्यात् चेत्, उत्सवाधिदैवत्यं तिथिवारनक्षत्रं च द्विर्हुत्वा ब्राह्मणान् भोजयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> asya prāyaścittam abjāgnau vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣusūktaṃ, dhvajaṃ, garuḍaṃ, vaiṣvakṣenaṃ, saudarśanaṃ, utsavādhidaivatyaṃ ca vyāhṛtyantaṃ hutvā, vaiṣṇavaṃ sampūjya utsavaṃ kārayet. utsavasya avabhṛtha-nakṣatra-saṅkhyā-krama-viparyasena avabhṛthaṃ kuryāt cet, utsavādhidaivatyaṃ tithivāranakṣatraṃ ca dvirhutvā brāhmaṇān bhojayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Garuḍa Consecration on Flag:** If the flag-hoisting is performed without the consecration of Garuḍa (*garuḍa-pratiṣṭhā*) on the flag-cloth, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Dhvaja*, and *Gāruḍa* oblations a hundred times, and perform the consecration again.

#### Verse / Paragraph 6
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजे गरुडप्रतिष्ठा-हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> ध्वजारोहणे गरुडप्रतिष्ठा-विहीने वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं (मुद्रितपाठे पुरषसूक्तं), ध्वजं, गरुडं च शतशो हुत्वा पुनः प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत् ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvaje garuḍapratiṣṭhā-hīne prāyaścittam)**
> dhvajārohaṇe garuḍapratiṣṭhā-vihīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, dhvajaṃ, garuḍaṃ ca śataśo hutvā punaḥ pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet ||

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Worship to Retinue Deities:** If the worship (*arcana*) or food offering (*nivedana*) to the flag-deity (*dhvaja-daiva*), Cakra, Viṣvaksena, or Nandīśa is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and their respective presiding deities' oblations, worship them again, and present the food offering.

#### Verse / Paragraph 7
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजदैवादि-पूजनहीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> ध्वजदैव-चक्र-विष्वक्सेन-नन्दीशानाम् अर्चनं निवेदनं वा हीनं चेत्, वैष्णवं तत्तद्देवत्यं च हुत्वा पुनरभ्यर्च्य निवेदयेत् ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvajadaivādi-pūjanahīne prāyaścittam)**
> dhvajadaiva-cakra-viṣvakṣena-nandīśānām arcanaṃ nivedanaṃ vā hīnaṃ cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ tattaddevatyaṃ ca hutvā punarabhyarcya nivedayet ||

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Drum Beating:** If the ritual beating of the kettle-drum (*bherī-tāḍana*) is omitted, offer the *Raudra* oblation and the *vyāhṛtis*, and then proceed with the beating of the kettle-drum.

#### Verse / Paragraph 8
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(भेरीताडन-हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> भेरीताडन-हीने रौद्रं व्याहतीश्च हुत्वा भेरीताडनं कारयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(bherītāḍana-hīne prāyaścittam)**
> bherītāḍana-hīne raudraṃ vyāhṛtīśca hutvā bherītāḍanaṃ kārayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Food Offering to Garuḍa:** If after the flag-hoisting is completed, the food offering to Garuḍa is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Dhvaja*, and *Gāruḍa* oblations, and make a double food offering.

#### Verse / Paragraph 9
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजगरुड-निवेदनहीने तन्मुद्गान्ननिवेदनहीने च)**
> ध्वजारोहणे कृते गरुडस्य निवेदने हीने वैष्णवं, ध्वजं, गरुडं च हुत्वा द्विगुणं निवेदयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvajagaruḍa-nivedanahīne tanmudgānnanivedanahīne ca)**
> dhvajārohaṇe kṛte garuḍasya nivedane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, dhvajaṃ, garuḍaṃ ca hutvā dviguṇaṃ nivedayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Mung-Bean Rice Offering:** If the offering of *mudgānna* (rice cooked with split green gram/mung beans) to Garuḍa is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Dhvaja*, and *Gāruḍa* oblations, and with diligent effort, offer the prescribed mung-bean preparation (*maudgika*).

#### Verse / Paragraph 10
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> तस्य मुद्गान्न-निवेदनहीने (मुद्रितपाठे मुद्रान्ननिवेदनहीने) वैष्णवं, ध्वजं, गरुडं च हुत्वा यत्नेन यथोक्तं मौद्गिकं (मुद्रितपाठे मौद्द्रिकं) निवेदयेत् ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> tasya mudgānna-nivedanahīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, dhvajaṃ, garuḍaṃ ca hutvā yatnena yathoktaṃ maudgikaṃ nivedayet ||

**Translation (English):**
*   **Theft or Damage to the Flag-Staff:** If the flag-staff (*dhvaja-daṇḍa*) is stolen by thieves, is missing, is destroyed by fire, or is struck by lightning (*aśani*), offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Vāyavya*, *Āgneya*, the *iṅkāra-ādi* (seed sounds of Garuḍa), *Gāruḍa*, and *Dhvaja* oblations; then erect the flag-staff, hoist the flag as before, and perform the remainder of the festival.

#### Verse / Paragraph 11
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजस्य चौरापहरणादौ प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> ध्वजदण्डे चोराद्यपहृते नष्टे (मुद्रितपाठे हीने) अग्निदग्धे अशनिनोत्पातिते च वैष्णवं, वायव्यम्, आग्नेयम्, इङ्कारादीन् (मुद्रितपाठे इंकारादीन्) गरुडं, ध्वजं च हुत्वा, ध्वजदण्डम् आरोप्य, पूर्ववत् ध्वजम् आरोप्य उत्सवशेषं कारयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvajasya caurāpaharaṇādau prāyaścittam)**
> dhvajadaṇḍe caurādyapahṛte naṣṭe agnidagdhe aśaninotpātite ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, vāyavyam, āgneyam, iṅkārādīn garuḍaṃ, dhvajaṃ ca hutvā, dhvajadaṇḍam āropya, pūrvavat dhvajam āropya utsavaśeṣaṃ kārayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Falling of the Flag-Cloth:** If the flag-cloth with Garuḍa (*dhvaja-paṭa*) falls to the ground, offer the *Brāhma*, *Prājāpatya*, *Saura*, *Saumya*, and *Āgneya* oblations along with the *vyāhṛtis*, and raise it back to its position as before, equipped with the bell.

#### Verse / Paragraph 12
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजपट-पतने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> ध्वजगरुडे (ध्वजपटे) पतिते ब्राह्मं, प्राजापत्यं, सौरं, सौम्यं, आग्नेयं, व्याहतीश्च हुत्वा पूर्ववत् घण्टासहितम् आरोपयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvajapaṭa-patane prāyaścittam)**
> dhvajagaruḍe (dhvajapaṭe) patite brāhmaṃ, prājāpatyaṃ, saura, saumyaṃ, āgneyaṃ, vyāhṛtīśca hutvā pūrvavat ghaṇṭāsahitam āropayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Exemption for Weather Exposure:** Since the flag-Garuḍa is considered a sky-flying entity (*ākāśa-cārin*), any exposure or obstruction to it by rain or heat does not constitute a defect (*doṣa*).

#### Verse / Paragraph 13
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(ध्वजगरुडस्य वर्षादिबाधे दोषाभावः)**
> ध्वजगरुडस्य आकाशचारित्वात् तस्य वर्षा-आतप-बाधो न दोषाय भवति।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(dhvajagaruḍasya varṣādibādhe doṣābhāvaḥ)**
> dhvajagaruḍasya ākāśacāritvāt tasya varṣā-ātapa-bādho na doṣāya bhavati.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Delay of Festival Past Regular Season:** If the seasonal festival is not performed in the designated month, resolve the final bath (*avabhṛtha*) on the corresponding day of another month and perform the festival. Its expiatory ritual consists of offering the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Dhvaja*, and *Gāruḍa* oblations ten times each, worshiping the Vaiṣṇavas, paying the sacrificial fees (*dakṣiṇā*) to the Acarya, and conducting the festival.

#### Verse / Paragraph 14
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(नियतकाले उत्सवाऽकरणे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> कालोत्सवम् उक्तमासे न कुर्यात् चेत्, अन्यस्मिन् मासे तद्दिने च अवभृथं संकल्प्य उत्सवं कारयेत्। अस्य प्रायश्चित्तं वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, ध्वजं, गरुडं च दशशो हुत्वा, वैष्णवान् संपूज्य आचार्यदक्षिणां दत्त्वा उत्सवम् आचरेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(niyatakāle utsavā'karaṇe prāyaścittam)**
> kālotsavam uktamāse na kuryāt cet, anyasmin māse taddine ca avabhṛthaṃ saṅkalpya utsavaṃ kārayet. asya prāyaścittaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, dhvajaṃ, garuḍaṃ ca daśaśo hutvā, vaiṣṇavān sampūjya ācāryadaxṣiṇāṃ dattvā utsavam ācaret.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Preliminary Ablutions:** If the ablution ceremony (*snapana*) at the commencement of the festival is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Vāruṇa* oblations, and sprinkle the deity with pure water.

#### Verse / Paragraph 15
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(उत्सवादौ स्नपन-हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> उत्सवादौ स्नपने हीने वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, वारुणं च हुत्वा शुद्धोदकैः अभिषेचयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(utsavādau snapana-hīne prāyaścittam)**
> utsavādau snapane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, vāruṇaṃ ca hutvā śuddhoadakaiḥ abhiṣecayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Festival Wrist-Band:** If the tying of the protective wrist-band (*pratisara-bandhana*) for the festival is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Vaiṣvaksena*, and *Saudarśana* oblations, and tie the wrist-band again.

#### Verse / Paragraph 16
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(उत्सवार्थ-प्रतिसर-हीने)**
> उत्सवार्थ-प्रतिसरबन्धने हीने वैष्णवं, वैष्वक्सेनं, सौदर्शनं च हुत्वा पुनः प्रतिसरं बध्नीयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(utsavārtha-pratisara-hīne)**
> utsavārtha-pratisarabandhane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣvakṣenaṃ, saudarśanaṃ ca hutvā punaḥ pratisaraṃ badhnīyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Defective Kumbha-Altar in Yajnasala:** If the kumbha-altar (*kumbha-vedī*) in the sacrificial pavilion (*yajña-śālā*) is deficient or excessive in its dimensions, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Āgneya*, and *Bhūmi-daivata* oblations.

#### Verse / Paragraph 17
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(यज्ञशाला-कुम्भवेद्योः प्रमाणहीनाधिक्ययोः प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> यज्ञशालायां कुम्भवेद्यां प्रमाणहीनायां अधिकायां च वैष्णवम्, आग्नेयम्, भूमिदेवत्यं च जुहुयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(yajñaśālā-kumbhavedyoḥ pramāṇahīnādhikyayoḥ prāyaścittam)**
> yajñaśālāyāṃ kumbhavedyāṃ pramāṇahīnāyāṃ adhikāyāṃ ca vaiṣṇavam, āgneyaṃ, bhūmidevatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Missing Garlands and Gateway Toranas:** In the absence of gateway arches (*toraṇas*), offer oblations to the door-keepers (*dauvārika*); if the sacred grass garland (*darbha-mālā*) is missing, offer the *Ārṣa* (sages') or *Vāruṇa* oblation; if decorations are omitted, offer the *Śrī-daivata* oblation.

#### Verse / Paragraph 18
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(तोरण-दर्भमाला-अलङ्काराणां हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> तोरणानाम् अलाभे दौवारिकदेवत्यं; दर्भमालायां हीनायाम् आर्षं (मुद्रितपाठे मार्षं); अलङ्कारहीने श्रीदेवत्यं च जुहुयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(toraṇa-darbhamālā-alaṅkārāṇāṃ hīne prāyaścittam)**
> toraṇānām alābhe dauvārikadevatyaṃ; darbhamālāyāṃ hīnāyām ārṣaṃ; alaṅkārahīne śrīdevatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Neglect of Morning or Evening Offerings:** If having offered the morning *bali*, the festival procession is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Vaiṣvaksena*, *Gāruḍa*, and the festival presiding deity's oblations, and perform a double festival procession in the evening. If the evening procession is omitted, double the morning procession.

#### Verse / Paragraph 19
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(प्रातःसायंबल्यादि-हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> प्रातःकाले बलिं निर्वाप्य उत्सवे हीने वैष्णवं, वैष्वक्सेनं, गरुडं, तदुत्सवाधिदेवत्यं च हुत्वा सायं द्विगुणम् उत्सवं कारयेत्। सायं हीने प्रातः द्विगुणं वर्धयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**

**Translation (English):**
*   **Festivals During Severe Storms:** If one conducts the festival of the Lord of Devas during heavy downpours, gale winds, lightning strikes, or flashes of lightning, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata* oblations, and then perform the festival.

#### Verse / Paragraph 37
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(वीथिमध्ये वर्षादिस्पर्शे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> वीथ्यन्तरे वर्षधाराप्रवेशे तृणपांसुयुतवातस्पर्शे च देवेशं संस्नाप्य, वैष्णवं, दिग्देवत्यं, वारुणं, वायव्यं, यद्देवाश्च (मुद्रितपाठे यद्देवादाँश्च) हुत्वा पुनरुत्सवं कारयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(vīthimadhye varṣādisparśe prāyaścittam)**
> vīthyaṃntare varṣādhārāpraveśe tṛṇapāṃsuyutavātasparśe ca deveaśaṃ saṃsnāpy, vaiṣṇavaṃ, digdevatyaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, vāyavyaṃ, yaddevāśca hutvā punarutsavaṃ kārayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Defilement of Processions by Elements:** If rain-showers enter the streets, or if the deity is touched by winds carrying grass and dust, bathe the Lord of Devas, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Dig-daivata*, *Vāruṇa*, *Vāyavya*, and *Yad-devāḥ* (mantras starting with *yad devā deva-hedanam...*) oblations, and perform the festival again.

#### Verse / Paragraph 38
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(उत्सवकाले कलहादौ प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> उत्सवकाले कलह-रुधिरस्रावेषु सत्सु वैष्णवम्, आग्नेयम्, ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, दिनाधिदैवत्यं च जुहुयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(utsavakāle kalahādou prāyaścittam)**
> utsavakāle kalaha-rudhirasrāveṣu satsu vaiṣṇavam, āgneyaṃ, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, dinādhidaivatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Violence and Bloodshed During Festivals:** If during the festival, fights occur resulting in the shedding of blood, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Āgneya*, *Brāhma*, *Raudra*, and *Dina-adhidevatya* oblations.

#### Verse / Paragraph 39
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(आलयाभ्यन्तरे शस्त्रादिना मरणे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> आलयाभ्यन्तरे शस्त्राद्यैः मरणे सति तत् शीघ्रम् अपहाय अब्जाग्नौ महाशान्तिं तद्दिनाधिदैवत्यं च जुहुयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(ālayābhyantare śastrādinā maraṇe prāyaścittam)**
> ālayābhyantare śastrādyaiḥ maraṇe sati tat śīghram apahāya abjāgnou mahāśāntiṃ taddinādhidaivatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Death by Violence inside the Temple:** If death by weapons or violence occurs inside the temple, remove the corpse immediately, and offer the *Mahāśānti* and *Dina-adhidevatya* oblations in the lotus-fire (*abja-agni*).

#### Verse / Paragraph 40
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(वीथ्यां मरणसम्भवे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> वीथ्यां मरणादौ संभूते तत् शीघ्रं व्यपोह्य शान्तिहोमं हुत्वा उत्सवं कारयेत्। एकनाडिकायाः यामाद् अर्वाक् कालगतं चेत् पुनरुत्सवं कारयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(vīthyāṃ maraṇasambhave prāyaścittam)**
> vīthyāṃ maraṇādou saṃbhūte tat śīghraṃ vyapōhya śāntihomaṃ hutvā utsavaṃ kārayet. ekanāḍikāyāḥ yāmād arvāk kālagataṃ cet punarutsavaṃ kārayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Death on the Procession Street:** If death occurs in the street, remove the corpse quickly, perform the peace-offering (*śānti-homa*), and conduct the festival. If a duration of one *nāḍikā* up to a *yāma* (three hours) has elapsed, perform the festival again.

#### Verse / Paragraph 41
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(अशीतिदण्डाभ्यन्तरे शवे सति प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> आलयस्य अशीतिदण्डाभ्यन्तरे वीथ्यां शवे सति तत्र उत्सवं न कुर्यात्; कुर्यात् चेद् राजराष्ट्रविनाशाय भवति; तस्मात् शीघ्रम् अपहाय प्रायश्चित्तं महाशान्तिं पार्षदं च हुत्वा पुनरुत्सवं कारयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(aśītidaṇḍābhyantare śave sati prāyaścittam)**
> ālayasya aśītidaṇḍābhyantare vīthyāṃ śave sati tatra utsavaṃ na kuryāt; kuryāt cet rājarāṣṭravināśāya bhavati; tasmāt śīghram apahāya prāyaścittaṃ mahāśāntiṃ pārṣadaṃ ca hutvā punarutsavaṃ kārayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Presence of Corpse near Temple:** If a corpse lies in the street within eighty *daṇḍas* (rods) of the temple, one should not perform the festival there; if performed, it causes the destruction of the king and the nation. Therefore, remove it quickly, offer the expiatory *Mahāśānti* and *Pārṣada* (attendant) oblations, and perform the festival again.

#### Verse / Paragraph 42
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(उत्सवमध्ये अन्योत्सव-शुभकर्मकरणे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> ध्वजारोहणाद् ऊर्ध्वं तीर्थदिनात् पूर्वम् अन्योत्सवं शुभकर्माणि न कुर्यात्; कुर्यात् चेद् आभिचारिकं भवति; तद्दोषशमनार्थं वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, पार्षदं च हुत्वा ब्राह्मणान् भोजयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(utsavamadhye anyotsava-śubhakarmakaraṇe prāyaścittam)**
> dhvajārohaṇād ūrdhvaṃ tīrthadināt pūrvam anyotsavaṃ śubhakarmāṇi na kuryāt; kuryāt cet ābhicārikaṃ bhavati; taddoṣaśamanārthaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, pārṣadaṃ ca hutvā brāhmaṇān bhojayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Forbidden Auspicious Ceremonies:** After the flag-hoisting and before the day of the final bath (*tīrtha-dina*), one should not perform other festivals or auspicious ceremonies; if performed, it becomes malevolent (*ābhicārika*). To pacify that defect, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Pārṣada* oblations, and feed the Brahmins.

#### Verse / Paragraph 43
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(तीर्थदिनात्पूर्वरात्रौ प्रतिसरबन्ध-शयनाऽभावे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> तीर्थदिनात् पूर्वरात्रौ प्रतिसरबन्धने शयने वा हीने वैष्णवं, श्रीभूमिदेवत्यं, सौदर्शनं च हुत्वा ते समाचरेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(tīrthadinātpūrvarātrou pratisarabandha-śayanā'bhave prāyaścittam)**
> tīrthadināt pūrvarātrou pratisarabandhane śayane vā hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrībhūmidevatyaṃ, soudarśanaṃ ca hutvā te samācaret.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Pre-Bath Rites:** If the wrist-band binding or reclining ceremony is omitted on the night before the final bath, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *Saudarśana* oblations, and perform them.

#### Verse / Paragraph 44
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(तीर्थस्नानकाले अतीते हीने वा प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> तीर्थस्नानकाले (मुद्रितपाठे तीर्थस्ननकाले) अतीते हीने वा वैष्णवं, वारुणं, स्कन्ददेवत्यं च जुहुयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(tīrthasnānākāle atīte hīne vā prāyaścittam)**
> tīrthasnānākāle atīte hīne vā vaiṣṇavaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, skandadevatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Delay of Holy Bath:** If the time for the holy bath (*tīrtha-snāna*) has passed or is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Vāyavya*, *Vāruṇa*, and *Skanda-daivata* oblations.

#### Verse / Paragraph 45
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(तीर्थदिने रात्रौ ध्वजावरोहण-हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> तद्रात्रौ ध्वजावरोहणे हीने ध्वजं, गरुडं (मुद्रितपाठे गारुडं), वैष्णवं च जुहुयात् ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(tīrthadine rātrou dhvajāvarohaṇa-hīne prāyaścittam)**
> tadrātrou dhvajāvarohaṇe hīne dhvajaṃ, garuḍaṃ, vaiṣṇavaṃ ca juhuyāt ||

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Flag-Lowering:** If the flag-lowering (*dhvaja-avarohaṇa*) is omitted on the night of the final bath day, offer the *Dhvaja*, *Gāruḍa*, and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations.

#### Verse / Paragraph 46
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(अवरोहणे बलिहीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> अवरोहणे बलौ हीने ध्वजदेवं संपूज्य वैष्णवं तदालयगतपरिषद्देवत्यं च हुत्वा ध्वजावरोहण कारयेत्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(avarohaṇe balihīne prāyaścittam)**
> avarohaṇe balou hīne dhvajadevaṃ saṃpūjya vaiṣṇavaṃ tadālayagatapariṣaddevatyaṃ ca hutvā dhvajāvarohaṇaṃ kārayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Bali at Flag-Lowering:** If the *bali* offering at the time of flag-lowering is omitted, worship the flag-deity (*dhvaja-deva*), offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and the temple's retinue-deities' (*pariṣad-daivata*) oblations, and lower the flag.

#### Verse / Paragraph 47
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(उत्सवान्त-स्नपनहीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> उत्सवान्तस्नपने हीने वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, जयादि-मूर्तिमन्त्रांश्च जुहुयात्।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(utsavānta-snapanahīne prāyaścittam)**
> utsavāntasnapane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, jayādi-mūrtimantrāṃśca juhuyāt.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Omission of Closing Ablutions:** If the ablution (*snapana*) at the end of the festival is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, and *Jaya-ādi* and *Mūrti-mantras*.

#### Verse / Paragraph 48
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **(अनुक्तदोष-प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> अत्र अनुक्तदोषाणां प्रायश्चित्तं वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, गायत्रीं च जुहुयात् इति विज्ञायते ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **(anuktadoṣa-prāyaścittam)**
> atra anuktadoṣāṇāṃ prāyaścittaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, gāyatrīṃ ca juhuyāt iti vijñāyate ||

**Translation (English):**
*   **Expiation for Unmentioned Defects:** For defects not mentioned here, the expiation is to offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Gāyatrī* oblations; this is the understood rule of the scriptures.
