> **बलिपात्र-लक्षणम् -**
> अथ बलिविधिं वक्ष्ये — सौवर्णं रजतं कांस्यं वा बलिपात्रं, आचार्यहस्तेन त्रिंशदङ्गुलविस्तारं भुजङ्गार्धविस्तारं (मुद्रितपाठे भुवङ्गाऽर्धविस्तारं) वा, समवृत्तं, तदर्धमेकाङ्गुलोन्नतं (मुद्रितपाठे तमरित एकाङ्गुलोन्नतं), सीमावृत्तं, तन्मध्ये पद्मस्य कर्णिकावत् अष्टाङ्गुलोन्नतं वृत्तं, तत्परितो द्व्यङ्गुलायतैः अष्टादलैर्युक्तं कारयेत्।
> ***balipātra-lakṣaṇam -***
> *atha balividhiṃ vakṣye — sauvarṇaṃ rajataṃ kāṃsyaṃ vā balipātraṃ, ācāryahastena triṃśadaṅgulavistāraṃ bhajaṅgārdhavistāraṃ vā, samavṛttaṃ, tadardham ekāṅgulonnataṃ, sīmāvṛttaṃ, tanmadhye padmasya karṇikāvad aṣṭāṅgulonnataṃ vṛttaṃ, tatparito dvyaṅgulāyataiḥ aṣṭādalairyuktaṃ kārayet.*

> **बलिदान-विधिः -**
> द्विप्रस्थैः शुद्धैस्तण्डुलैश्चरुं पक्त्वा, पात्रं प्रक्षाल्य, अधिदैवं बलिरक्षकम् अभ्यर्च्य, संशोध्य, चरुं प्रक्षिप्य, अभिघार्य, अन्नं संमर्द्य, कर्णिकाकारे द्वादशाङ्गुलं दशाङ्गुलं अष्टाङ्गुलं (मुद्रितपाठे मष्टाऽङ्गुलं) वा उन्नतम्, मूले द्विगुणपरिणाहम्, अग्रे अर्धपरिणाहं एवं बलिं कृत्वा, देवाभिमुखे निधाय, पूर्ववत् कौतुकाङ्गुलौ (मुद्रितपाठे कौतुकाङ्गलौ) देवं ध्यात्वा, 'योगेशं परब्रह्माणं परमात्मानं भक्तवत्सल'मिति मन्त्रैः समावाह्य, आसनाद्यैः एकादशोपचारैः अर्चयेत्।
> ***balidāna-vidhiḥ -***
> *dviprasthaiḥ śuddhaistaṇḍulaiścaruṃ paktvā, pātraṃ prakṣālya, adhidaivaṃ balirakṣakam abhyarcya, saṃśodhya, caruṃ praxipya, abhighārya, annaṃ saṃmardya, karṇikākāre dvādaśāṅgulaṃ daśāṅgulaṃ aṣṭāṅgulaṃ vā unnatam, mūle dviguṇapariṇāham, agre ardhapariṇāhaṃ evaṃ baliṃ kṛtvā, devābhimukhe nidhāya, pūrvavat koutukāṅgulou devaṃ dhyātvā, 'yogeśaṃ parabrahmāṇaṃ paramātmanaṃ bhaktavatsalam' iti mantraiḥ samāvāhya, āsanādyaiḥ ekādaśopacāraiḥ arcayet.*

> उत्तमे: त्रिकालेषु अन्नबलिं; मध्यमे: प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः; अधमे: मध्याह्न एव। सायंप्रातः अन्नाभावे अक्षतं कुडुबं (मुद्रितपाठे कुडुपं) यथालाभं वा पात्रे निधाय, अक्षतैः देवं समाराध्यार्चयेत्।
> *uttame: trikāleṣu annabaliṃ; madhyame: prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ; adhame: madhyāhna eva. sāyaṃprātaḥ annābhāve axataṃ kuḍubaṃ yathālābhaṃ vā pātre nidhāya, axataiḥ devaṃ samārādhyārcayet.*

> **नृत्तादि-सेवा -**
> गणिकाः देवदासीर्वा आहूय, नृत्तमण्डपमध्ये मण्डलम् उपलिप्य, अप्सरसश्च आवाह्य (मुद्रितपाठे अप्सरसश्चावाहा), ता अप्सरसः संस्मृत्वा, प्रोक्ष्य, पुष्पं दत्त्वा, नृत्तगेयं च कारयेत्।
> ***nṛttādi-sevā -***
> *gaṇikāḥ devadāsīrvā āhūya, nṛttamaṇḍapamadhyye maṇḍalam upalipya, apsarasāśca āvāhya, tā apsarasaḥ saṃsmṛtvā, prokṣya, puṣpaṃ dattva, nṛttageyaṃ ca kārayet.*
> 
> **बलि-प्रस्थानम् -**
> पश्चात् शिष्यम् आहूय, कटकाङ्गुलीयोष्णीषोत्तरीयाभरणादिभिः अलङ्कृत्य, गरुडं बुद्ध्वा (मुद्रितपाठे गरुडंबुध्वा), आराध्य, 'अतो देवा' इत्यादिना देवम् अनुमन्त्य (मुद्रितपाठे देवमनुमान्य), 'उद्यन्तम्' इति बलिम् उद्धृत्य, 'देवस्य त्वा' इति शिष्यस्य शिरसि स्थापयेत्। व्यजनैः धूपदीपैः हेमादिपरिच्छदैः वितानादिभिः यथाशक्त्यलङ्कारैः भक्तैश्च सहितो गच्छेत्। ततो वाद्यघोषसमायुक्तं दिक्षु अष्टासु नृत्तगेयं च कारयेत्।
> ***bali-prasthānam -***
> *paścāt śiṣyam āhūya, kaṭakāṅgulīyoṣṇīṣottarīyābharaṇādibhiḥ alaṅkṛtya, garuḍaṃ buddhvā, ārādhya, 'ato devā' ityādinā devam anumantya, 'udyantam' iti balim uddhṛtya, 'devasya tvā' iti śiṣyasya śirasi sthāpayet. vyajanaiḥ dhūpadīpaiḥ hemādiparicchadaiḥ vitānādibhiḥ yathāśaktyalaṅkāraiḥ bhaktaiśca sahito gacchet. tato vādyaghoṣasamāyuktaṃ dikṣu aṣṭāsu nṛttageyaṃ ca kārayet.*
> 
> **बलि-प्रदक्षिणा-क्रमः -**
> आवरणद्वयाधिके सर्वेषु प्रथमादि प्रत्येकं सकृत्, द्वितीये त्रिः, प्रथमे त्रिरेव वा, प्रथमे सकृत्, द्वितीये द्विरित्येके (मुद्रितपाठे द्वितीयेद्वि रित्येके)। 'प्र तद्विष्णुः' इति प्रदक्षिणं कारयेत्। अन्तः प्रविश्य, भूतपीठान्तरे तिष्ठन् (मुद्रितपाठे भूतवीशान्तरेतिष्ठन्) पादौ प्रक्षालयेत्। अर्चकः हस्ताभ्यां बलिम् आदाय, देवाभिमुखे न्यस्य, कौतुके देवम् ओङ्कारेण समारोपयेत्। बल्यग्रम् आदाय विष्वक्सेनाय दत्त्वा, तच्छेषं भूतपीठे सोदकं क्षिपेत्।
> ***bali-pradakṣiṇā-kramaḥ -***
> *āvaraṇadvayādhike sarveṣu prathamādi pratyekaṃ sakṛt, dvitīye triḥ, prathame trireva vā, prathame sakṛt, dvitīye dvirityeke. 'pra tadvaiṣṇuḥ' iti pradakṣiṇaṃ kārayet. antaḥ praviśya, bhūtapīṭhāntare tiṣṭhan pādau prakṣālayet. arcakaḥ hastābhyāṃ balim ādāya, devābhimukhe nyasya, koutuke devam oṅkāreṇa samāropayet. balyagram ādāya viṣvaksenāya dattva, taccheṣaṃ bhūtapīṭhe sodakaṃ xiṣpet.*
> 
> **नित्योत्सव-विधिः -**
> नित्योत्सवम् इच्छन् बलिबेरम् आराध्य, हविर्निवेद्य, शिष्यस्य शिरसि स्थापयित्वा, बल्यग्रतो नयेत्। अथवा शिबिकायां गरुडं बुद्ध्वा आराध्य, देवं समारोप्य, पूर्वोक्तगुणसम्पन्नैः शिष्यैः वाहयित्वा, सर्वालङ्कारसंयुक्तं बलिना सह पूर्ववत् प्रदक्षिणं कारयेत्। पश्चाद् अभ्यन्तरं प्रविश्य, अलङ्कृते मण्डपे पीठे देवं संन्यस्य, पाद्याद्यर्घ्यान्तम् अभ्यर्च्य, नीराजनं कृत्वा, नृत्तगेयवाद्यैः विनोदं कारयित्वा, पूर्ववत् पादुकाऽर्चनं (मुद्रितपाठे पादुकार्चनं) कृत्वा, अभ्यन्तरे देवं संस्थापयेत्; एवं नित्योत्सवं त्रिसन्ध्यं सायम्प्रातर्वा कुर्यात्।
> ***nityotsava-vidhiḥ -***
> *nityotsavam icchan baliberam ārādhya, havirnivedya, śiṣyasya śirasi sthāpayitvā, balyagrato nayet. athavā śibikāyāṃ garuḍaṃ buddhvā ārādhya, devaṃ samāropya, pūrvoktaguṇasampannaiḥ śiṣyaiḥ vāhayitvā, sarvālaṅkārasaṃyuktaṃ balinā saha pūrvavat pradakṣiṇaṃ kārayet. paścād abhyantaraṃ praviśya, alaṅkṛte maṇḍape pīṭhe devaṃ saṃnyasya, pādyādyarghyāntam abhyarcya, nīrājanaṃ kṛtvā, nṛttageyavādyaiḥ vinodaṃ kārayitvā, pūrvavat pādukā'rcanaṃ kṛtvā, abhyantare devaṃ saṃsthāpayet; evaṃ nityotsavaṃ trisaṃdhyaṃ sāyamprātarvā kuryāt.*
> 
> **नित्योत्सव-फलम् -**
> नित्योत्सवेन महोत्सवफलं लभेत, भगवान् प्रीतो भवेत्। राज्ञः राष्ट्रस्य च बलवृद्धिर्भवति। बलिभ्रमणकाले ये सेवन्ते ते सर्वे पदे पदे यज्ञफलं लभेरन्। नव-षट्-पञ्च-मूर्ति-विमाने विष्णोः आदिमूर्तेरेव बलिम् आचरेत्।
> ***nityotsava-phalam -***
> *nityotsavena mahotsavaphalaṃ labheta, bhagavān prīto bhavet. rājñaḥ rāṣṭrasya ca balavṛddhirbhavati. balibhramaṇakāle ye sevante te sarve pade pade yajñaphalaṃ labheran. nava-ṣaṭ-pañca-mūrti-vimāne viṣṇoḥ ādimūrtereva balim ācaret.*

> **द्वात्रिंशदुपचाराः -**
> अथ अर्चनाङ्गोपचारान् वक्ष्ये — उपचारो विग्रहश्चैकार्थीभवतः; १. आसनं, २. स्वागतं, ३. अनुमानं, ४. पाद्यं, ५. आचमनीयं, ६. पुष्पम्, ७. गन्धः, ८. धूपः, ९. दीपः, १०. अर्घ्यं, ११. आचमनीयं, १२. स्नानं, १३. प्लोतं, १४. वस्त्रं, १५. उत्तरीयम्, १६. यज्ञोपवीतं, १७. पाद्यं, १८. आचमनीयं, १९. पुष्पम्, २०. गन्धः, २१. धूपः, २२. दीपः, २३. आचमनीयं, २४. हविः, २५. पानीयं, २६. आचमनीयं, २७. मुखवासः, २८. बलिः, २९. प्रणामः, ३०. प्रदक्षिणा, ३१. पुष्पाञ्जलिः, ३२. स्तुतिः — इति द्वात्रिंशदुपचाराः। एते नृत्तगेयसंयुक्ताः उत्तमोत्तमम् इत्याहुः ॥

> **उपचार-भेदाः -**
> मुखवासान्ताः प्रणामदक्षिणायुताः नवविंशत्युपचाराः; मुखवासान्ताः दक्षिणायुताः अष्टाविंशत्युपचाराः; अर्घ्याचमनान्ताः प्रणामदक्षिणायुताः त्रयोदशोपचाराः; अर्घ्याचमनान्ताः एकादशोपचाराः (मुद्रितपाठे एकादशो प्रचाराः); पाद्य-अर्घ्य-आचमनान्ताः अष्टोपचाराः; पुष्प-अर्घ्य-आचमनान्ताः षडुपचाराः; पुष्पदानं प्रणामश्च द्वौ उपचारौ; प्रणामः एकोपचारः।
> ***upacāra-bhedāḥ -***
> *mukhavāsāntāḥ praṇāmadaṣiṇāyutāḥ navaviṃśatyupacārāḥ; mukhavāsāntāḥ daṣiṇāyutāḥ aṣṭāviṃśatyupacārāḥ; arghyācamanāntāḥ praṇāmadaṣiṇāyutāḥ trayodaśopacārāḥ; arghyācamanāntāḥ ekādaśopacārāḥ; pādyārghyācamanāntāḥ aṣṭopacārāḥ; puṣpārghyācamanāntāḥ ṣadupacārāḥ; puṣpadānaṃ praṇāmaśca dvou upacārou; praṇāmaḥ ekopacāraḥ.*
> 
> त्रयोदशविग्रहाणाम् आदौ प्रधानम् आवाहनं, अन्ते विसर्गश्च, आसन-स्नपनं च कुर्यात्। सर्वत्रैकोपचारः द्वावुपचारौ च, होमे षडुपचारान्, स्नपने अष्टोपचारान्, हविषि हीने त्रयोदशोपचारान् अष्टोपचारान् वा, हविषि लब्धे अष्टाविंशत्युपचारान् नवविंशत्युपचारान् वा, विमानार्चनायामेव द्वात्रिंशदुपचाराः... **(ग्रन्थपातः)**
> *trayodaśavigrahāṇām ādou pradhānam āvāhanaṃ, ante visargaśca, āsana-snapanāni ca kuryāt. sarvatraikopacāraḥ dvāvupacārau ca, home ṣadupacārān, snapane aṣṭopacārān, haviṣi hīne trayodaśopacārān aṣṭopacārān vā, haviṣi labdhe aṣṭāviṃśatyupacārān navaviṃśatyupacārān vā, vimānārcanāyāmeva dvātriṃśadupacārāḥ... **(granthapātaḥ)***

#### English Translation
*   **The Design of the Offering Vessel (*Bali-pātra*):** Now I shall describe the rules for the offering (*bali*) ritual: the offering vessel (*bali-pātra*) should be made of gold, silver, or bronze, measuring thirty inches (*aṅgula*) by the hand of the *ācārya* (teacher/priest), or half that size (*bhujanga-ardha-vistāra*), perfectly circular.

*   **Dimensions of the Vessel:** It should have a rim raised by one inch (*ekāṅgula-unnata*), and in its center, there should be a circular portion raised by eight inches (*aṣṭāṅgula-unnata*) like the pericarp of a lotus, surrounded by eight petals measuring two inches in length.

*   **Preparation of the offering (*Bali*):** Having cooked the oblation (*caru*) with two *prasthas* of clean rice, washed the vessel, worshipped the guardian deity of the offering (*bali-rakṣaka*), purified it, placed the *caru* in it, sprinkled it with ghee, and kneaded the food.

*   **Shaping the *Bali* Food:** The *bali* food should be shaped like a lotus pericarp, twelve, ten, or eight inches high, with a base double the circumference and the tip half the circumference, and placed facing the deity.

*   **Invocation and Worship:** Having meditated on the Lord in the *kautuka-bera* (portable icon) as before, and invoked Him with the mantras "Yogeśa, Parabrahman, Paramātman, Bhaktavatsala," one should worship Him with the eleven services (*ekādaśa-upacāra*) starting with offering a seat (*āsana*).

*   **Schedule of Daily Offerings:** In the highest category (*uttama*), food offerings (*anna-bali*) are made three times a day (morning, noon, and evening); in the middle category (*madhyama*), in the morning and noon; and in the lowest category (*adhama*), only at noon.

*   **Alternative Offerings:** In the evening and morning, if cooked food is unavailable, one should place raw unbroken rice (*akṣata*) or a cup (*kuḍuba*) of rice as available in the vessel, and worship the Lord with *akṣata*.

*   **Devotional Dances and Music:** Summoning the courtesans or temple dancers (*gaṇikā* or *devadāsī*), plastering a circular design (*maṇḍala*) in the center of the dance hall (*nṛtta-maṇḍapa*), invoking the celestial nymphs (*apsaras*), worshipping them, contemplating those *apsaras*, sprinkling them, and offering flowers, one should have dance and music performed.

*   **Role of the Disciple representing Garuḍa:** Afterwards, having summoned the disciple, decorating him with bracelets, rings, turban, upper garment, ornaments, etc., identifying him as Garuḍa, worshipping him, and obtaining permission from the deity with the mantra "Ato devā," one should lift the *bali* with the mantra "Udyantam" and place it on the disciple's head with "Devasya tvā."

*   **The Ritual Procession:** One should proceed accompanied by fans (*vyajana*), incense, lamps, golden vessels, canopies, and decorations according to capability, along with devotees.

*   **Musical Proclamations:** Then, accompanied by the sound of musical instruments (*vādya-ghoṣa*), one should perform dance and music in the eight directions.

*   **Circumambulation Paths:** If there are more than two enclosures (*āvaraṇa*), the circumambulation should be done once in all enclosures starting from the first, three times in the second, or three times in the first, or once in the first and twice in the second according to some authorities.

*   **Concluding the Procession:** One should perform the circumambulation with the mantra "Pra tad viṣṇuḥ," enter inside the temple, stand at the *bhūta-pīṭha* (or between the *bhūta* and *īśa* quarters), and wash the feet.

*   **Returning the Divine Energy:** The priest (*arcaka*) should take the *bali* in both hands, place it facing the deity, and return the divine energy to the *kautuka-bera* with the sound of "Om".

*   **Concluding Offerings:** Taking the first portion (*balyagra*) of the offering, he should offer it to Viṣvaksena, and throw the remaining portion with water onto the *bhūta-pīṭha*.

*   **The Daily Festival (*Nityotsava*):** One desiring the daily festival (*nityotsava*) should worship the *bali-bera* (processional icon), offer oblation (*havis*), place it on the disciple's head, and carry it behind the *bali* food.

*   **Palanquin Procession option:** Alternatively, placing the deity on a palanquin (*śibikā*), invoking Garuḍa, mounting the Lord, having Him carried by disciples endowed with the aforementioned qualities, fully decorated, one should circumambulate with the *bali* as before.

*   **Concluding the Festival:** Afterwards, entering the interior, placing the deity on the pedestal in the decorated hall (*maṇḍapa*), worshipping Him with services from *pādya* (foot-water) up to *arghya* (water offering), performing the waving of lights (*nīrājana*), entertaining with dance, vocal and instrumental music, worshipping the sandals (*pādukā-arcanam*) as before, one should establish the deity in the inner sanctum.

*   **Daily Festival Frequency:** Thus, the daily festival should be performed three times a day, or morning and evening.

*   **Blessings of *Nityotsava*:** By the daily festival, one obtains the fruit of the great festival (*mahotsava*), the Lord becomes pleased, and the strength of the king and the nation increases.

*   **Spiritual merit for devotees:** All who worship during the circumambulation of the *bali* obtain the fruit of a sacrifice (*yajña*) at every step.

*   **Multiple Deity Layouts:** In a temple containing nine, six, or five icons (*nava-ṣaṭ-pañca-mūrti-vimāna*), the *bali* should be offered only to the primary icon (*ādi-mūrti*) of Viṣṇu.
