# Chapter 64: Expiations for Fire, Lightning, and Wind Damage to the Snapanamaṇḍapa and Other Halls (Snapanamaṇḍapādau agnidagdhe aśanivātahate vā prāyaścittam)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules and Expiatory Rites for Damage by Fire, Lightning, and Wind, and Icon Defects

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ चतुःषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **(स्नपनमण्डपादिदाहादौ प्रायश्चित्त-विधिः)**
> ***atha catuḥṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***(snapanamaṇḍapādidāhādau prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)***
> 
> प्रमादात् आलये स्नपनमण्डपे च आस्थानमण्डपे महानसे प्राकार-गोपुर-स्नानोदक-पानीयादि-स्थानेषु अग्निदग्धेषु अशनिपात-वाताहतेषु वा, पञ्चाग्नौ पारमात्मिकं (पारमात्मिकम् ईङ्कारादीन्), मिन्दाहुतिं, आग्नेयं च व्याहृत्यन्तं हुत्वा, पुनस्सन्धानं कारयेत् ।
> *pramādāt ālaye snapanamaṇḍape ca āsthānamaṇḍape mahānase prākāra-gopura-snānodaka-pānīyādi-sthāneṣu agnidagdheṣu aśanipāta-vātāhateṣu vā, pañcāgnou pāramātmikaṃ (pāramātmikam īṅkārādīn), mindāhutiṃ, āgneyaṃ ca vyāhṛtyantaṃ hutvā, punassandhānaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> शिलाग्रहणकाले दारुग्रहणकाले वा क्रियाहीने विपरीते च, वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, मिन्दाहुतिं विच्छिन्नं च व्याहृत्यन्तं जुहुयात् ।
> *śilāgrahaṇakāle dārugrahaṇakāle vā kriyāhīne viparīte ca, vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, mindāhutiṃ vicchinnaṃ ca vyāhṛtyantaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> द्वारस्तम्भ-भुवङ्ग-पतङ्ग-कवाट-बोधिका-उत्तर-तुला-स्तम्भादिषु प्रमाणहीनेषु दोषदुष्टेषु अग्निदग्धेषु जीर्णेषु कृमिदष्टेषु च तानि त्यक्त्वा, अन्यानि आहृत्य पूर्ववत् संयोज्य, अभ्युक्ष्य, नित्याग्नौ वैष्णवं, सौम्यं, आग्नेयं च जुहुयात् ।
> *dvārastambha-bhuvaṅga-pataṅga-kavāṭa-bodhikā-uttara-tulā-stambhādiṣu pramāṇahīneṣu doṣaduṣṭeṣu agnidagdheṣu jīrṇeṣu kṛmidaṣṭeṣu ca tāni tyaktvā, anyāni āhṛtya pūrvavat saṃyojya, abhyuxya, nityāgnou vaiṣṇavaṃ, soumyaṃ, āgneyaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> गर्भन्यासार्थं पीठन्यासार्थं च रत्नानाम् अलाभे सुवर्णं निक्षिप्य, विष्णुसूक्तं; धातूनाम् अलाभे पारदं ब्राह्मं रौद्रं च; बीजानाम् अलाभे यवं मुद्गं, वायव्यं वैष्णवं च प्रायश्चित्तं जुहुयात् ।
> *garbhavinyāsārthaṃ pīṭhavinyāsārthaṃ ca ratnānām alābhe suvarṇaṃ nixipya, viṣṇusūktaṃ; dhātūnām alābhe pāradaṃ brāhmaṃ raudraṃ ca; bījānām alābhe yavaṃ mudgaṃ, vāyavyaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca prāyaścittaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> ध्रुवबेरार्थ-शूलाग्रहणे शूलस्थापन-विपर्यासे च, ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, वैष्णवं व्याहृत्यन्तं हुत्वा विधिना संयोजयेत् ।
> *dhruvaberārtha-śūlāgrahaṇe śūlasthāpana-viparyāse ca, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, vaiṣṇavaṃ vyāhṛtyantaṃ hutvā vidhinā saṃyojayet.*
> 
> अप्रमाणे विमाने प्रमाणहीनं बेरम् अज्ञानात् स्थापितं चेत्, तत् राज-राष्ट्र-विनाशाय भवति। तद्-दोषशमनार्थं महाशान्तिं हुत्वा, तद् विमानं तत्-तत्-बेरं च विधिना कृत्वा विधिवत् स्थापयेत् ।
> *apramāṇe vimāne pramāṇahīnaṃ beram ajñānāt sthāpitaṃ cet, tat rāja-rāṣṭra-vināśāya bhavati. tad-doṣaśamanārthaṃ mahāśāntiṃ hutvā, tad vimānaṃ tat-tat-beraṃ ca vidhinā kṛtvā vidhivat sthāpayet.*
> 
> बेरोक्त-शूलानां प्रत्येकं तदुक्तमान-द्वि-त्रि-चतुः-पञ्च-षड्-यवमात्रं हीनं चेत्, ताम्रेण सम्पूर्णं कृत्वा, वैष्णवं, पुरुषसूक्तं, 'दद्भ्यः स्वाहा' इति अङ्गहोमं च हुत्वा पुनरारभेत ।
> *berōkta-śūlānāṃ pratyekaṃ taduktāmāna-dvi-tri-catuḥ-pañca-ṣaḍ-yavamātraṃ hīnaṃ cet, tāmreṇa sampūrṇaṃ kṛtvā, vaiṣṇavaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, 'dadbhyah svāhā' iti aṅgahomaṃ ca hutvā punarārabhet.*
> 
> **(बेरनिर्माण-दोष-प्रायश्चितत्त्वम्)**
> स्नेहद्रव्य-चूर्ण-कषाय-ओषधि-धूप-हीनेषु प्रयोग-विपर्यासेषु च, रज्जुबन्ध-अष्टबन्ध-मृदालेपन-घटीशर्करालेपन-पटाच्छादन-आभरणादिषु क्रमहीनेषु वर्णव्यतिक्रमे च, वैष्णवं, ब्राह्मं, ऐन्द्रं, आग्नेयं, महाभूतदैवत्यं, प्राजापत्यं च व्याहृत्यन्तं हुत्वा यथाविधि यथास्थाने क्रमेण कारयेत् ।
> ***(beranirmāṇa-doṣa-prāyaścittam)***
> *snehadravya-cūrṇa-kaṣāya-oṣadhi-dhūpa-hīneṣu prayoga-viparyāseṣu ca, rajjubandha-aṣṭabandha-mṛdālepana-ghaṭīśarkarālepana-paṭācchādana-ābharaṇādiṣu kramahīneṣu varṇavyatikrame ca, vaiṣṇavaṃ, brāhmaṃ, aindraṃ, āgneyaṃ mahābhūtadaivataṃ, prājāpatyaṃ ca vyāhṛtyantaṃ hutvā yathāvidhi yathāsthāne krameṇa kārayet.*
> 
> मृण्मयं महाबेरम् अर्धचित्रं न कुर्वीत ।
> *mṛṇmayaṃ mahāberam ardha-citraṃ na kurvīta.*
> 
> सौवर्णं, रजतं, ताम्रजं, शिलाजं, दारवं, रत्नजं वा अर्धचित्रं वा कारयेत् ।
> *souvarṇaṃ, rajataṃ, tāmrajaṃ, śilājaṃ, dāravaṃ, ratnajaṃ vā ardha-citraṃ vā kārayet.*
> 
> बेरे अनुक्तवर्णैः आलिप्ते कृत्रिमवर्णैः आलिप्ते वा, निर्यासाद्भिः प्रक्षाल्य, पञ्चाग्नौ वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, जयादींश्च हुत्वा पश्चात् उक्तवर्णैः अनुलेपयेत् ।
> *bere anuktavarṇaiḥ ālipte kṛtrimavarṇaiḥ ālipte vā, niryāsādbhiḥ praxālya, pañcāgnou vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, jayādīṃśca hutvā paścāt uktavarṇaiḥ anulepayet.*
> 
> इष्टकाकल्पितं ध्रुवबेरं सुधया अयुक्तं नपुंसकवृक्षेण नपुंसकशिलाया वा कृतं, अयः-पाषाणचूर्ण-लशुन-हिङ्गु-तैलाद्यैः संयुक्तं, पक्वमृण्मयं शूलोपरि रज्जुबन्धहीनं, मृद्धीनं, घटीशर्करा-कल्कहीनं, प्रमाणहीनं, मुक्तवर्णहीनं, भूषणहीनं, अज्ञानाद् अर्धलोभाद् वा स्थापितं चेत्, तद् आभिचारिकं भवति ।
> *iṣṭakākalpitaṃ dhruvaberaṃ sudhayā ayuktaṃ napuṃsakavṛxeṇa napuṃsakaśilayā vā kṛtaṃ, ayaḥ-pāṣāṇacūrṇa-laśuna-hiṅgu-tailādyaiah saṃyuktaṃ, pakvamṛṇmayaṃ śūlopari rajjubandhahīnaṃ, mṛdhīnaṃ, ghaṭīśarkarā-kalkahīnaṃ, pramāṇahīnaṃ, muktavarṇahīnaṃ, bhūṣaṇahīnaṃ, ajñānād ardhalobhād vā sthāpitaṃ cet, tad ābhicārikaṃ bhavati.*
> 
> तद् शीघ्रम् अपहाय, पञ्चाग्नौ वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, 'यद्देवाः' इति मन्त्रैः ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, ऐन्द्रं, आग्नेयं, वारुणं, सर्वदैवत्यं, पारमात्मिकं च जुहुयात् ।
> *tad śīghram apahāya, pañcāgnou vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, 'yad devāḥ' iti mantraiah brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, aindraṃ, āgneyaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, sarvadaivataṃ, pāramātmikaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> एषा महाशान्तिः सर्वदोषोपशमनी भवति ।
> *eṣā mahāśāntiḥ sarvadoṣopaśamanī bhavati.*
> 
> पश्चात् बेरं विधिवत् संस्कृत्य विधिना स्थापयेत् ।
> *paścāt beraṃ vidhivat saṃskṛtya vidhinā sthāpayet.*
> 
> आरकूट-अयः-वृत्तलोहादि-अनुक्तद्रव्यैः कृतं कौतुकादिबेरम् अज्ञानात् स्थापितं चेत्, तद् आभिचारिकं भवति। तद्-दोषशमनार्थं महाशान्तिं हुत्वा बेरं सलक्षणं कृत्वा विधिना स्थापयेत्। कौतुकं स्थितम् आसीनं वा कारयेत्।
> *ārakūṭa-ayaḥ-vṛttalohādi-anuktadravyaiah kṛtaṃ kautukādiberaṃ ajñānāt sthāpitaṃ cet, tad ābhicārikaṃ bhavati. tad-doṣaśamanārthaṃ mahāśāntiṃ hutvā beraṃ salaxaṇaṃ kṛtvā vidhinā sthāpayet. kautukaṃ sthitam āsīnaṃ vā kārayet.*
> 
> नैव शयानं दैविके आर्षे पौरुषे च, यथापूर्वं तथा आचरेत् । अन्यथा दोषो भवति।
> *naiva śayānaṃ daivike ārṣe pauruṣe ca, yathāpūrvaṃ tathā ācaret. anyathā doṣo bhavati.*
> 
> कौतुकादिबेरे निर्दोषे अर्च्यमाने तत्र उत्कृष्टद्रव्यकृतबेर-प्रवेशं नैव कारयेत्। कुर्यात् चेत् यजमान-विनाशनं भवति।
> *kautukādibere nirdoṣe arcyamāne tatra utkṛṣṭadravyakṛtabera-praveśaṃ naiva kārayet. kuryāt cet yajamāna-vināśanaṃ bhavati.*
> 
> अर्च्यमाने कौतुकादिबेरे विरूपे, विवर्णे, झर्झराद्यैर्युक्ते वा, तच्छीह्रि (तच्छक्तिं) महाबेरे समारोप्य, नवीकृत्य, बिम्बशुद्धिं कृत्वा पुनः स्थापयेत् ।
> *arcyamāne kautukādibere virūpe, vivarṇe, jharjharādyaiah yunkte vā, tacchaktiṃ mahābere samāropya, navīkṛtya, bimbaśuddhiṃ kṛtvā punaḥ sthāpayet.*
> 
> **(बेरापहरणे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> अर्च्यमानं कौतुकबिम्बं नृपादिभिः चोरैः शत्रुभिर्वा अपहृतं चेत्, तद् देशशुद्धिं कृत्वा, महाशान्तिं हुत्वा, तस्मिन् रत्नं सुवर्णकूर्चं वा निक्षिप्य, ध्रुवात् अर्कमण्डलाद् वा देवम् आवाह्य, नित्यं समभ्यर्च्य, पूर्वद्रव्येण उत्कृष्टद्रव्येण वा पूर्ववद् बेरं सलक्षणं कृत्वा, कालानपेक्षं पूर्ववत् प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत्।
> ***(berāpaharaṇe prāyaścittam)***
> *arcyamānaṃ kautukabimbaṃ nṛpādibhiḥ coraiah śatrubhirvā apahṛtaṃ cet, tad deśaśuddhiṃ kṛtvā, mahāśāntiṃ hutvā, tasmin ratnaṃ suvarṇakūrcaṃ vā nixipya, dhruvāt arkamaṇḍalād vā devam āvāhya, nityaṃ samabhyarcya, pūrvadravyeṇa utkṛṣṭadravyeṇa vā pūrvavad beraṃ salaxaṇaṃ kṛtvā, kālānapexaṃ pūrvavad pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> मधूच्छिष्टक्रियां विना बेरं कृतं चेत्, वैष्णवं, ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, सौरं, आग्नेयं च प्रायश्चित्तं यजेत् ।
> *madhūcchiṣṭakriyāṃ vinā beraṃ kṛtaṃ cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, souraṃ, āgneyaṃ ca prāyaścittaṃ yajet.*
> 
> अज्ञानाद् अन्यालये स्थापितं चेत्, तद्-दोषशमनार्थं महाशान्तिं हुत्वा, वैष्णवान् सम्पूज्य, ब्राह्मणान् भोजयित्वा, पूर्वस्थाने विधिना स्थापयेत्।
> *ajñānād anyālaye sthāpitaṃ cet, tad-doṣaśamanārthaṃ mahāśāntiṃ hutvā, vaiṣṇavān sampūjya, brāhmaṇān bhojayitvā, pūrvasthāne vidhinā sthāpayet.*
> 
> ग्रामादीनाम् आलयस्य च विनाशे तद् बेरम् आहृत्य आलयान्तरे यावत्कालं अर्चनं युक्तं तावत्कालं यथालाभम् अर्चयेत्।
> *grāmādīnām ālayasya ca vināṣe tad beram āhṛtya ālayāntare yāvatkālaṃ arcanaṃ yuktaṃ tāvatkālaṃ yathālābham arcayet.*
> 
> **(बेरमानदोष-लक्षणानि)**
> पीठसंघाते कृते तद् बेरे दक्षिणावनते मृत्युः, पश्चिमावनते अर्थहानिः, उत्तरावनते अभिवृद्धिः, पूर्वावनते पुत्रहानिः, कुक्षिच्छिद्रे धान्यनाशः, उदरच्छिद्रे धनक्षयः, कृशे कार्श्यं, स्थूले महाव्याधिः, दीर्घे अनायुष्यं, ह्रासे अनावृष्टिः भवति।
> ***(beramānadoṣa-laxaṇāni)***
> *pīṭhasaṅghāte kṛte tad bere daxiṇāvanate mṛtyuḥ, paścimāvanate arthahāniḥ, uttarāvanate abhivṛddhiḥ, pūrvāvanate putrahāniḥ, kuxicchidre dhānyanāśaḥ, udaracchidre dhanaxayaḥ, kṛśe kārśyaṃ, sthūle mahāvyādhiḥ, dīrghe anāyuṣyaṃ, hrāse anāvṛṣṭiḥ bhavati.*
> 
> अन्याङ्गेषु न्यूनाधिकेषु सर्वहानिः भवति, तस्मात् सर्वप्रयत्नेन बेरं सलक्षणं कृत्वा विधिना स्थापयेत्।
> *anyāṅgeṣu nyūnādhikeṣu sarvahāniḥ bhavati, tasmāt sarvaprayatnena beraṃ salaxaṇaṃ kṛtvā vidhinā sthāpayet.*
> 
> ध्रुवस्थापनात् पश्चात् महाप्रतिष्ठायाः पूर्वम् अनुक्तप्रायश्चित्तं वक्ष्ये - कापिलेन घृतेन पञ्चाग्नौ वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, व्याहृत्यन्तं यजेत् ॥
> *dhruvasthāpanāt paścāt mahāpratiṣṭhāyāḥ pūrvam anuktaprāyaścittaṃ vaxye - kāpilena vyāhṛtyantaṃ yajet.*

#### English Translation
*   **Accidental Fire and Storms:** If, due to negligence, the main temple, the *snapana-maṇḍapa* (hall of ablution), the *āsthāna-maṇḍapa* (assembly hall), the *mahānasa* (kitchen), the walls (*prākāra*), gateways (*gopura*), or bathing/drinking water structures are damaged by fire, struck by lightning, or destroyed by wind, perform oblations in the five fires (*pañcāgni*) with the *Pāramātmika* hymns, the *īṅkāra-ādi* seed-syllables, the *mindāhuti* (oblation for errors), the *vicchinna-homa* (oblation for interruptions), and the *āgneya* (fire-deity) oblations ending with the *vyāhṛtis*, and then execute the repairs and re-joining.

*   **Errors in Quarrying Material:** If during the stone-gathering (*śilā-grahaṇa*) or wood-gathering (*dāru-grahaṇa*) rites, there is a deficiency in ritual actions (*kriyā-hīna*) or contrary performance (*viparīta*), offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *mindāhuti*, and *vicchinna* oblations ending with the *vyāhṛtis*.

*   **Damage to Architectural Elements:** If the doorposts, thresholds, lintels, doors, brackets, lintel-supports, crossbeams, pillars, and other architectural members are incorrect in measurement, defective, damaged by fire, worn out, or insect-damaged, discard them, bring other materials, assemble them as before, sprinkle them with holy water, and offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Saumya*, and *Āgneya* oblations in the daily fire (*nityāgni*).

*   **Deficiencies in Sacred Deposits:** For the womb-deposit (*garbha-nyāsa*) and pedestal-deposit (*pīṭha-nyāsa*), if gems are unavailable, place gold and offer the *Viṣṇu-sūkta*; if metals are unavailable, place mercury (*pārada*) and offer the *Brāhma* and *Raudra* oblations; if seeds are unavailable, use barley (*yava*) and green gram (*mudga*) and offer the *Vāyavya* and *Vaiṣṇava* expiatory (*prāyaścitta*) oblations.

*   **Errors in Wooden Core Assembly:** If during the acquisition of the wooden core (*śūla*) for the *dhruva-bera* (stationary deity), or if there is a reversal in the installation of the core, offer the *Brāhma*, *Raudra*, and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations ending with the *vyāhṛtis*, and assemble them according to the rules.

*   **Incorrect Proportions of Shrines and Icons:** If, out of ignorance, an incorrectly proportioned icon (*bera*) is installed in a temple of incorrect measurements, it leads to the destruction of the king and the kingdom; to pacify that defect, perform the great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*), remake the temple and the icon according to the rules, and install them properly.

*   **Deficiencies in Core Dimensions:** If each of the prescribed core timbers (*śūla*) for the icon is short of the specified measurements by two, three, four, five, or six barley-widths (*yava*), cover the deficient part with copper, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Puruṣa-sūkta* oblations, perform the limb-oblation (*aṅga-homa*) reciting "Dadbhyah svāhā", and then resume the work.

*   **Errors in Intermediate Preparatory Layers:** If there are delays or reversals in the application of oil mixtures, powders, decoctions, herbs, incense, rope-binding (*rajju-bandha*), eight-fold binding (*aṣṭa-bandha*), clay-plastering (*mṛd-ālepana*), mortar-plastering (*ghaṭī-śarkarā-ālepana*), cloth-covering, or ornamentation, or if there is a confusion in colors, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Brāhma*, *Aindra*, *Āgneya*, *Mahābhūtadaivata*, and *Prājāpatya* oblations ending with the *vyāhṛtis*, and carry out the steps in order according to the rules at the proper place.

*   **Clay Image Configurations:** A main stationary deity (*mahā-bera*) made of clay must not be fashioned as a low-relief/half-carved image (*ardha-citra*).

*   **Allowable Forms for Other Materials:** Icons made of gold, silver, copper, stone, wood, or gems may be fashioned as half-carved (*ardha-citra*) or fully-sculpted (*citra*).

*   **Errors in Icon Painting:** If the icon is painted with unprescribed or artificial colors, wash it with plant resins/juices, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, and *Jaya-ādi* oblations in the five fires, and then apply the prescribed colors.

*   **Severe Defects in Brick Icons:** If a stationary deity made of brick is fashioned without plaster (*sudhayā ayuktam*), made from a neuter tree (*napuṃsaka-vṛkṣa*) or neuter stone (*napuṃsaka-śilā*), mixed with iron filings, stone powder, garlic, asafoetida, or oils (unprescribed substances), or if it is made of baked clay, lacks rope-binding over the core, lacks clay layer, lacks mortar-kalka layer, is incorrect in measurements, lacks colors or ornaments, and is installed out of ignorance or greed, it becomes an black-magic icon (*ābhicārika*).

*   **Removal of Defective Icons:** Remove such an icon immediately; kindle the five fires and offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, the "Yad devāḥ" hymns, and the *Brāhma*, *Raudra*, *Aindra*, *Āgneya*, *Vāruṇa*, *Sarvadaivata*, and *Pāramātmika* oblations. This great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*) pacifies all defects. Afterwards, purify the icon according to the rules and install it properly.

*   **Forbidden Materials for Processional Icons:** If a processional icon (*kautuka-bera* etc.) is made of brass, iron, bell-metal (*vṛtta-loha*), or other forbidden materials and is installed out of ignorance, it becomes a black-magic icon; to pacify that defect, perform the great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*), fashion an icon with correct attributes, and install it according to the rules. Fashion the processional icon in either a standing (*sthita*) or sitting (*āsīna*) posture.

*   **Forbidden Postures for Processional Icons:** A processional icon must never be made in a reclining (*śayāna*) posture in temples categorized as *Daivika*, *Ārṣa*, or *Pauruṣa*; do as established of old, otherwise a defect arises.

*   **No Intrusion into Shrines:** In a shrine where a defectless processional icon is already being worshiped, one must never introduce another icon made of superior materials; doing so brings about the destruction of the patron (*yajamāna*).

*   **Restoration of Damaged Icons:** If the processional icon being worshiped becomes deformed, discolored, or cracked, transfer its divine energy (*śakti*) into the main stationary deity (*mahā-bera*), renovate and purify the icon (*bimba-śuddhi*), and install it again.

*   **Theft or Loss of Icons:** If the processional icon being worshiped is carried away by kings, thieves, or enemies, perform the site-purification (*deśa-śuddhi*), offer the great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*), deposit gems or a golden brush (*suvarṇa-kūrca*) at the site, invoke the deity from the stationary deity (*dhruva-bera*) or from the solar orb (*arka-maṇḍala*), perform daily worship, carve a new icon with correct attributes from the same or superior material, and perform the consecration without waiting for an auspicious time.

*   **Icons Made Without Wax Casting:** If an icon is made without the lost-wax process (*madhūcchiṣṭa-kriyā*), perform the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Brāhma*, *Raudra*, *Saura*, and *Āgneya* expiatory oblations.

*   **Installation in Wrong Shrines:** If out of ignorance, a stationary deity (*dhruva-bera*) or processional icon (*kautuka-bera*) is installed in another temple, perform the great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*), worship the Vaiṣṇavas, feed the Brahmins, and install it back in its original place according to the rules.

*   **Destruction of Village or Temple:** If the village or the temple is destroyed, rescue the icon, place it in another temple, and perform the worship there with whatever resources are available as long as it is appropriate.

*   **Signs and Faults of Icon Proportions:** If after the pedestal-assembly is completed, the icon leans to the south, it causes death; if it leans to the west, loss of wealth; if it leans to the north, prosperity; if it leans to the east, loss of sons; a hole in the side of the belly (*kukṣi-cchidra*) causes loss of grain; a hole in the abdomen (*udara-cchidra*) causes depletion of wealth; if the icon is too thin (*kṛśa*), it causes emaciation to the patron; if too thick (*sthūla*), great disease; if too tall (*dīrgha*), short life; if too short (*hrāsa*), drought.

*   **Defects in Other Limbs:** If other limbs are deficient or excessive, complete ruin occurs; therefore, with all effort, fashion the icon with correct attributes and install it according to the rules.

*   **Unspecified Defects After Installation:** I shall now explain the expiations not mentioned before for the period after the installation of the stationary deity but before the great consecration (*mahā-pratiṣṭhā*): offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, and *Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā* oblations ending with the *vyāhṛtis* in the five fires using cow's ghee (*kāpila-ghṛta*).
