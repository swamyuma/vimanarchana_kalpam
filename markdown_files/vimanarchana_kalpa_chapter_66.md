# Chapter 66: Expiations for Daily Worship (Nityārcanā-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules and Expiatory Rites for Omissions, Deficiencies, and Errors in Daily Rituals and Boundary Offerings

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ षट्षष्टितमः पटलः**
> **(नित्यार्चना-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिः)**
> ***atha ṣaṭṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***(nityārcanā-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)***
> 
> अथ नित्यार्चना-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिं वक्ष्ये — सूर्योदयाद् मध्याह्नाद् अस्तमयाच्च पूर्वं (मुद्रितपाठे सूर्योदया न्मध्याह्नादस्तमयाञ्च पूर्वी) कवाटोद्घाटने हीने, नित्याग्नौ चुल्ल्यां (मुद्रितपाठे चुल्यां) वा अग्निं परिस्तीर्य, आज्येन वैष्णवं, धात्रादि-दैवत्यं द्वैवारिकं (मुद्रितपाठे धात्रादिदेवत्यंद्विवारिकं) च हुत्वा शीघ्रम् उद्घाटयेत्।
> *atha nityārcanā-prāyaścitta-vidhiṃ vakṣye — sūryodayād madhyāhnād astamayācca pūrvaṃ kavāṭōdghāṭane hīne, nityāgnou cullyāṃ vā agniṃ paristīrya, ājyena vaiṣṇavaṃ, dhātrādi-daivataṃ dvaivārikaṃ ca hutvā śīghram udghāṭayet.*
> 
> सम्मार्जन-उपलेपन-निर्माल्य-शोधनेषु (मुद्रितपाठे मार्जनोप्लेपननिर्मा्ल्यशोधनेषु) हीनेषु वैष्णवं, वारुणं, वायव्यं, वैष्वक्सेनं (विष्वक्सेनं) च जुहुयात्।
> *sammārjana-upalepana-nirmālya-śodhaneṣu hīneṣu vaiṣṇavaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, vāyavyaṃ, vaiṣvakṣenaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> सर्वेषां द्रव्याणां तोयं प्रतिनिधिः, तस्मात् तोयं सङ्गृह्य अर्चनम् आरभेत।
> *sarveṣāṃ dravyāṇāṃ toyaṃ pratinidhiḥ, tasmāt toyaṃ saṅgṛhya arcanam ārabhet.*
> 
> देवे स्नानविहीने वैष्णवं, वारुणं च हुत्वा 'आपो हि ष्ठा' इति मन्त्रस्नानं कृत्वा, अभिषेकस्य अशक्तौ (मुद्रितपाठे अभिषेकस्थाऽशक्तौ) कुशोदकैः प्रोक्ष्य, वस्त्रादीन् व्यपोह्य धौतं परिधापयेत्। नित्यस्नापनम् अशक्यं चेत् (मुद्रितपाठे नित्यस्नापनमशक्यंचेत्) विष्णुपञ्चदिनेषु स्नापयेत् इति केचित्।
> *deve snānavihīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, vāruṇaṃ ca hutvā 'āpo hi ṣṭhā' iti mantrasnānaṃ kṛtā, abhiṣekasya aśaktou kuśodakaiḥ proṣya, vastrādīn vyapohya dhautaṃ paridhāpayet. nityasnāpanam aśakyaṃ cet viṣṇupañcadineṣu snāpayet iti kecit.*
> 
> ध्रुवबेरे पीठे पूर्वविन्यस्तपुष्पाणि संशोध्य पुष्पन्यासं कुर्यात्। तेनैव ध्रुवबेरस्य नित्यार्चनं भवेत्।
> *dhruvabere pīṭhe pūrvavinyastapuṣpāṇi saṃśodhya puṣpanyāsaṃ kuryāt. teanaiva dhruvaberasya nityārcanaṃ bhavet.*
> 
> पुष्पन्यासं हित्वा (मुद्रितपाठे पुष्पन्यासंहित्वा) कौतुकस्य नित्यार्चने कृते वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, विष्णुगायत्रीं च हुत्वा तदैव पुष्पन्यासं कुर्यात्।
> *puṣpanyāsaṃ hitvā kautukasya nityārcane kṛte vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, viṣṇugāyatrīṃ ca hutvā tadaiva puṣpanyāsaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> सम्बन्धकूर्चे हीने वैष्णवं, मुनिमन्त्रं च हुत्वा कूर्चं संन्यसेत् (न्यसेत्)।
> *sambandhakūrce hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, munimantraṃ ca hutvā kūrcaṃ saṃnyaset.*
> 
> धात्रादि-परिषदाम् अर्चने हीने तत्-तद्-देवत्यं वैष्णवं च हुत्वा पश्चात् परिषदम् अर्चयेत्।
> *dhātrādi-pariṣadām arcane hīne tat-tad-devatyaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā paścāt pariṣadam arcayet.*
> 
> आसनादि-सर्व-उपचार-विपर्यासे 'क्षमस्व' इति देवं प्रणम्य पुनरर्चयेत् ॥
> *āsanādi-sarva-upacāra-viparyāse 'kṣamasva' iti devaṃ praṇamya punararcayet.*
> 
> एतेषु एकं हीनं चेत् तत्-तद्-देवतामन्त्रं वैष्णवं च जप्त्वा पुनः तद्-उपचारादि सर्वोपचारैः अर्चयेत्।
> *eteṣu ekaṃ hīnaṃ cet tat-tad-devatāmantraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca japtvā punaḥ tad-upacārādi sarvopacāraiah arcayet.*
> 
> उक्तद्रव्यालाभे पुष्पं, तोयं, अक्षतं वा गृहीत्वा तत्-तत्-द्रव्यं ध्यात्वा अर्चयेत्।
> *uktadravyālābhe puṣpaṃ, toyaṃ, akṣataṃ vā gṛhītvā tat-tat-dravyaṃ dhyātvā arcayet.*
> 
> अर्चनाकाले अजस्रदीपः मूषिका-कृमि-कीट-पतङ्ग-शलभादि-पतनेन (मुद्रितपाठे मूषिकाक्रिमिकीटपतंग शलभादि) स्वयम् एव मरुता वा विच्छिन्नश्चेत्, द्विगुणं दीपं समुद्दीप्य सौरं, रौद्रं, आग्नेयं, वैष्णवं च हुत्वा पुनरर्चयेत्।
> *arcanākāle ajasradīpaḥ mūṣikā-kṛmi-kīṭa-pataṅga-śalabhādi-patanena svayam eva marutā vā vicchinnaścet, dviguṇaṃ dīpaṃ samuddīpya souraṃ, raudraṃ, āgneyaṃ, vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā punararcayet.*
> 
> तत्काले सर्वे दीपाः नश्यन्ति चेत् महत्तरो दोषो भवति; देवं शुद्धोदकैः संस्नाप्य (मुद्रितपाठे शुद्धो-दकैस्संस्नाप्य), कुशोदकैश्च अभिषिच्य (मुद्रितपाठे कुशोदकैश्चाभिषिच्य), पूर्ववद् प्रायश्चित्तं शान्तिं च हुत्वा देवम् अभ्यर्च्य, द्विगुणं हविः निवेद्य, आचार्याय दक्षिणां दद्यात्।
> *tatkāle sarve dīpāḥ naśyanti cet mahattaro doṣo bhavati; devaṃ śuddhoadakaiḥ saṃsnāpya, kuśodakaiśca abhiṣicya, pūrvavad prāyaścittaṃ śāntiṃ ca hutvā devam abhyarcya, dviguṇaṃ haviḥ nivedya, ācāryāya daxiṇāṃ dadyāt.*
> 
> दीपेषु बहुषु वर्तमानेषु एको नष्टश्चेत् (मुद्रितपाठे एकं(को) नष्टं(श्वे) चेत्), तद् न दोषाय भवति।
> *dīpeṣu bahuṣu vartamāneṣu eko naṣṭaścet, tad na doṣāya bhavati.*
> 
> अर्चनाकालाद् अन्यस्मिन् अजस्रदीपे विच्छिन्ने वैष्णवं, विष्णुगायत्रीम् आग्नेयं च हुत्वा द्विगुणम् उद्दीपयेत्।
> *arcanākālād anyasmin ajasradīpe vicchinne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇugāyatrīm āgneyaṃ ca hutvā dviguṇam uddīpayet.*
> 
> मन्त्राणां स्खलने तन्मूर्तिं सप्रणवां (मुद्रितपाठे तन्मूर्त्या सप्रणवया) कारयेत्।
> *mantrāṇāṃ skhalane tanmūrtiṃ sapraṇavāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> अर्चनाकाले पटे हीने प्राजापत्यं वैष्णवं च हुत्वा प्रच्छन्नपटं कृत्वा अर्चयेत्।
> *arcanākāle paṭe hīne prājāpatyaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā pracchannapaṭaṃ kṛtvā arcayet.*
> 
> **(दर्शनदोष-प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> वेददूषक-पाषण्ड-पापरोगान्वितानां प्रतिलोमादि-अन्त्यजादीनां च दर्शने वैष्णवं, ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, आग्नेयं च व्याहृत्यन्तं हुत्वा अर्चयेत्।
> ***(darśanadoṣa-prāyaścittam)***
> *vedadūṣaka-pāṣaṇḍa-pāparogānvitānāṃ pratilomādi-antyajādināṃ ca darśane vaiṣṇavaṃ, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, āgneyaṃ ca vyāhṛtyantaṃ hutvā arcayet.*
> 
> आवरणाद् बाह्ये पञ्चाशद्-दण्ड-अभ्यन्तरे (मुद्रितपाठे आवरणाद्बाह्योपंचाशद्दण्डाम्यन्तरे) मनुष्याणां मृतिर्भवति चेत्, शवोद्धरणात् पूर्वं देवेशस्य अर्चनं हविर्निवेदनं च न कारयेत्। ध्रुव-कौतुकयोः पुष्पन्यासम् एव कारयेत्। पश्चात् कालातीते शान्तिं हुत्वा द्विगुणम् अर्चयेत्।
> *āvaraṇād bāhye pañcāśad-daṇḍa-abhyantare manuṣyāṇāṃ mṛtirbhavati cet, śavoddharaṇāt pūrvaṃ deveśasya arcanaṃ havirnivedanaṃ ca na kārayet. dhruva-kautukayoḥ puṣpanyāsam eva kārayet. paścāt kālātīte śāntiṃ hutvā dviguṇam arcayet.*
> 
> एककालम् अर्चने हीने द्वितीये द्विगुणं, तृतीये त्रिगुणं कुर्यात्।
> *ekakālam arcane hīne dvitīye dviguṇaṃ, tṛtīye triguṇaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> एकाहम् अर्चने हीने वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा; द्व्यहे द्विगुणं, त्र्यहे त्रिगुणं, एवं द्वादशाहान्तं वर्धयेत्।
> *ekāham arcane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ ca hutvā; dvyahe dviguṇaṃ, tryahe triguṇaṃ, evaṃ dvādaśāhantaṃ vardhayet.*
> 
> द्वादशाहे अतीते चेत्, औपासनाग्निं निधाय (मुद्रितपाठे औपासनाग्निमाधाय), वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, तद्-आलय-गत-परिषद्-देवता-मन्त्रं च हुत्वा, वित्तानुसारेण देवं कलैशः संस्नाप्य अभ्यर्च्य हविः निवेदयेत् (मुद्रितपाठे हविर्निदियेत्)।
> *dvādaśāhe atīte cet, aupāsanāgniṃ nidhāya, vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, tad-ālaya-gat-pariṣad-devatā-mantraṃ ca hutvā, vittānusāreṇa devaṃ kalaiśaḥ saṃsnāpya abhyarcya haviḥ nivedayet.*
> 
> मासहीनं चेत्, आलयाभिमुखे सभ्याग्निम् आदाय वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, सर्वदैवत्यं च हुत्वा देवम् अष्टचत्वारिंशत्कलशैः संस्नाप्य अभ्यर्च्य हविः निवेदयेत्। एवं मासे प्रकुर्वीत।
> *māsahīnaṃ cet, ālayābhimuxe sabhyāgnim ādāya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, sarvadaivataṃ ca hutvā devam aṣṭacatvāriṃśatkalaiśaḥ saṃsnāpya abhyarcya haviḥ nivedayet. evaṃ māse prakurvīta.*
> 
> द्वितीये द्विगुणं, तृतीये त्रिगुणं, एवं संवत्सरान्तं वर्धयेत्।
> *dvitīye dviguṇaṃ, tṛtīye triguṇaṃ, evaṃ saṃvatsarāntaṃ vardhayet.*
> 
> संवत्सरे अतीते पञ्चाग्नौ सप्ताहं महाशान्तिं हुत्वा, वैष्णवान् सम्पूज्य, ब्राह्मणान् भोजयित्वा, देवं शताष्टकलैशः संस्नाप्य पुनःप्रतिष्ठां कारयेत्।
> *saṃvatsare atīte pañcāgnou saptāhaṃ mahāśāntiṃ hutvā, vaiṣṇavān sampūjya, brāhmaṇān bhojayitvā, devam śatāṣṭakalaiśaḥ saṃsnāpya punaḥpratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> पुनःप्रतिष्ठायाम् अक्ष्युन्मेष-अधिवासौ हित्वा, अन्यत् सर्वं पूर्ववद् आचरेत्।
> *punaḥpratiṣṭhāyām axyuṇmeṣa-adhivāsou hitvā, anyat sarvaṃ pūrvavad ācaret.*
> 
> **(हविर्दोष-प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> हविषि हीने तद्-ग्रामवासिनः सर्वे जनाः क्षुत्पिपासादिभिः पीडिताः भवेयुः। तस्मात् प्रयत्नतः देवस्य हविः सम्यक् निवेदयेत्।
> ***(havirdoṣa-prāyaścittam)***
> *haviṣi hīne tad-grāmavāsinaḥ sarve janāḥ kṣutpipāsādibhiḥ pīḍitāḥ bhaveyuh. tasmāt prayatnataḥ devasya haviḥ samyak nivedayet.*
> 
> एककाले हविषि हीने द्वितीये द्विगुणं, तृतीये त्रिगुणं निवेदयेत्।
> *ekakāle haviṣi hīne dvitīye dviguṇaṃ, tṛtīye triguṇaṃ nivedayet.*
> 
> एकाहे अतीते वैष्णवं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, मूर्तिहोमं च हुत्वा देवं शुद्धोदकैः संस्नाप्य महाहविः प्रभूतं निवेदयेत्।
> *ekāhe atīte vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, mūrtihomaṃ ca hutvā devaṃ śuddhoadakaiḥ saṃsnāpya mahāhavih prabhūtaṃ nivedayet.*
> 
> सोपदंशं हविः अपक्वं (मुद्रितपाठे रपक््वं), शीतं, पर्युषितं, स्थालीशेषं, पात्रान्तरगतं, विवृतं, स्रावितं, लङ्घितं, विकृतं, अन्त्यजादि-दृष्टं च देवेशाय निवेदयेत् चेत्, वैष्णवं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, आग्नेयं, वारुणं, वायव्यं च दशशो हुत्वा पुनश्च हविः निवेदयेत्।
> *sopadaṃśaṃ haviḥ apakvaṃ, śītaṃ, paryuṣitaṃ, sthālīśeṣaṃ, pātrāntaragataṃ, vivṛtaṃ, srāvitaṃ, laṅghitaṃ, vikṛtaṃ, antyajādi-dṛṣṭaṃ ca deveśāya nivedayet cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, āgneyaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, vāyavyaṃ ca daśaśo hutvā punaḥ ca haviḥ nivedayet.*
> 
> अशुचिना स्पृष्टं हविः निवेदयेत् चेत्, देवं संस्नाप्य वैष्णवं, विष्णुगायत्रीं च त्रयस्त्रिंशत्कृत्वो हुत्वा, शान्तिहोमविधिना शान्तिं हुत्वा, देवं प्रोक्षणैः प्रोक्ष्य, पुण्याहं वाचयित्वा, द्विगुणम् अभ्यर्च्य हविः निवेदयेत्।
> *aśucinā spṛṣṭa haviḥ nivedayet cet, devaṃ saṃsnāpya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇugāyatrīṃ ca trayastriṃśatkṛtvo hutvā, śāntihomavidhinā śāntiṃ hutvā, devaṃ proṣaṇaiah proṣya, puṇyāhaṃ vācayitvā, dviguṇam abhyarcya haviḥ nivedayet.*
> 
> शर्करा-अङ्गार-व्रीहि-तुषादि-युते हविषि निवेदिते अष्टाक्षरेण, विष्णुगायत्र्या, वैष्णवैश्च संस्तूय 'क्षमस्व' इति प्रणम्य याचेत्।
> *śarkarā-aṅgāra-vrīhi-tuṣādi-yute haviṣi nivedite aṣṭākṣareṇa, viṣṇugāyatryā, vaiṣṇavaiah ca saṃstūya 'kṣamasva' iti praṇamya yacet.*
> 
> मक्षिका-पतङ्ग-मशक-पिपीलिका-कृमि-कीट-केशाद्यैः संयुक्तं (मुद्रितपाठे केशायैस्संयुक्तं) हविषि निवेदिते, देवं शुद्धोदकैः संस्नाप्य वैष्णवं, विष्णुगायत्रीं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च अष्टोत्तरशतं हुत्वा द्विगुणं निवेदयेत्। महाहविषि एतैः संयुक्ते सति, तत्र पुरुषाशनमात्रं व्यपोह्य, भस्म-जलैः कुशेन 'आपो हि ष्ठा', 'हिरण्यवर्णा', 'पवमान' इति मन्त्रैः प्रोक्ष्य तद् निवेदयेत्।
> *maxikā-pataṅga-maśaka-pipīlikā-kṛmi-kīṭa-keśādyaiah saṃyuktaṃ haviṣi nivedite, devaṃ śuddhoadakaiḥ saṃsnāpya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇugāyatrīṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ ca aṣṭottaraśataṃ hutvā dviguṇaṃ nivedayet. mahāhavṣi etaiah saṃyukte sati, tatra puruṣāśanamātraṃ vyapohya, bhasma-jalaiah kuśena 'āpo hi ṣṭhā', 'hiraṇyavarṇā', 'pavamāna' iti mantraiah proṣya tad nivedayet.*
> 
> प्रायश्चित्तार्चनार्थम् अर्कोदयात् पूर्वं पक्वं हविः उपदंशं च सोष्णं चेत्, तस्मिन् काले निवेदयेत्। अस्तमयात् पूर्वं पक्वं हविः उपदंशं च सोष्णम् अस्तमयात् पश्चात् देवस्य सायमर्चनं कृत्वा तद् विधिवद् निवेदयेत्।
> *prāyaścittārcanārtham arkodayāt pūrvaṃ pakvaṃ haviḥ upadaṃśaṃ ca soṣṇaṃ cet, tasmin kāle nivedayet. astamayāt pūrvaṃ pakvaṃ haviḥ upadaṃśaṃ ca soṣṇam astamayāt paścāt devasya sāyamarcanaṃ kṛtā tad vidhivad nivedayet.*
> 
> ताम्बूलम् असंस्कृतं लेप-केश-अन्वितं जन्तुस्पृष्टं निवेदयेत् चेत्, वैष्णवं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा पुनः ताम्बूलं निवेदयेत्।
> *tāmbūlam asaṃskṛtaṃ lepa-keśa-anvitaṃ jantuspṛṣṭaṃ nivedayet cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ ca hutvā punaḥ tāmbūlaṃ nivedayet.*
> 
> अन्येषु अर्चनाङ्ग-द्रव्येषु दुष्टेषु हीनेष्वपि तत्-तत्-अधिदैवतमन्त्रं वैष्णवं च हुत्वा तत्-तत्-द्रव्यैः अर्चयेत्।
> *anyeṣu arcanāṅga-drayeṣu duṣṭeṣu hīneṣvapya tat-tat-adhidaivatamantraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā tat-tat-drayaih arcayet.*
> 
> अथवा सर्वेषां द्रव्याणाम् अलाभे पुष्पं जलं वा प्रतिनिधिं सङ्कल्प्य तद् द्रव्यं स्मृत्वा अर्चयेत्।
> *athavā sarveṣāṃ drayāṇām alābhe puṣpaṃ jalaṃ vā pratinidhiṃ saṅkalpya tad drayaṃ smṛtā arcayet.*
> 
> नित्यहोमे हीने वैष्णवं दशशो हुत्वा यथोक्तहोमं द्विगुणं जुहुयात्।
> *nityahome hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ daśaśo hutvā yathōktahomaṃ dviguṇaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> अग्निसंरक्षणे अशक्तश्चेत्, कुण्डाद् 'अयं ते योनिः' इति समिधम् आरोपयेत्। अथवा स्वात्मन्य् आरोप्य, लौकिकाग्नौ आरोप्य, एवम् अहरहः जुहुयाद् इति विज्ञायते।
> *agnisaṃraxṇe aśaktaścet, kuṇḍād 'ayaṃ te yoniḥ' iti samidham āropayet. athavā svātmany āropya, loukikāgnou āropya, evam aharahaḥ juhuyād iti vijñāyate.*
> 
> **(बलिविधि-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिः)**
> अथ बलिविधि-प्रायश्चित्तं वक्ष्ये — सुर-ऋषि-मनुष्याणां बलम् अस्मात् प्रवर्धते, तस्माद् बलिरिति व्याख्यातः; तदर्थं देवेशं दिने दिने त्रिषु कालेषु उक्तेषु द्रव्येषु आवाह्य अभ्यर्च्य बलिभ्रमणं कारयेत्।
> ***(balividhi-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)***
> *atha balividhi-prāyaścittaṃ vakṣye — sura-ṛṣi-manuṣyāṇāṃ balam asmāt pravardhate, tasmād baliriti vyākhyātaḥ; tadarthaṃ deveśaṃ dine dine triṣu kāleṣu ukteṣu drayeṣu āvāhya abhyarcya balibhramaṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> एवं कर्तुम् अशक्तश्चेद् उक्ते पात्रे केवलं बलिद्रव्यं निक्षिप्य, तस्य उपरि यथोक्तं बलिबेरं न्यस्य (मुद्रितपाठे बलिबेरंसन्यस्य), शिरसा धारयन् त्रिकालं द्विकालं एककालं वा आलयं प्रदक्षिणं कारयेत्।
> *evaṃ kartum aśaktaśced ukte pātre kevalaṃ balidrayaṃ nixipya, tasya upari yathōktaṃ baliberaṃ nyasya, śirasā dhārayan trikālaṃ dvikālaṃ ekakālaṃ vā alayaṃ pradaxiṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> अथवा बलिद्रव्यं विना शिविकादि-याने (मुद्रितपाठे विनाशिबिकादियाने) गजे वा आरोप्य आलयं प्रदक्षिणं कारयेत्।
> *athavā balidrayaṃ vinā śivikādi-yāne gaje vā āropya alayaṃ pradaxiṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> **(बलिकरण-लोप-प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> प्रातःकाले बलिभ्रमणे हीने वैष्णवं, सौरं, सौम्यं, प्राजापत्यं च जुहुयात्।
> ***(balikaraṇa-lopa-prāyaścittam)***
> *prātahkāle balibhramoṇe hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, souraṃ, soumyaṃ, prājāpatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> प्रदक्षिणे हीने अधिके च वैष्णवं, गारुडं च हुत्वा बलिभ्रमणं कारयेत्।
> *pradaxiṇe hīne adhike ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, gāruḍaṃ ca hutvā balibhramaṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> प्रातर्बलौ हीने मध्याह्ने द्विगुणं, मध्याह्ने च हीने सायं त्रिगुणं कुर्यात्।
> *prātarbalou hīne madhyāhne dviguṇaṃ, madhyāhne ca hīne sāyaṃ triguṇaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> एकाहे हीने नित्याग्नौ चुल्ल्यां वा अग्निं परिस्तीर्य वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, दिग्दैवतं च हुत्वा विधिना प्रदक्षिणं कारयेत्।
> *ekāhe hīne nityāgnou cullyāṃ vā agniṃ paristīrya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, digdaivataṃ ca hutvā vidhinā pradaxiṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> द्व्यहे द्विगुणं, त्र्यहे त्रिगुणम्, एवं द्वादशाहान्तं वर्धयेत्।
> *dvyahe dviguṇaṃ, tryahe triguṇaṃ, evaṃ dvādaśāhantaṃ vardhayet.*
> 
> द्वादशाहे अतीते अग्निम् औपासनम् आदाय वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, ब्राह्मं, Raudraṃ (रौद्रं), दिग्दैवतं च हुत्वा पूर्ववद् बलिभ्रमणं कारयेत्।
> *dvādaśāhe atīte agnim oupāsanam ādāya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, digdaivataṃ ca hutvā pūrvavad balibhramaṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> मासे अतीते पौण्डरीकाग्निम् आदाय वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, पारमात्मिकं, ईङ्कारादीन् तद्-आलय-गत-परिषद्-दैवत्यं च हुत्वा तथैव बलिभ्रमणं कारयेत्।
> *māse atīte pouṇḍarīkāgnim ādāya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, pāramātmikaṃ, īṅkārādīn tad-ālaya-gat-pariṣad-daivataṃ ca hutvā tathaiva balibhramaṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> द्विमासे द्विगुणं, त्रिमासे त्रिगुणम्, एवं संवत्सरान्तं वर्धयेत्। संवत्सरे अतीते देवाभिमुखे दक्षिणे वा सभ्याग्निं संसाध्य वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, सर्वदैवत्यं च जुहुयात्; आचार्याय दक्षिणां दत्त्वा बल्युद्धरणं कारयेत्।
> *dvimāse dviguṇaṃ, trimāse triguṇaṃ, evaṃ saṃvatsarāntaṃ vardhayet. saṃvatsare atīte devābhimuxe daxiṇe vā sabhyāgniṃ saṃsādhya vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, sarvadaivataṃ ca juhuyāt; ācāryāya daxiṇāṃ dattva balyuddharaṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> अन्नबलौ भूमौ पतिते, भिन्ने, जीर्णे च पूर्ववद् बलिम् आपाद्य वैष्णवं, गारुडं, प्राजापत्यं च हुत्वा प्रदक्षिणं कारयेत्।
> *annabalou bhūmou patite, bhinne, jīrṇe ca pūrvavad balim āpādya vaiṣṇavaṃ, gāruḍaṃ, prājāpatyaṃ ca hutvā pradaxiṇaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> बल्युद्धरणकाले येनकेनचित् विघ्ने सति सौरं, सौम्यं, वैष्णवं च हुत्वा पुनरारभेत। पात्रालाभे हविःपात्रम् आदाय वैष्णवं जुहुयात्।
> *balyuddharaṇakāle yeanakeanacit vighne sati souraṃ, soumyaṃ, vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā punarārabhet. pātrālābhe haviḥpātram ādāya vaiṣṇavaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> प्रमाणहीने अन्नबलौ वैष्णवं प्राजापत्यं च जुहुयात्।
> *pramāṇahīne annabalou vaiṣṇavaṃ prājāpatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> देवस्य अलङ्कारे हीने श्रीदैवत्यं; छत्र-पिञ्छादीनाम् अलाभे वारुणं; चामरादीनाम् अलाभे वायव्यं; दीपालाभे च आग्नेयं वैष्णवं च प्रत्येकं व्याहृत्यन्तं जुहुयात्।
> *devasya alaṅkāre hīne śrīdaivatyāṃ; chatra-piñchādīnām alābhe vāruṇaṃ; cāmarādīnām alābhe vāyavyaṃ; dīpālābhe ca āgneyaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca pratyekaṃ vyāhṛtyantaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> दोषदुष्ट-हविर्निवेदन-प्रायश्चित्तं कारयेत् ॥
> *doṣaduṣṭa-havirnivedana-prāyaścittaṃ kārayet.*

#### English Translation
*   **Opening the Sanctuary Doors:** If there is a delay in opening the temple doors before sunrise, noon, and sunset, kindle the daily fire (*nityāgni*) or the kitchen hearth (*cullyā*), offer ghee oblations to Viṣṇu, and the gatekeeper-deities like Dhātṛ twice, and then open the doors quickly.

*   **Cleaning the Premises:** If sweeping, plastering, or the removal of old flowers (*nirmālya*) are omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Vāruṇa*, *Vāyavya*, and *Vaiṣvaksena* (to Visvaksena) oblations.

*   **Water as a Universal Substitute:** Water is the representative of all materials; therefore, in the absence of other materials, collect water and begin the worship.

*   **Omission of Deity Bathing:** If the daily bath (*snāna*) of the deity is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Vāruṇa* oblations, perform the mental bath using the "Āpo hi ṣṭhā" hymns; if physical bathing is impossible, sprinkle with water containing kuśa grass (*kuśodaka*), remove the old garments, and put on washed clothes. Some sages say that if daily bathing is impossible, perform it on the five auspicious days of Viṣṇu (*Viṣṇu-pañca-dina*).

*   **Worship on the Pedestal:** After clearing the flowers previously offered on the pedestal of the stationary deity (*dhruva-bera*), perform the flower-deposits (*puṣpa-nyāsa*). By doing this alone, the daily worship of the stationary deity is fulfilled.

*   **Omission of Flower Deposits:** If the flower-deposit is omitted during the daily worship of the processional deity (*kautuka-bera*), offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Viṣṇu-gāyatrī* oblations, and perform the flower-deposits immediately.

*   **Omission of Connecting Grass Brush:** If the connecting grass brush (*sambandha-kūrca*) is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Muni-mantra* oblations, and deposit the brush.

*   **Omission of Retinue Worship:** If the worship of Dhātṛ and other retinue deities (*parivāra-devatā*) is omitted, offer oblations to those respective deities and the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation, and then worship the retinue.

*   **Reversal of Offerings:** If there is a reversal in the order of seat-offerings (*āsana*) and other services (*upacāra*), bow to the Lord saying "Forgive me", and perform the worship again. If one of these is omitted, chant the mantra of that respective deity and the *Vaiṣṇava* mantra, and then worship again with all services.

*   **Missing Worship Offerings:** In the absence of the specified worship materials, take flowers, water, or unbroken rice (*akṣata*), meditate on those materials, and perform the worship.

*   **Extinguishment of Main Lamp:** During the worship, if the continuous lamp (*ajasra-dīpa*) goes out on its own or due to wind, or if mice, worms, insects, moths, or grasshoppers fall into it, light two lamps, offer the *Saura*, *Raudra*, *Āgneya*, and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations, and resume the worship.

*   **Extinguishment of All Lamps:** If all lamps in the temple go out at the same time, a severe fault arises; bathe the deity with pure water and sprinkle with *kuśodaka*, perform expiatory rites and a peace ceremony (*śānti*), worship the deity, offer double food offerings, and give donations to the priest.

*   **Partial Extinguishment:** If there are many lamps and only one is extinguished, it does not constitute a fault. If the continuous lamp goes out at a time other than the worship hour, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-gāyatrī*, and *Āgneya* oblations, and light two lamps.

*   **Mistakes in Mantra Chanting:** If there is a slip or mistake in the pronunciation of mantras, perform the worship of the respective deity form reciting the Praṇava (*oṃ*).

*   **Missing Altar Screen:** If the screen/curtain (*paṭa*) is missing during the worship, offer the *Prājāpatya* and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations, put up a concealing screen, and then perform the worship.

*   **Impurities from Seeing Forbidden People:** If one sees a defiler of the Vedas, a heretic (*pāṣaṇḍa*), a person with sinful disease, or low-caste/outcaste persons, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Brāhma*, *Raudra*, and *Āgneya* oblations ending with the *vyāhṛtis*, and then worship the deity.

*   **Corpses Outside the Enclosure:** If a human death occurs outside the enclosure but within fifty *daṇḍas* (bow-lengths), do not perform the worship or offer food to the Lord until the corpse is removed. Perform only the flower-deposit (*puṣpa-nyāsa*) for the stationary (*dhruva*) and processional (*kautuka*) deities. After the corpse is removed and the time of pollution passes, perform a peace ceremony (*śānti*) and worship with double offerings.

*   **Omission of Daily Worship:** If the daily worship is omitted for one session (*eka-kāla*), double the offerings in the next session; if for two sessions, double; if for three, triple. If worship is omitted for an entire day, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata* oblations; if for two days, double; if for three, triple; thus increase up to twelve days. If twelve days pass without worship, kindle the domestic fire (*aupāsanāgni*), offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, and the mantras of the retinue deities, bathe the deity with pots of water according to one's wealth, worship Him, and offer food.

*   **Omission of Worship for a Month:** If worship is omitted for a month, kindle the *Sabhya* fire in front of the temple, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *Sarvadaivata* oblations, bathe the deity with forty-eight pots of water, worship Him, and offer food. Do this for a month's delay.

*   **Omission of Worship for More Than a Month:** If two months pass, double the offerings; if three months, triple; thus increase up to one year. If a year passes, kindle the five fires, perform a great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*) for a week, worship the Vaiṣṇavas, feed the Brahmins, bathe the deity with one hundred and eight pots of water, and perform the re-consecration (*punaḥ-pratiṣṭhā*). In this re-consecration, omit the eye-opening and reclining rites, and perform all other steps as before.

*   **Importance of Food Offerings:** If the food offering (*havis*) is omitted, all villagers suffer from hunger, thirst, and diseases; therefore, with great effort, offer the food properly to the Lord.

*   **Omission of Food Offerings:** If the food offering is omitted for one session, double it in the next; if for two, double; if for three, triple. If a whole day passes without food offerings, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *mūrti-homa* (form-oblation) oblations, bathe the deity with pure water, and offer a large quantity of food (*mahā-havis*).

*   **Defective Food Offerings:** If uncooked, cold, stale, leftover, or transferred food, or food that has been exposed, spilt, jumped over, disfigured, or seen by outcastes is offered to the Lord of gods, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-devata*, *Āgneya*, *Vāruṇa*, and *Vāyavya* oblations ten times each, and then offer fresh food.

*   **Defiled Food Offerings:** If food touched by an impure person is offered, bathe the deity, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Viṣṇu-gāyatrī* oblations thirty-three times, perform the peace ceremony (*śānti*), sprinkle the deity, perform the *puṇyāha* purification, worship with double offerings, and then offer fresh food.

*   **Impurities in Food Offerings:** If food containing gravel, embers, unhusked grain, or chaff is offered, chant the eight-syllable mantra (*aṣṭākṣara*), the *Viṣṇu-gāyatrī*, and the *Vaiṣṇava* hymns, bow to the Lord saying "Forgive me", and pray for forgiveness. If food containing flies, moths, mosquitoes, ants, worms, insects, or hair is offered, bathe the deity with pure water, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-gāyatrī*, and *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata* oblations one hundred and eight times, and offer double food. If such impurities are found in a large quantity of food, remove a portion equivalent to a man's meal (*puruṣāśana-mātra*), sprinkle the rest with ash-water, darbha grass, and the "Āpo hi ṣṭhā", "Hiranyavarṇa", and "Pavamāna" hymns, and then offer it.

*   **Expiations for Worship Timing:** For the expiation of worship timing, if the cooked food and side dishes are still hot before sunrise, offer them at that time. If they are ready before sunset, perform the evening worship after sunset, and then offer them.

*   **Defiled Betel Leaves:** If betel leaves (*tāmbūla*) that are unprepared, coated, containing hair, or touched by insects are offered, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata* oblations, and offer fresh betel leaves.

*   **Defects in Other Worship Materials:** If other worship materials are defective or missing, offer oblations to their respective presiding deities and the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation, and then perform the worship with those materials. In the absence of all materials, use flowers or water as representatives, meditate on the materials, and perform the worship.

*   **Omission of Daily Fire Sacrifice:** If the daily fire sacrifice (*nitya-homa*) is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation ten times, and then offer double the specified oblations.

*   **Inability to Maintain the Fire:** If one is unable to maintain the sacred fire, transfer it to a fuel-stick (*samidh*) from the fire-pit reciting "Ayaṃ te yoniḥ". Alternatively, transfer it to oneself, or to a common fire; thus, perform the daily offerings every day—so it is understood.

*   **Expiations for Boundary Offerings:** I shall now explain the expiations for the boundary offerings (*bali-prāyaścitta*): the strength of gods, sages, and humans increases from this offering; therefore, it is called *bali*. For this purpose, invoke the Lord of gods daily during the three sessions in the specified materials, worship Him, and perform the boundary circumambulation (*bali-bhramaṇa*).

*   **Inability to Carry the Deity:** If one is unable to carry the processional icon, place the boundary offering in the designated vessel, place the boundary icon (*bali-bera*) on top of it, carry it on the head, and circumambulate the temple during the three, two, or one session. Alternatively, carry the boundary offering in a palanquin, vehicle, or on an elephant, and circumambulate the temple.

*   **Omissions in Boundary Offerings:** If the morning boundary circumambulation is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Saura*, *Saumya*, and *Prājāpatya* oblations. If there is a deficiency or excess in circumambulation, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Gāruḍa* oblations, and perform the circumambulation.

*   **Double and Triple Offerings for Delay:** If the morning boundary offering is omitted, make it double at noon; if the noon offering is omitted, make it triple in the evening.

*   **Omission for One Day:** If the boundary offering is omitted for one day, kindle the daily fire or the kitchen hearth, circumambulate the fire, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Digdevatā* oblations, and perform the circumambulation according to the rules. If two days pass, double the offerings; if three days, triple; thus increase up to twelve days.

*   **Omission for Twelve Days:** If twelve days pass without boundary offerings, kindle the domestic fire, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, *Brāhma*, *Raudra*, and *Digdevatā* oblations, and perform the circumambulation as before.

*   **Omission for a Month:** If a month passes, kindle the *Pauṇḍarīka* fire, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Pāramātmika*, the *īṅkāra-ādi* seed-syllables, and the mantras of the retinue deities, and perform the circumambulation. If two months pass, double; if three, triple; up to one year. If a year passes, kindle the *Sabhya* fire in front of or to the south of the deity, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *Sarvadaivata* oblations, give donations to the priest, and perform the removal (*balyuddharaṇa*).

*   **Defiled Boundary Offerings:** If the food offering (*anna-bali*) falls on the ground, breaks, or is worn out, prepare a new offering as before, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Gāruḍa*, and *Prājāpatya* oblations, and perform the circumambulation.

*   **Interruptions in Offering:** If any obstacle arises during the boundary offering, offer the *Saura*, *Saumya*, and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations, and resume the ceremony. If the vessel is unavailable, take the food-plate, and offer the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation. If the food offering is deficient in quantity, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Prājāpatya* oblations.

*   **Missing Decorations and Parasols:** If the deity's ornaments are missing, offer the *Śrī-daivata* oblation; if the umbrella (*chatra*) or feather-bundle (*piñcha*) are missing, offer the *Vāruṇa* oblation; if the fly-whisks (*cāmara*) are missing, offer the *Vāyavya* oblation; if the lamp is missing, offer the *Āgneya* and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations, each ending with the *vyāhṛtis*. Perform expiations for defective food offerings.
