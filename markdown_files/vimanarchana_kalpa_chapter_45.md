# Chapter 45: Worship of a Single Icon and the Ninefold Worship (Eka-berārcana-vidhiḥ / Nava-vidhārcana-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules for Single-Icon and Nine-Class Worship Services

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ पञ्चचत्वारिंशः पटलः**
> **एकबेरार्चनाविधिः -**
> ***atha pañcacatvāriṃśaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***ekaberārcanavidhiḥ -***
> 
> अथ एकबेरार्चनविधिं वक्ष्ये — प्रभाते स्नात्वा आलयप्रदक्षिणं प्रणामं च कृत्वा, 'निरस्तम्' इति कवाटम् उद्घाट्य, दीपान् उद्दीप्य; शिष्येण 'धारासु' इति उदकं गृहीत्वा, देवस्य निर्माल्यम् आदाय, विष्वक्सेनं समाराध्य; गर्भालय-आदिषु सर्वत्र सम्मार्जनं कृत्वा, अद्भिः अभ्युक्ष्य, उपलेपनं कृत्वा; अर्चनापात्राणि हविःपात्राणि च प्रक्षाल्य, हवींषि पाचयित्वा, पुष्प-गन्ध-आदि-अर्चनद्रव्याणि सङ्गृह्य;
> *atha ekaberārcanavidhiṃ vakṣye — prabhāte snātvā ālayapradakṣiṇaṃ praṇāmaṃ ca kṛtvā, 'nirastam' iti kavāṭam udghāṭya, dīpān uddīpya; śiṣyeṇa 'dhārāsu' iti udakaṃ gṛhītvā, devasya nirmālyam ādāya, viṣvaksenaṃ samārādhya; garbhālaya-ādiṣu sarvatra sammārjanaṃ kṛtvā, adbhiḥ abhyuxya, upalepanaṃ kṛtvā; arcanāpātrāṇi haviṣpātrāṇi ca praxālya, havīṃṣi pācayitvā, puṣpa-gandha-ādi-arcanadravyāṇi saṅgṛhya;*
> 
> 'प्र तद्विष्णुः' इति देवस्य पुष्पासनं दत्त्वा, 'विश्वाधिकानाम्' इति स्वागतं, 'मनाऽभिमन्ता' इति अनुमानं, 'त्रीणि पदा' इति पाद्यं, 'शं नो देवीः' इति आचमनं दत्त्वा; 'अतो देवाः' इत्यादिना तैलाभ्यञ्जनं कृत्वा, 'परिलिखितम्' इति आम्लादिना संशोध्य, 'वारीश्चतसृभिः' (मुद्रितपाठे वारीश्चतस्र) इति वारिणा संस्नापयेत्।
> *'pra tadvaiṣṇuḥ' iti devasya puṣpāsanaṃ dattva, 'viśvādhikānām' iti svāgataṃ, 'manobhimantā' iti anumānaṃ, 'trīṇi padā' iti pādyaṃ, 'śaṃ no devīḥ' iti ācamanaṃ dattva; 'ato devāḥ' ityādinā tailābhyañjanaṃ kṛtvā, 'parilikhitam' iti āmlādinā saṃśodhya, 'vārīścataṣṛbhiḥ' iti vāriṇā saṃsnāpayet.*
> 
> 'इषे त्वोर्जे त्वा', 'यज्ञस्य', 'शुन्धध्वम्' (मुद्रितपाठे शुदध्वं) इति त्रिभिः त्रिभिः अभिषेचयेत्। सहस्रधारा यदि स्यात् — वस्त्र-माल्यादिना गन्धाद्यैः अलङ्कृत्य, पाद्यादि-अर्घ्यान्तम् अभ्यर्च्य, सहस्रधारया अभिषेचयेत्; 'मित्रः सुपर्णः' इति प्लोतेन विमृज्य, 'विष्णोर्नुकम्' इति वस्त्राद्यैः अलङ्कृत्य, तथैव आचमनं दत्त्वा, देवस्य पादयोः मध्ये 'विष्णवे नमः' इति पुष्पं दद्यात्।
> *'iṣe tvorje tvā', 'yajñasya', 'śundhadvam' iti tribhiḥ tribhiḥ abhiṣecayet. sahasradhārā yadi syāt — vastra-mālyādinā gandhādyaiḥ alaṅkṛtya, pādyādi-arghyāntam abhyarcya, sahasradhārayā abhiṣecayet; 'mitraḥ suparṇaḥ' iti plotena vimṛjya, 'viṣṇoḥnukam' iti vastrādyaiḥ alaṅkṛtya, tathāiva ācamanaṃ dattva, devasya padayoḥ madhye 'viṣṇave namaḥ' iti puṣpaṃ dadyāt.*
> 
> प्रागादि-चतुर्दिक्षु पुरुषादि-आवरणत्रय-देवानां तत्-तत्-नाम्ना प्रणवादि-नमोऽन्तेन पुष्पन्यासं करोति। गर्भगतान् (मुद्रितपाठे गर्भालगत्यान्) देवान् धात्रादीन्, द्वारदेवान् द्वारपालान्, अन्यपरिषदश्च तत्-तत्-नाममन्त्रेण अभ्यर्च्य; अभ्यन्तरं प्रविश्य, देवं प्रणम्य, 'तद्विष्णोः' इति पुष्पं, 'तद्विप्रासः' इति गन्धं, 'परोमात्रया' इति धूपं, 'विष्णोः कर्माणि' इति दीपं, 'त्रिरिन्द्रः' इत्यर्घ्यं, तथा आचमनं दत्त्वा; 'तदस्य प्रियं' इति हविर्निवेद्य, द्वारपालादिभ्यो बलिं निर्वाप्य, होमं हुत्वा, पूर्ववत् पानीय-आचमन-मुखवासान् दत्त्वा; विधिना बलिम् आराध्य, बलिबेरेण सहैव आलयं प्रदक्षिणं कृत्वा, अष्टाक्षरमन्त्रेण पुष्पाञ्जलिं दद्यात्। आवाहन-विसर्गाभ्यां विना अन्यत् सर्वं समानम् इति विज्ञायते।
> *prāgādi-caturdikṣu puruṣādi-āvaraṇatraya-devānāṃ tat-tat-nāmnā praṇavādi-namo'ntena puṣpanyāsaṃ karoti. garbhagatān devān dhātrādīn, dvāradevān dvārapālān, anyapariṣadaśca tat-tat-nāmamantreṇa abhyarcya; abhyantaraṃ praviśya, devaṃ praṇamya, 'tadvaiṣṇoḥ' iti puṣpaṃ, 'tadviprāsaḥ' iti gandhaṃ, 'paromātrayā' iti dhūpaṃ, 'viṣṇoḥ karmāṇi' iti dīpaṃ, 'tririndraḥ' ityargyaṃ, tathā ācamanaṃ dattva; 'tadadya priyaṃ' iti havirnivedya, dvārapālādibhyo baliṃ nirvāpya, homaṃ hutvā, pūrvavat pānīya-ācamana-mukhavāsān dattva; vidhinā baliṃ ārādhya, balibereṇa sahaiva ālayaṃ pradakṣiṇaṃ kṛtvā, aṣṭākṣaramantreṇa puṣpāñjaliṃ dadyāt. āvāhana-visargābhyāṃ vinā anyat sarvaṃ samānam iti vijñāyate.*
> 
> **नवविधार्चन-विधिः (The Nine Classes of Worship) -**
> अथ नवविधार्चनाविधिं वक्ष्ये —
> ***navavidhārcana-vidhiḥ -***
> *atha navavidhārcanavidhiṃ vakṣye —*
> 
> **१. उत्तमोत्तमम् (Highest of the Highest):**
> *   **हविः-परिमाणम्:** देवस्य हविः: द्रोणं; देव्योः: तदर्धं (अर्धद्रोणं); पुरुषादीनां चतुर्णां: द्रोणार्धं; तद्देवीनां: तदर्धं; नरनारायण-नारसिंह-वराहाणां: द्रोणार्धम्; अनन्तशयनस्य: द्रोणं तदर्धं वा; उत्सवस्य: द्रोणार्धं; तद्देव्योः: तदर्धं; स्नपन-बलिबेरयोः: द्रोणं तदर्धं वा; अवताराणां: द्रोणार्धं तदर्धं वा;
> *   **परिषद्-हविः:** पूजनमुन्योः (भृगु-मार्कण्डेययोः), ब्रह्मेशानयोः, वीश-शैषिकयोश्च: आढकम्; अन्येषां परिवाराणां होमस्य च: आढकार्धम्।
> *   **त्रिकाल-हविः-पाकः:** देवेशस्य देव्योश्च — प्रातः: मौद्गिकं; मध्याह्ने: गौल्यं; रात्रौ: पायसं; अर्धयामे (अर्धरात्रे) च: आढकद्वयं हविरूपसंयुक्तं; त्रिकालम्: आढकद्वयं मन्त्रहविः; कर्पूरसहित-ताम्बूलम्।
> *   **अन्य-विधानानि:** नित्याग्निकुण्डे: अमूर्ता-आराधनं (मुद्रितपाठे अमूर्ता राधनं); त्रिकालम् अन्नबलियुक्तं; कर्पूरसहितान् षोडश-अजस्रदीपान्; त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: द्विशतं, रात्रौ: पञ्चशतं श्रद्धादीपान्; कर्पूर-अगुरुकलित-धूपं; जाती-चम्पक-उत्पल-आदि-सुगन्धिपुष्पाढ्यं (मुद्रितपाठे पुष्याढ्य); द्वात्रिंशद्विग्रहैः अर्चनं; देवगणिकाभिः देवदासीभिः भक्तैश्च कृत-नृत्तगेयसंयुक्तं; नर्तक-गायक-वांशिक-मौरज-दार्दविक-आद्यैः संयुक्तं; प्रातः प्रबोधनघोषयुतं; स्नानकाले, हविर्दाने, बलिदाने, पटोद्घाटने (पटोद्धरणे), बलिभ्रमणे च काले शङ्ख-भेरी-आदि-घोषणं, नाडिकाभेरीताडनं; सप्तप्राकारसंयुक्तं सर्वपरिषद्देवतार्चनम् उत्तमोत्तमम् ॥
> ***1. uttamottamam:***
> **   **haviḥ-parimāṇam:** devasya haviḥ: droṇaṃ; devyoḥ: tadardhaṃ; puruṣādīnāṃ caturṇāṃ: droṇārdhaṃ; taddevīnāṃ: tadardhaṃ; naranārāyaṇa-nārasiṃha-varāhāṇāṃ: droṇārdham; anantaśayanasya: droṇaṃ tadardhaṃ vā; utsavasya: droṇārdhaṃ; taddevyoḥ: tadardhaṃ; snapan-baliberayoḥ: droṇaṃ tadardhaṃ vā; avatārāṇāṃ: droṇārdhaṃ tadardhaṃ vā;*
> **   **pariṣad-haviḥ:** pūjakamunyoḥ, brahmeśānayoḥ, vīśa-śaiṣikayośca: āḍhakam; anyeṣāṃ parivārāṇāṃ homasya ca: āḍhākārdham.*
> **   **trikāl-haviḥ-pākaḥ:** deveśasya devyośca — prātaḥ: maudgikaṃ; madhyāhne: gaulyaṃ; rātrou: pāyasaṃ; ardhayāme ca: āḍhakadvayaṃ havirupasaṃyuktaṃ; trikālam: āḍhakadvayaṃ mantrahāviḥ; karpūrasahita-tāmbūlam.*
> **   **anya-vidhānāni:** nityāgnikuṇḍe: amūrtā-ārādhanaṃ; trikālam annabaliyuktaṃ; karpūrasahitān ṣoḍaśa-ajasradīpān; triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: dviśataṃ, rātrou: pañcaśataṃ śraddhādīpān; karpūra-agurukalita-dhūpaṃ; jātī-campaka-utpala-ādi-sugandhipuṣpāḍhyaṃ; dvātriṃśadvigrahaiḥ arcanaṃ; devagaṇikābhiḥ devadāsībhiḥ bhaktaiśca kṛta-nṛttageyasaṃyuktaṃ; nartaka-gāyak-vāṃśika-mauraja-dārdavika-ādyaiḥ saṃyuktaṃ; prātaḥ prabodhaghoṣayutaṃ; snānākāle, havirādanē, balidānē, paṭodghāṭanē, balibhramaṇē ca kāle śaṅkha-bherī-ādi-ghoṣaṇaṃ, nāḍikābherītāḍanaṃ; saptaprākārasaṃyuktaṃ sarvapariṣaddevatarcanam uttamottamam.*
> 
> **२. उत्तममध्यमम् (Highest of the Middle):**
> *   इन्द्रादि-लोकपालानाम् अन्येषां परिवाराणां हविर्निवेदनं; विना बलिदानं; द्विसमाजस्रदीपान्, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: शतपञ्चाशत् (१५०), रात्रौ: चतुःशतं (४००) श्रद्धादीपान्; पूजावेलायां मन्त्रहविर्निवेदनं; द्विकालहोमं; द्विकालान्नबलियुक्तं; षट्प्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनं; पूर्ववत् नृत्तगेयवाद्ययुक्तम् उत्तममध्यमम्।
> ***2. uttamamadhyamam:***
> **   indrādi-lokapālānām anyeṣāṃ parivārāṇāṃ havirnivedanaṃ; vinā balidānaṃ; dvisamajasradīpān, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: śatapañcāśat, rātrou: catuḥśataṃ śraddhādīpān; pūjāvelāyāṃ mantrahāvirnivedanaṃ; dvikālahomaṃ; dvikālānnabaliyuktaṃ; ṣaṭprākārastha-devarcanaṃ; pūrvavat nṛttageyavādayuktam uttamamadhyamam.*
> 
> **३. उत्तमाधमम् (Highest of the Lowest):**
> *   पूजकमुन्योः, वीश-शैषिकयोश्च हविः विना; द्वादश-अजस्रदीपान्, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; पूजावेलानिवेदनं; द्विकालहोमं; तथैव अन्नबलियुक्तं; पञ्चप्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनं; पूर्ववत् नृत्तगेयवाद्यैर्युक्तम् उत्तमाधमम्।
> ***3. uttamādhamam:***
> **   pūjakamunyoḥ, vīśa-śaiṣikayośca haviḥ vinā; dvādaśa-ajasradīpān, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; pūjāvelānivedanaṃ; dvikālahomaṃ; tathāiva annabaliyuktaṃ; pañcaprākārastha-devarcanaṃ; pūrvavat nṛttageyavādyairyuktam uttamādhamam.*
> 
> **४. मध्यमोत्तमम् (Middle of the Highest):**
> *   पूजकमुन्योः अवताराणां (मुद्रितपाठे मुन्योखताराणां) च द्विकालनिवेदनं; दश (द्विपञ्च) अजस्रदीपान्, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: शतं (१००), रात्रौ: त्रिशतं (३००) सन्ध्यादीपान्; देवदासीभिः भक्तैश्च कृत-नृत्तगेयवाद्यसंयुक्तं; प्रातः होमं; मध्याह्ने अन्नबलियुक्तं; पञ्चप्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनं मध्यमोत्तमम्।
> ***4. madhyamottamam:***
> **   pūjakamunyoḥ avatārāṇāṃ ca dvikālanivedanaṃ; daśa ajasradīpān, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: śataṃ, rātrou: triśataṃ sandhyādīpān; devadāsībhiḥ bhaktaiśca kṛta-nṛttageyavādyasaṃyuktaṃ; prātaḥ homaṃ; madhyāhne annabaliyuktaṃ; pañcaprākārastha-devarcanaṃ madhyamottamam.*
> 
> **५. मध्यमाध्यमम् (Middle of the Middle):**
> *   अवताराणाम् एककालनिवेदनं; स्नानोत्सवबलिबेराणाम् आढकहविर्निवेदनम्; अष्ट-अजस्रदीपान्, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: अशीतिं (८०), रात्रौ: द्विशतं (२००) श्रद्धादीपान्; चतुष्प्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनं; नृत्तगेयवाद्यैर्युक्तं मध्यममध्यमम्।
> ***5. madhyamamadhyamam:***
> **   avatārāṇām ekakālanivedanaṃ; snapanotsavabaliberāṇām āḍhakahavirnivedanam; aṣṭa-ajasradīpān, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: aśītiṃ, rātrou: dviśataṃ śraddhādīpān; catuṣprākārastha-devarcanaṃ; nṛttageyavādyairyuktaṃ madhyamamadhyamam.*
> 
> **६. मध्यमाधमम् (Middle of the Lowest):**
> *   कौतुकस्य हविः: द्रोणं तदर्धं वा; देव्योः यथालाभं मौद्गिक-गौल्य-पायस-अपूपानां निवेदनं; षडजस्रदीपान्, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: षष्टिं (६०), रात्रौ: शतपञ्चाशत् (१५०) श्रद्धादीपान्; अन्यत् सर्वं पूर्ववत् मध्यमाधमम्।
> ***6. madhyamādhamam:***
> **   koutukasya haviḥ: droṇaṃ tadardhaṃ vā; devyoḥ yathālābhaṃ maudgika-gaulya-pāyasa-apūpānāṃ nivedanaṃ; ṣadajasradīpān, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: ṣaṣṭiṃ, rātrou: śatapañcāśat śraddhādīpān; anyat sarvaṃ pūrvavat madhyamādhamam.*
> 
> **७. अधमोत्तमम् (Lowest of the Highest):**
> *   देवेशस्य: द्रोणार्धं हविः; अर्धयामे च आढकं; देवस्य अर्धं देव्योः; स्नानोत्सवबलिबेराणाम् एककालनिवेदनं; सायंप्रातः पूजावेलानिवेदनं; चतुरजस्रदीपान्, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: चत्वारिंशत् (४०), रात्रौ: शतं (१००) श्रद्धादीपान्; त्रिप्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनं; नृत्तगेयवाद्यैर्युक्तम् अधमोत्तमम्।
> ***7. adhamottamam:***
> **   deveśasya: droṇārdhaṃ haviḥ; ardhayāme ca āḍhakaṃ; devasya ardhaṃ devyoḥ; snapanotsavabaliberāṇām ekakālanivedanaṃ; sāyaṃprātaḥ pūjāvelānivedanaṃ; caturajasradīpān, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: catvārīṃśat, rātrou: śataṃ śraddhādīpān; triprākārastha-devarcanaṃ; nṛttageyavādyairyuktam adhamottamam.*
> 
> **८. अधममध्यमम् (Lowest of the Middle):**
> *   देवेशस्य: आढकं हविः; देव्योः: तदर्धं; त्रिकालं द्विकालं वा; स्नानोत्सवबलिबेराणाम् एककालनिवेदनं, एककाले पूजावेलानिवेदनं; गौल्यादीनि विना; मध्याह्ने अन्नबलिहोमौ; द्वौ अजस्रदीपौ, तत् त्रिगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्; प्रातर्मध्याह्नयोः: दश (१०), रात्रौ: विंशतिं (२०) श्रद्धादीपान्; नृत्तगेयविहीनं वाद्ययुतं; द्विप्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनम् अधममध्यमम्।
> ***8. adhamamadhyamam:***
> **   deveśasya: āḍhakaṃ haviḥ; devyoḥ: tadardhaṃ; trikālaṃ dvikālaṃ vā; snapanotsavabaliberāṇām ekakālanivedanaṃ, ekakāle pūjāvelānivedanaṃ; gaulyādīni vinā; madhyāhne annabalihomou; dvou ajasradīpou, tat triguṇān sandhyādīpān; prātarmadhyāhnayoḥ: daśa, rātrou: viṃśatiṃ śraddhādīpān; nṛttageyavihīnaṃ vādayutaṃ; dviprākārastha-devarcanam adhamamadhyamam.*
> 
> **९. अधमाधमम् (Lowest of the Lowest):**
> *   देवेशस्य: द्विकालम् एककालं वा आढकं हविः, तदर्धं मध्याह्ने देव्योः यथालाभं व्यञ्जनैर्युक्तं; एकम् अजस्रदीपं, तद्विगुणान् सन्ध्यादीपान्, यथालाभं श्रद्धादीपान्; अन्यत् सर्वं विना एकप्राकारस्थ-देवार्चनम् अधमाधमम् आहुः।
> ***9. adhamādhamam:***
> **   deveśasya: dvikālam ekakālaṃ vā āḍhakaṃ haviḥ, tadardhaṃ madhyāhne devyoḥ yathālābhaṃ vyañjanairyuktaṃ; ekam ajasradīpaṃ, tadviguṇān sandhyādīpān, yathālābhaṃ śraddhādīpān; anyat sarvaṃ vinā ekaprākārastha-devarcanam adhamādhamam āhuḥ.*
> 
> **पूजा-भार-लक्षणाः (Scale of Oblation Weights) -**
> उत्कृष्टे च उत्कृष्टं कर्तव्यं, अन्यथा महत्तरो दोषो भवति। एकस्मिन् आलये देवेशादिदेवानां एकाहस्य उक्तकाले नित्यहविरर्थं तण्डुलम् —
> *   एककालस्य **दशभारं** (यथाभारोपदंशैर्युक्तम्): *उत्तमोत्तमम्*।
> *   **अष्टभारं**: *उत्तममध्यमम्*।
> *   **समभारं** (दशप्रस्थतुल्यं वा): *उत्तमाधमम्*।
> *   **षड्भारं**: *मध्यमोत्तमम्*।
> *   **पञ्चभारं**: *मध्यममध्यमम्*।
> *   **चतुर्भारं**: *मध्यमाधमम्*।
> *   **त्रिभारं**: *अधमोत्तमम्*।
> *   **द्विभारं**: *अधममध्यमम्*।
> *   **एकभारं**: *अधमाधमम्* इति एके।
> ***pūjā-bhāra-lakṣaṇāḥ -***
> *utkṛṣṭe ca utkṛṣṭaṃ kartavyaṃ, anyathā mahattaro doṣo bhavati. ekasmin ālaye deveśādidevanāṃ ekāhasya uktakāle nityahavirarthaṃ taṇḍulam —*
> **   ekakālasya **daśabhāraṃ**: *uttamottamam*.*
> **   **aṣṭabhāraṃ**: *uttamamadhyamam*.*
> **   **samabhāraṃ**: *uttamādhamam*.*
> **   **ṣadbhāraṃ**: *madhyamottamam*.*
> **   **pañcabhāraṃ**: *madhyamamadhyamam*.*
> **   **catubhāraṃ**: *madhyamādhamam*.*
> **   **tribhāraṃ**: *adhamottamam*.*
> **   **dvibhāraṃ**: *adhamamadhyamam*.*
> **   **ekabhāraṃ**: *adhamādhamam* iti eke.*

#### English Translation
*   **Preparations for Single-Icon Worship:** Now I shall describe the rules for the worship of a single icon (*eka-bera-arcana*): having bathed in the morning, circumambulated the temple, and prostrated.

*   **Entering and Purifying the Courts:** Opening the door with the mantra "Nirastam", lighting the lamps, and the disciple taking the water with "Dhārāsu"; taking the offered flowers of the Lord, worshipping Viṣvaksena, and cleaning all places starting with the sanctum sanctorum (*garbhālaya*), sprinkling with water, plastering, washing the worship vessels and oblation vessels, cooking the oblations, and gathering the worship materials starting with flowers and sandalwood.

*   **Upacāras for the Single Icon:** Offering a flower-seat with "Pra tad viṣṇuḥ", welcome with "Viśvādhikānā", permission with "Mano'bhimantā", foot-water with "Trīṇi padā", sipping water with "Śaṃ no devīḥ", oil massage with "Ato devā", cleaning with tamarind etc. with "Parilikhitam", and bathing with water with four vessels of water (*vārīśca tasṛbhiḥ*).

*   **Bathing and Showering:** Pouring water three times each with the three mantras "Iṣe tvā", "Yajñasya", and "Śundhadvam"; if there is a thousand-holed shower vessel (*sahasra-dhārā*), having decorated the deity with garments, garlands, sandalwood, etc., and worshipped Him from *pādya* (foot-water) up to *arghya* (water offering), one should bathe Him with the thousand-holed shower.

*   **Dressing and Flower offerings:** Wiping with the cloth with the mantra "Mitraḥ suparṇaḥ", decorating with garments etc. with "Viṣṇor nukam", offering sipping water as before, and offering a flower in the center of the Lord's feet with "Viṣṇave namaḥ".

*   **Flower Placement and Retinue Worship:** In the four directions starting with the east, one should perform the flower-placement (*puṣpa-nyāsa*) for the deities of the three enclosures of Puruṣa etc. with their respective names ending with Namah and preceded by Pranava. Having worshipped the deities in the sanctum sanctorum, Dhātṛ and others, the door deities, the door-keepers, and the other retinue deities with their respective name-mantras, entered the interior, prostrated to the Lord.

*   **Final Services and Oblation:** Offering flower with "Tadvaiṣṇoḥ", sandalwood with "Tadviprāsaḥ", incense with "Paromātrayā", lamp with "Viṣṇoḥ kırmaṇi", *arghya* with "Tririndraḥ", and sipping water; offering oblation with "Tad asya priyam", distributing the *bali* (offering) to the door-keepers, performing fire-oblation (*homa*), offering drinking water, *ācamana*, and mouth-fresheners as before, circumambulating the temple along with the *bali-bera*, one should offer a handful of flowers with the eight-syllable mantra (*aṣṭākṣara*). It is known that all other details are similar, excluding invocation and dismissal (*āvāhana-visarga*).

*   **The Nine Classes of Temple Liturgy (*Nava-vidhārcana*):** Now I shall describe the rules for the ninefold worship (*nava-vidhā-arcana*):

*   **1. Uttamottama (The Highest of the Highest Class):**
    *   **Oblation Quantities:** The Lord's oblation is one *droṇa*, the two Goddesses' is half that (half *droṇa*); the four deities starting with Puruṣa's is half a *droṇa*, their Goddesses' is half that; Nara-Nārāyaṇa, Narasiṃha, and Vārāha's is half a *droṇa*; Ananta-śayana's is one *droṇa* or half that; the festival icon's is half a *droṇa*, the two Goddesses' is half that; the bathing and offering icons' is one *droṇa* or half that; the incarnations' is half a *droṇa* or half that.
    *   **Oblation for Retinue:** The worshipper sages (Bhṛgu and Mārkaṇḍeya), Brahmā, Śiva, Viṣvaksena, and the flag-deity's is one *āḍhaka*; the other retinue deities and the fire-oblation's is half an *āḍhaka*.
    *   **Schedule of Oblations:** The oblation for the Lord and the two Goddesses in the morning is *maudgika* (mung bean rice), at noon is *gaulya* (sweet rice), at night is *pāyasa* (milk rice), and at midnight is two *āḍhakas* of oblation along with side dishes. The three-time oblation is two *āḍhakas*, which is the mantra-oblation, along with betel leaves and camphor.
    *   **Liturgy Scales:** In the daily fire-pit, there is worship of the formless fire. The worship is accompanied by three-time *anna-bali*, sixteen perpetual lamps with camphor, triple the number of twilight lamps, two hundred lamps in the morning and noon, and five hundred lamps at night, along with incense of camphor and aguru, flowers of jasmine, champaka, and blue lotus. The worship is performed for the thirty-two forms of the deity, accompanied by dance and music performed by temple dancers (*devadāsīs*) and devotees, along with flute players, drum players, and cymbol players. It includes morning waking proclamations, blowing of conch and beating of drums during bathing, oblation, *bali*, veil lifting, and circumambulation, and is performed in a temple of seven enclosures.

*   **2. Uttamamadhama / Uttamamadhyama (Highest of the Middle Class):** For the world guardians (*lokapālas*) and other retinue deities, offering oblation, without *bali*, two perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, one hundred and fifty lamps in the morning and noon, four hundred lamps at night, offering mantra-oblation during worship, twice daily fire-oblation, twice daily *anna-bali*, in a temple of six enclosures, accompanied by dance, vocal and instrumental music.

*   **3. Uttamādhama (Highest of the Lowest Class):** For the worshipper sages, Viṣvaksena, and flag-deity, without oblation, twelve perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, oblation at worship times, twice daily fire-oblation, accompanied by *anna-bali*, in a temple of five enclosures, with dance and music.

*   **4. Madhyamottama (Middle of the Highest Class):** For the worshipper sages and incarnations, twice daily oblation, ten perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, one hundred lamps in the morning and noon, three hundred lamps at night, twilight lamps, accompanied by dance and music by temple dancers and devotees, morning fire-oblation, noon *anna-bali*, in a temple of five enclosures.

*   **5. Madhyamamadhyama (Middle of the Middle Class):** For the incarnations, once daily oblation; for bathing, festival, and offering icons, one *āḍhaka* of oblation; eight perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, eighty lamps in the morning and noon, two hundred lamps at night, in a temple of four enclosures, with dance and music.

*   **6. Madhyamādhama (Middle of the Lowest Class):** For the *kautuka-bera*, one *droṇa* or half that; for the Goddesses, *maudgika*, *gaulya*, *pāyasa*, and cakes (*apūpa*) as available; six perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, sixty lamps in the morning and noon, one hundred and fifty lamps at night, all others as before.

*   **7. Adhamottama (Lowest of the Highest Class):** For the Lord, half a *droṇa* of oblation; at midnight, one *āḍhaka*; for the Goddesses, half that; for bathing, festival, and offering icons, once daily oblation; morning and evening worship-time oblation; four perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, forty lamps in the morning and noon, one hundred lamps at night, in a temple of three enclosures, with dance and music.

*   **8. Adhamamadhyama (Lowest of the Middle Class):** For the Lord, one *āḍhaka* of oblation; for the Goddesses, half that; three times or twice daily oblation; once daily oblation for bathing, festival, and offering icons; once daily worship-time oblation; without *gaulya* etc.; noon *anna-bali* and fire-oblation; two perpetual lamps, triple the number of twilight lamps, ten lamps in the morning and noon, twenty lamps at night, without dance, with instrumental music, in a temple of two enclosures.

*   **9. Adhamādhama (Lowest of the Lowest Class):** For the Lord, twice or once daily, one *āḍhaka* of oblation; for the Goddesses, half that; with yams (*vyañjana*) at noon as available; one perpetual lamp, double the number of twilight lamps, lamps as available; all others as before, without dance and music, in a temple of one enclosure.

*   **Daily Grains and Side Dish Weight Scales (*Pūjā-bhāra-lakṣaṇam*):** One should perform the highest worship for the highest deity; otherwise, a great defect arises. In a single temple, for the daily oblation of the Lord and other deities, the rice quantity per single service is:
    *   **Ten *bhāras*** (along with equivalent side dishes): *Uttamottama*.
    *   **Eight *bhāras***: *Uttamamadhyama*.
    *   **Equal *bhāra*** (or standard *bhāra*): *Uttamādhama*.
    *   **Six *bhāras***: *Madhyamottama*.
    *   **Five *bhāras***: *Madhyamamadhyama*.
    *   **Four *bhāras***: *Madhyamādhama*.
    *   **Three *bhāras***: *Adhamottama*.
    *   **Double *bhāra*** (two *bhāras*): *Adhamamadhyama*.
    *   **Single *bhāra*** (one *bhāra*): *Adhamādhama*, according to some authorities.
