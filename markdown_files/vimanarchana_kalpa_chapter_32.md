# Chapter 32: Consecration — The Threefold Classification of Fire-Offerings, Priests' Chantings, and Mantras of the Deities and the Supreme Self (Homatraividhyam, Hautra-śaṃsanādi-sarvadevatya-pāramātmikānukramaṇikā-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: The Threefold Classification of Fire-Offerings and Worship Around the Altar (Homa-traividhyam, Vedy-utsavādi-deva-pūjanam)

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ द्वात्रिंशः पटलः -**
> **हौत्र-शंसन-आदि-सर्वदेवत्य-पारमात्मिक-अनुक्रमणिका-विधिः**
> ***atha dvātriṃśaḥ paṭalaḥ -***
> ***hautra-śaṃsana-ādi-sarvadevatya-pāramātmika-anukramaṇikā-vidhiḥ***
> 
> **होम-त्रैविध्यम् -**
> एवं कृत्वा पश्चात् सर्वेषां परिवाराणाम् आलयं परितो होमम् आचरेत्।
> ***homa-traividhyam -***
> *evaṃ kṛtvā paścāt sarveṣāṃ parivārāṇām ālayaṃ parito homam ācaret.*
> 
> *   **अधम-होमः (Lowest grade):** Perform homas for Lakṣmī-Bhūmī (*Śrī-Bhūmyoḥ*), Brahmā-Śiva (*Brahma-Īśānayoḥ*), Bhṛgu-Mārkaṇḍeya, and Vīśa-Śeṣa (*Vīśa-Śeṣayoḥ*).
> *   **मध्यम-होमः (Middle grade):** Perform homas for the temple-guardians (*vimāna-pālas*), Indra and the world-guardians (*lokapālas*), and the Sun and Śeṣa.
> *   **उत्तम-होमः (Highest grade):** Perform homas for the door-guardians (*dvāra-pālas*), Chakra, Banner, Conch, the five elements (*mahābhūtas*), and Arjuna.
> **   **adhama-homaḥ:** perform homas for śrī-bhūmyoḥ, brahma-īśānayoḥ, bhṛgu-mārkaṇḍeyoḥ, vīśa-śeṣayoḥ.*
> **   **madhyama-homaḥ:** perform homas for vimāna-pālān, indrādi-lokapālān, āditya-śeṣayoḥ.*
> **   **uttama-homaḥ:** perform homas for dvāra-pālān, cakra-dhvaja-śaṅkha-mahābhūta-pāka-arjunānām.*
> 
> **वेदिं परितः देवार्चन-क्रमः -**
> यज्ञ-शालां महा-वेदिं वा परितः सर्वान् देवान् समभ्यर्च्य; महा-वेद्याः उपरिष्ठात् प्राग्-आदि-चतुर्-दिक्षु ध्रुव-कौतुकयोः आवरण-त्रय-देवान्, गर्भालय-अधिपान्;
> ***vediṃ paritaḥ devārcana-kramaḥ -***
> *yajña-śālāṃ mahā-vediṃ vā paritaḥ sarvān devān samabhyarcya; mahā-vedyāḥ upariṣṭhāt prāg-ādi-catur-dikṣu dhruva-kautukayoḥ āvaraṇa-traya-devān, garbhālaya-adhipān;*
> 
> दक्षिणे ब्रह्माणं मार्कण्डेयम्, उत्तरे शङ्करं ख्यातीशं (ख्यातीशं च), द्वारेषु धात्रादि-द्वारपालान्, विमानपालादीन् सर्वान् देवान् प्रथम-आवरण-आदि-आवरण-क्रमेण तत्-तत्-दिक्षु समर्चयेत् ॥
> *dakṣiṇe brahmāṇaṃ mārkaṇḍeyam, uttare śaṅkaraṃ khyātīśaṃ, dvāreṣu dhātrādi-dvārapālān, vimānapālādīn sarvān devān prathama-āvaraṇa-ādi-āvaraṇa-krameṇa tat-tad-dikṣu samarcayet.*
> 
> **प्रथमावरणे एव सर्व-आवरण-देवानां निवेश-पक्षः -**
> अथवा — इन्द्रं, न्यक्षं, श्रियं, हविःपालम्, अग्निं, भौमं, जगद्ध्रुवं, विवस्वन्तं, यमं, दुर्गां, समरोहिणीः, सप्त-मातृः (सममातृः), नीलं च, महाकालीं, पुरुषं, वरुणं, मित्रं, बुधं, ज्येष्ठां, पुष्येशं, वायुं, शुक्रं, सप्त-ऋषीन् (समर्षीन्), गङ्गां, धान्यं, क्षत्तारं, निशाकरं, पाञ्चभौतिकं, शान्तम्, ईशानं, बहिरक्षकं, वाग्देवीं च —
> ***prathamāvaraṇe eva sarva-āvaraṇa-devānāṃ niveśa-pakṣaḥ -***
> *athavā — indraṃ, nyakṣaṃ, śriyaṃ, haviḥpālam, agniṃ, bhaumaṃ, jagaddhruvaṃ, vivasvantaṃ, yamaṃ, durgāṃ, samarohiṇīḥ, sapta-mātṛḥ, nīlaṃ ca, mahākālīṃ, puruṣaṃ, varuṇaṃ, mitraṃ, budhaṃ, jyeṣṭhāṃ, puṣyeśaṃ, vāyuṃ, śukraṃ, sapta-ṛṣīn, gaṅgāṃ, dhānyaṃ, kṣattāraṃ, niśākaraṃ, pāñcabhautikaṃ, śāntam, īśānaṃ, bahirakṣakaṃ, vāgdevīṃ ca —*
> 
> एवं पूर्व-आदि-प्रदक्षिणं, प्राङ्-मुखे श्रीभूत-गरुड-चक्र-ध्वज-शङ्ख-यूथाधिप-विष्णु-भूतान् यथा-क्रमेण एकादश-उपचारैः अभ्यर्च्य, तत्-तद्-नाम्ना अर्चयेद् इति केचित् ॥
> *evaṃ pūrva-ādi-pradakṣiṇaṃ, prāṅ-mukhe śrībhūta-garuḍa-cakra-dhvaja-śaṅkha-yūthādhipa-viṣṇu-bhūtān yathā-krameṇa ekādaśa-upacāraiḥ abhyarcya, tat-tad-nāmnā arcayed iti kecit.*

#### English Translation
*   **Three Classes of Fire Offerings:** Having completed the preliminary rites, the priest offers homas around all the subsidiary shrines. They are classified into three grades:
    *   **Lowest (Adhama):** Offering fires to Lakshmī and Bhūmī, Brahmā and Śiva, Bhṛgu and Mārkaṇḍeya, and Vīśa and Śeṣa.
    *   **Middle (Madhyama):** Offering fires to the temple-guardians (*vimāna-pālas*), Indra and the world-guardians (*lokapālas*), and the Sun (*Āditya*) and Śeṣa.
    *   **Highest (Uttama):** Offering fires to the gateway guards (*dvāra-pālas*), Chakra, Banner, Conch, the five elements, and Arjuna.

*   **Worship Around the Altar:** Having worshipped all the deities around the sacrificial hall or the main altar (*mahā-vedi*), the priest worships the deities of the three enclosures (*āvaraṇa-traya*) and the sanctum lords to the east, south, west, and north of the main altar.

*   **Directional Assignments:** He worships Brahmā and Mārkaṇḍeya to the south; Śaiva deities (*Śaṅkara*) and Khyātīśa (Khyāti-pati) to the north; and the gate-guardians (*Dhātṛ* etc.) and temple-guardians at the doors, in clockwise order starting from the first enclosure.

*   **Consolidated Enclosure Alternative:** Alternatively, the priest may arrange all the enclosure deities in the first enclosure:
    *   Indra, Nyakṣa, Lakshmī (*Śrī*), Haviḥpāla, Agni, Bhauma, Jagaddhruva, Vivasvat, Yama, Durgā, the Samarohoṇīs, the seven mothers (*sapta-mātṛs*), Nīla, Mahākālī, Puruṣa, Varuṇa, Mitra, Budha, Jyeṣṭhā, Puṣyeśa, Vāyu, Śukra, the seven sages (*sapta-ṛṣīs*), Gaṅgā, Dhānya, Kṣattṛ, the Moon (*Nisha-kara*), the elemental deities, Śānta, Īśāna, Bahirakṣaka, and Sarasvatī (*Vāgdevī*).
    *   Moving clockwise starting from the east, and facing east, he worships: Śrībhūta, Garuḍa, Chakra, Banner, Conch, the troop-commanders (*yūthādhipa*), and the elemental spirits of Viṣṇu, offering eleven services (*ekādaśa-upacāras*) and chanting their respective names.

### Section 2: Hotr Hymns and Oblations in the Sabhya Fire (Hautra-praśaṃsanam, Praṇidhāv-āvāhanaṃ ca)

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **हौत्र-प्रशंसनम् -**
> प्रभूत-बलिम् आहत्य बलि-निर्वापं च कारयेत्। पश्चाद् होतारम् आहूय वस्त्र-उत्तरीय-आभरणैः अभिपूजयेत् (अभिपुजयेत्) ।
> ***hautra-praśaṃsanam -***
> *prabhūta-balim āhatya bali-nirvāpaṃ ca kārayet. paścād hotāram āhūya vastra-uttarīya-ābharaṇair abhipūjayet.*
> 
> स होता सभ्यस्य पुरतः तिष्ठेत्; सभ्य-अध्वर्युः अग्निं प्रज्वाल्य 'होतरेहि' इति वदति। स होता 'अध्वर्यो देवेतः' (अध्वर्योर्दिवते) इति उक्त्वा, आचम्य, प्रणव-पूर्वं 'नमः प्रवक्त्रे' (वै॰ म॰ प्र॰ ८) इति वदन् तिष्ठन् सोच्चं (उच्चैः) वैखानस-सूत्र-उक्त-विधिना हौत्रं प्रशंसयेत्।
> *sa hotā sabhyasya purataḥ tiṣṭhet; sabhya-adhvaryuḥ agniṃ prajvālya 'hotarehi' iti vadati. sa hotā 'adhvaryo devetaḥ' iti uktvā, ācamya, praṇava-pūrvaṃ 'namaḥ pravaktre' (vai. ma. pra. 8) iti vadaṇ tiṣṭhan soccaṃ (uccaiḥ) vaikhānasa-sūtra-ukta-vidhinā hautraṃ praśaṃsayet.*
> 
> अध्वर्युः तेन उक्तम् ओंकारं श्रुत्वा, सप्तदश-पालाश-इध्मान् (समदशपालाशेश्ध्मन्) पद्म-मुकुल-अग्रान् घृत-अक्तान् गृहीत्वा, अग्नौ 'ओं स्वाहा' इति क्षिपेत्;
> *adhvaryuḥ tena uktam oṃkāraṃ śrutvā, saptadaśa-pālāśa-idhmān padma-mukula-agrān ghṛta-aktān gṛhītvā, agnau 'oṃ svāhā' iti kṣipet;*
> 
> होता 'ब्राह्मणो भारतः' (वै॰ म॰ प्र॰ ८) इति अन्तम् उक्त्वा, यजमानस्य गोत्र-प्रवरं क्रमेण वदेत्। ग्रामो (यजमानो) यजमानः चेत्, काश्यप-प्रवरं वदेत्।
> *hotā 'brāhmaṇo bhārataḥ' (vai. ma. pra. 8) iti antam uktvā, yajamānasya gotra-pravaraṃ krameṇa vadet. grāmo yajamānaḥ cet, kāśyapa-pravaraṃ vadet.*
> 
> 'आयातु' (वै॰ म॰ प्र॰ ८) इति मन्त्र-अन्ते 'विष्णु-आदि-महाभूत-अन्तम् आवह' इति वदेत् ॥
> *'āyātu' (vai. ma. pra. 8) iti mantra-ante 'viṣṇu-ādi-mahābhūta-antam āvaha' iti vadet.*
> 
> **प्रणिधौ आवाहनं सभ्ये उक्तहोमः -**
> तथैव अध्वर्युः — आदिमूर्ति-आदि-शर्व-ान्तं दक्षिणे प्रणिधौ, धात्रादि-महाभूत-ान्तम् उत्तर-प्रणिधौ समावाह्य; आवाहन-क्रमेण जुष्टाकारं (making acceptable) कृत्वा, तत्-क्रमेण आज्य-आहुतीः जुहोति।
> ***praṇidhau āvāhanaṃ sabhye uktahomaḥ -***
> *tathaiva adhvaryuḥ — ādimūrti-ādi-śarva-antaṃ dakṣiṇe praṇidhau, dhātrādi-mahābhūta-antam uttara-praṇidhau samāvāhya; āvāhana-krameṇa juṣṭākāraṃ kṛtvā, tat-krameṇa ājya-āhutīr juhoti.*
> 
> सभ्य-अग्निं परिषिच्य, पालाश-इध्मान् गृहीत्वा, मूल-अग्राभ्यां घृतं स्पर्शयित्वा, देवं ध्यायन् विष्णु-गायत्र्या जुहोति।
> *sabhya-agniṃ pariṣicya, pālāśa-idhmān gṛhītvā, mūla-agrābyāṃ ghṛtaṃ sparśayitvā, devaṃ dhyāyan viṣṇu-gāyatryā juhoti.*
> 
> अग्निहोत्र-हवण्या आज्यं गृहीत्वा, 'अतो देवा' (ऋ॰ १-२२-१६) इत्यादिना हुत्वा; पश्चाद् उपभृता आज्यम् आदाय, विष्णु-सूक्तैः च हुत्वा; जुह्वां पञ्च-वारम् आदाय, वैष्णवं षोडश-वारं द्वादश-वारं वा जुहोति।
> *agnihotra-havaṇyā ājyaṃ gṛhītvā, 'ato devā' (ṛ. 1-22-16) ityādinā hutvā; paścād upabhṛtā ājyam ādāya, viṣṇu-sūktaiś ca hutvā; juhvāṃ pañca-vāraṃ ādāya, vaiṣṇavaṃ ṣoḍaśa-vāraṃ dvādaśa-vāraṃ vā juhoti.*
> 
> स्रुवेण आज्यम् आदाय, पञ्च-वारुणं, मूल-होमं, 'यद्देवा देवहेडनम्' इति कूष्माण्ड-होमं च सकृत् हुत्वा;
> *sruveṇa ājyam ādāya, pañca-vāruṇaṃ, mūla-homaṃ, 'yaddevā devaheḍanam' iti kūṣmāṇḍa-homaṃ ca sakṛt hutvā;*
> 
> पश्चात् — 'स्वस्ति चैवेहा स्वाहा, प्रजापतये स्वाहा, आदित्येभ्यः स्वाहा, विश्वेभ्यो देवेभ्यः स्वाहा, मरुद्गणेभ्यः स्वाहा, भूम्यै च पृथिव्यै च महते च स्वाहा' इति एतैः दशभिः मन्त्रैः शतशः जुहुयात्।
> *paścāt — 'svasti caivehā svāhā, prajāpataye svāhā, ādityebhyaḥ svāhā, viśvebhyo devebhyaḥ svāhā, marudgaṇebhyaḥ svāhā, bhūmyai ca pṛthivyai ca mahate ca svāhā' iti etaiḥ daśabhiḥ mantrair śataśaḥ juhuyāt.*
> 
> एषा सहस्र-आहुतिः — एनाम् उपांशु (whispered/silent) यजेत्।
> *eṣā sahasra-āhutiḥ — enām upāṃśu yajet.*
> 
> वैष्णवं, विष्णु-सूक्तं, पुरुष-सूक्तं, ध्रुव-कौतुकयोः पुरुषादि-आवरण-त्रय-देवानां वैष्णवं च तत्-तत्-मन्त्रैः जुहुयात् ॥
> *vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇu-sūktaṃ, puruṣa-sūktaṃ, dhruva-kautukayoḥ puruṣādi-āvaraṇa-traya-devānāṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca tat-tat-mantrair juhuyāt.*

#### English Translation
*   **The Hotr Rites:** Having gathered the grand food offerings (*prabhūta-bali*), the priest distributes them. Next, summoning the *hotṛ* (reciting priest), he honors him with garments, upper-cloths, and ornaments.

*   **The Dialogue:** The *hotṛ* stands in front of the *Sabhya* fire-pit. The *Sabhya* assistant (*adhvaryu*), having kindled the fire, says: *"O Hotṛ, come!"* The *hotṛ* replies: *"O Adhvaryu, devotee of the gods!"*, sips water (*ācamana*), and standing, chants aloud the *hautra* praises according to the Vaikhānasa manual, beginning with *"namaḥ pravaktre"* preceded by the *praṇava*.

*   **The Fire Oblations:** Hearing the *hotṛ* pronounce *"Om"*, the *adhvaryu* takes seventeen firewood sticks of Palāśa wood shaped like lotus buds at the tips and smeared with ghee, and casts them into the fire with *"Oṃ svāhā"*.

*   **Declaring Ancestry:** The *hotṛ*, chanting up to *"brāhmaṇo bhārataḥ"*, declares the lineage (*gotra*) and lineage-sages (*pravara*) of the patron in order. If the patron represents the village community collectively, he declares the *Kāśyapa-pravara*. At the end of the mantra *"āyātu"*, he says: *"I invoke the deities from Viṣṇu down to the five elements."*

*   **Invocations into Vessels:** Likewise, the *adhvaryu* invokes the deities from Ādimūrti to Śarva into the southern vessel (*dakṣiṇa-praṇidhi*), and from Dhātṛ to the elements into the northern vessel (*uttara-praṇidhi*). Making them acceptable, he offers ghee oblations in order.

*   **Fire Worship with Ghee:** Having sprinkled water around the *Sabhya* fire and taken Palāśa wood, he smears both the base and tip with ghee, and meditating on the Lord, offers it with the *Viṣṇu-Gāyatrī*.

*   **Using the Ladles:** Taking ghee with the *agnihotra-havaṇī* spoon, he offers it with *"ato devā"* (Ṛg Veda 1.22.16); next, taking ghee with the *upabhṛt* ladle, he offers it with the *Viṣṇu-sūkta* hymns; taking ghee five times in the *juhū* ladle, he offers the *Vaiṣṇava* homa sixteen or twelve times.

*   **Minor Homas:** Taking ghee in the *sruva*, he offers the five *Vāruṇa* homas, the *mūla-homa*, and the *Kūṣmāṇḍa-homa* (penance for omissions) once.

*   **The Thousand-fold Offering (Sahasrāhuti):** Then, he should offer oblations one hundred times with each of the ten mantras ending in: *"svasti caivehā svāhā, prajāpataye svāhā, ādityebhyaḥ svāhā, viśvebhyo devebhyaḥ svāhā, marudgaṇebhyaḥ svāhā, bhūmyai ca pṛthivyai ca mahate ca svāhā."* This constitutes the *sahasra-āhuti* (thousand-fold offering) and must be performed silently (*upāṃśu*).

*   **Final Homas:** He then offers the *Vaiṣṇava* homa, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and the specific *Vaiṣṇava* offerings for the enclosure deities of the fixed and movable images.

### Section 3: The Mantras for All the Enclosure Deities (Sarvadevatya-mantra-kramaḥ)

#### Verse / Paragraph 1
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **सर्व-दैवत्य-मन्त्र-क्रमः -**
> १. **श्रियै (लक्ष्मी):** 'श्रिया जाता' (वै॰ म॰ प्र॰ ८), 'हिरण्यवर्णा' (श्रीसूक्तं) इति द्वौ।
> २. **मह्यै (भूमि):** 'मेदिनी देवी' (भूमि-सूक्तं), 'भूमिर्भूम्यै' इति द्वौ।
> ३. **ब्रह्मणे:** 'ब्रह्म जज्ञानं' (यजु॰), 'हिरण्यगर्भः' (ऋ॰ १०-१२१-१) इति द्वौ।
> ४. **रुद्राय (शिव):** 'रुद्रं मन्युं' (यजु॰), 'त्र्यम्बकं' (ऋ॰ ७-५९-१२) इति द्वौ।

### Section 5: Specific Mantras in the Five Main Fires (Tattad-agnau homa-mantra-viśeṣāḥ)

#### Verse / Paragraph 1
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**

> *   **आहवनीये:** पुरुष-सूक्तं, भृगु-मार्कण्डेययोः मन्त्रं, ऐन्द्रं, आग्नेयं च;

> *   **आवसथ्ये:** एकाक्षरादि-सूक्तं, रौद्रं, कौबेरं, ईशान-दैवत्यं च;

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **tat-tad-agnau home mantra-viśeṣāḥ -**

**Translation (English):**
*   **The Main Fire Mantras:** The priest offers specific series of chants in the respective fires:
    *   **In the Āhavanīya:** The *Puruṣa-sūkta*, the mantras for Bhṛgu and Mārkaṇḍeya, and the *Aindra* and *Āgneya* (to Indra and Agni) hymns.

*   **The 105 Standard Chants:** These constitute the **one hundred and five (105)** standard *pāramātmika* hymns of the Vaikhānasa school.

### Section 7: Night Vigil and Conclusion of the Oblations (Vaiṣṇavānta-homānantaraṃ kartavyam)

#### Verse / Paragraph 1
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **वैष्णव-अन्त-होमानन्तरं कर्तव्यम् -**
> परिवाराणां तत्-तत्-होमेषु तत्-तन्-मन्त्रं वैष्णवं च द्वादश-वारं, अष्ट-वारं, चतुर्-वारं वा पर्यायतः जुहोति।

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **vaiṣṇava-anta-homānantaraṃ kartavyam -**
> parivārāṇāṃ tat-tat-homeṣu tat-tan-mantraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca dvādaśa-vāraṃ, aṣṭ-vāraṃ, catur-vāraṃ vā paryāyataḥ juhoti.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Final Oblations:** In the fire offerings of the subsidiary deities, the priest offers their respective mantras and the *Vaiṣṇava* mantras twelve, eight, or four times in rotation.

#### Verse / Paragraph 2
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> विष्णु-सूक्तेन पञ्चसु अग्निषु दधि-क्षीर-घृत-लाजैः युक्तम् उपजुहूर् (उपजु हु) आहुत्या; सभ्य-अग्निं पौण्डरीक-अग्निं च विना, सर्वेषु अग्निषु अन्त-होमं हुत्वा, अग्नीन् विसृज्य;

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> viṣṇu-sūktena pañcasu agniṣu dadhi-kṣīra-ghṛta-lājaiḥ yuktam upajuhūr āhutyā; sabhya-agniṃ pauṇḍarīka-agniṃ ca vinā, sarveṣu agniṣu anta-homaṃ hutvā, agnīn visṛjya;

**Translation (English):**
*   **The Consolidated Final Homa (*Anta-homa*):** Chanting the *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, he offers oblations of yogurt, milk, ghee, and puffed rice using the *upajuhū* ladle in the five main fires. Excluding the *Sabhya* and *Pauṇḍarīka* fires, he performs the final consolidated oblation (*anta-homa*) in all other fire-pits.

#### Verse / Paragraph 3
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> नृत्य-गेय-वाद्यैः च रात्रि-शेषं व्यपोहयेत् ॥

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> nṛtya-geya-vādyaiḥ ca rātri-śeṣaṃ vyapohayet.

**Translation (English):**
*   **Dismissal and Vigil:** The fires are then dismissed. The remainder of the night should be spent in vigil with the entertainment of sacred dance, song, and musical instruments.

#### Colophon
**Sanskrit (Devanagari):**
> **इति श्रीवैखानसे मरीचिप्रोक्ते विमानार्चनाकल्पे हौत्र-शंसन-आदि-सर्वदेवत्य-पारमात्मिक-अनुक्रमणिका नाम द्वात्रिंशः पटलः ॥ ३२ ॥**

**Transliteration (IAST):**
> **iti śrīvaikhānase marīciprokte vimānārcanākalpe hautra-śaṃsana-adi-sarvadevatya-pāramātmika-anukramaṇikā nāma dvātriṃśaḥ paṭalaḥ || 32 ||**
