# Chapter 59: Iconography and Consecration of Kṛṣṇa, Kalkin, and Ādimūrti (Kṛṣṇa-kalkyādi-mūrti-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules for the Dimensions, Iconography, and Consecration of Kṛṣṇa, Kalkin, and Ādimūrti

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ एकोनषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **कृष्ण-कल्क्यादि-मूर्ति-विधिः -**
> ***atha ekonaṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***kṛṣṇa-kalkyādi-mūrti-vidhiḥ -***
> 
> **(कृष्णस्य लक्षणम्)**
> अथ कृष्णस्य लक्षणं वक्ष्ये — मध्यम-दशतालमितं द्विभुजं श्यामाभं रक्तवस्त्रधरं सर्वाभरणभूषितं उद्बद्धकुन्तलम्; दक्षिणेन हस्तेन क्रीडायष्टिधरं, उद्धृत-वामकूर्परं, हिक्कासूत्राद् अधो द्वादशाङ्गुलं (मुद्रितपाठे दशाङ्गुलं), दक्षिणकर-कटकाग्रं नाभिदघ्नं वा; तस्मात् षट्सप्तत्युत्तराष्टादशाङ्गुलं (मुद्रितपाठे षट्समाऽष्टादशाऽङ्गुलनिम्नं), नासाग्रात् पादतलान्तं, हिक्कासूत्राद् वामाङ्गुलं, सूत्रात् तस्यार्धहृदयं, नाभेः दक्षिणे कलाङ्गुलं, मेढ्रभागं, वामे जङ्घान्तं, जगती यवं, सूत्रान्मात्रात्रयं पाष्ण्यन्तरम्; एवं समभङ्गसूत्रम् अन्यत् सर्वं राघवस्य उक्तवत्।
> ***(kṛṣṇasya laxaṇam)***
> *atha kṛṣṇasya laxaṇaṃ vakṣye — madhyama-daśatālamitaṃ dvibhujaṃ śyāmābhaṃ raktavastradharaṃ sarvābharaṇabhūṣitaṃ udbaddhakuntalam; daxiṇena hastena krīḍāyaṣṭidharaṃ, uddhṛta-vāmakūrparaṃ, hikkāsūtrād adho dvādaśāṅgulaṃ, daxiṇakara-kaṭakāgraṃ nābhidaghnaṃ vā; tasmāt ṣaṭsaptatyuttarāṣṭādaśāṅgulaṃ, nāsāgrāt pādatalāntaṃ, hikkāsūtrād vāmāṅgulaṃ, sūtrāt tasyārdhahṛdayaṃ, nābheh daxiṇe kalāṅgulaṃ, meḍhrabhāgaṃ, vāme jaṅghāntaṃ, jagatī yavaṃ, sūtrān mātrātrayaṃ pāṣṇyantaram; evaṃ samabhaṅgasūtram anyat sarvaṃ rāghavasya uktavat.*
> 
> दक्षिणे रुक्मिणीदेवीं हेमाभां धम्मिल्लकुन्तलयुतां वा, प्रसारित-दक्षिणहस्तां, सपद्म-वामहस्तां; वामपार्श्वे सत्यभामां श्यामाभां धम्मिल्लकुन्तलयुतां सोत्पल-दक्षिणहस्तां, प्रसारित-वामकरां सर्वाभरणसंयुक्तां कारयेत्।
> *daxiṇe rukmiṇīdevīṃ heamābhāṃ dhammilla-kuntala-yutāṃ vā, prasārita-daxiṇahastāṃ, sapadma-vāmahastām; vāmapārśve satyabhāmāṃ śyāmābhāṃ dhammilla-kuntala-yutāṃ sotpala-daxiṇahastāṃ, prasārita-vāmakarāṃ sarvābharaṇasaṃyuktāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> वामपार्श्वे गरुडं प्राञ्जलीकृत्य स्थितं कारयेत्। देवस्य दक्षिणहस्तं लीलायष्टियुतं, वामं सशङ्खं वा कारयेत्। तद्रूपं कौतुकं विष्णुं चतुर्भुजं वा कारयेत् ॥
> *vāmapārśve garuḍaṃ prāñjalīkṛtya sthitaṃ kārayet. devasya daxiṇahastaṃ līlāyaṣṭiyutaṃ, vāmaṃ saṅkhaṃ vā kārayet. tadrūpaṃ kautukaṃ viṣṇuṃ caturbhujaṃ vā kārayet.*
> 
> **(नवनीतनाटस्य लक्षणम्)**
> नवनीतनाटस्य (नवनीतनृत्तस्य) वामपादं समाकुञ्च्य स्थितं, दक्षिणम् उत्तान-कुञ्चितं, दक्षिणहस्तम् अभयं नवनीतयुतं वा, वामं प्रसार्य अनुत्तानं सर्वाभरणभूषितं अम्बरहीनं अम्बर-आधार-युतं नवनीतनृत्तरूपं कारयेत्।
> ***(navanītanāṭasya laxaṇam)***
> *navanītanāṭasya vāmapādaṃ samākuñcya sthitaṃ, daxiṇam uttāna-kuñcitaṃ, daxiṇahastam abhayaṃ navanītayutaṃ vā, vāmaṃ prasārya anuttānaṃ sarvābharaṇabhūṣitaṃ ambarahīnaṃ ambara-ādhāra-yutaṃ navanītanṛttarūpaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> **(कालियमर्दनस्य लक्षणम्)**
> एवम् एव कालिया-अहिफणोपरिस्थितं दक्षिणकरं सपताकं, वामकरेण अहिपुच्छं सङ्गृह्य नृत्यन्तं कारयेत्। कृष्णरूपाणि असङ्ख्यानि वक्तुं न शक्यानि, तस्माद् यथेष्टरूपं कारयेत् ॥
> ***(kāliyāmardanasya laxaṇam)***
> *evam eva kāliyā-ahiphaṇōparisthitaṃ daxiṇakaraṃ sapatākaṃ, vāmakareṇa ahipucchaṃ saṅgṛhya nṛtyantaṃ kārayet. kṛṣṇarūpāṇi asaṅkhyāni vaktuṃ na śakyāni, tasmād yatheṣṭarūpaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> **(कृष्णस्य प्रतिष्ठा)**
> अस्य प्रतिष्ठाविशेषं वक्ष्ये — पूर्वोक्त-यागशाला-मध्ये शय्यावेदिं परितः पञ्चाग्नीन् पौण्डरीकाग्निं च सङ्कल्प्य; पौण्डरीके प्रधानाग्नौ हौत्रं प्रशंस्य, 'कृष्णं पुण्यं नारायणं त्रिदशाधिपम्' इति कृष्णस्य; 'रुक्मिणीं सुदतीं देवीं रमाम्' इति रुक्मिण्याः; 'सत्यभामां सतीं सन्नतीं क्षामान्' इति सत्यभामायाः; गरुडस्य पूर्ववत्; 'नवनीतनाट कृष्णं देवकीसुतं बालरूपम्' इति नवनीतनटस्य; 'कालियमर्दनं कृष्णं गोपालं वटपत्रशायिनम्' इति कालियमर्दनस्य; अन्येषां रूपाणां तत्-तत्-कर्मनाम संयोज्य आवाहनादीनि कारयेत्।
> ***(kṛṣṇasya pratiṣṭhā)***
> *asya pratiṣṭhāviśeṣaṃ vakṣye — pūrvokta-yāgāśālā-madhye śayyāvediṃ paritaḥ pañcāgnīn pouṇḍarīkāgniṃ ca saṅkalpya; pouṇḍarīke pradhānāgnou houtraṃ praśaṃsya, 'kṛṣṇaṃ puṇyaṃ nārāyaṇaṃ tridaśādhipam' iti kṛṣṇasya; 'rukmiṇīṃ sudatīṃ devīṃ ramām' iti rukmiṇyāḥ; 'satyabhāmāṃ satīṃ sannatīṃ kṣāmām' iti satyabhāmāyāḥ; garuḍasya pūrvavat; 'navanītanāṭa kṛṣṇaṃ devakīsutaṃ bālarūpam' iti navanītanāṭasya; 'kāliyāmardanaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ gopālaṃ vaṭapatraśāyinam' iti kāliyāmardanasya; anyeṣāṃ rūpāṇāṃ tat-tat-karmanāma saṃyojya āvāhanādīni kārayet.*
> 
> 'सत्यः सत्यज्ञः' (वै॰ म॰ प्र॰ ७) इति मन्त्रेण अष्टोत्तरशतम् आवर्त्य हुत्वा; शेषं पूर्ववद् आचरेत्।
> *'satyaḥ satyajñaḥ' (vai. ma. pra. 7) iti mantreṇa aṣṭottaraśatam āvartya hutvā; śeṣaṃ pūrvavad ācaret.*
> 
> नित्यार्चनायां ध्रुवबेरम् अष्टोपचारैः, कौतुकं षोडशोपचार-मन्त्रैः संयोज्य अर्चयेत्। स्नपन-उत्सवादीनि पूर्ववत् कारयेत् ॥
> *nityārcanāyāṃ dhruvaberaṃ aṣṭopacāraih, kautukaṃ ṣoḍaśopacāra-mantraih saṃyojya arcayet. snapanotsavādīni pūrvavat kārayet.*
> 
> रुक्मिणी-सत्यभामयोः पृथक् प्रतिष्ठा चेत् श्री-भूम्योरिव कारयेत् ॥
> *rukmiṇī-satyabhāmāyoh pṛthag pratiṣṭhā cet śrī-bhūmyoriva kārayet.*
> 
> **(कल्किनः लक्षणम्)**
> कल्किनं मध्यम-दशतालमितम् अश्वाकारं मुखम् अन्यत् नराकारं च चतुर्भुजं शङ्ख-चक्र-खड्ग-खेटकधरं उग्ररूपं भयानकं एवं देवरूपं कृत्वा; तद्रूपं कौतुकं विष्णुरूपं चतुर्भुजं वा कारयेत् ॥
> ***(kalkinah laxaṇam)***
> *kalkinaṃ madhyama-daśatālamitaṃ aśvākāraṃ muṣaṃ anyat narākāraṃ ca caturbhujaṃ śaṅkha-cakra-khaḍga-kheṭakadharaṃ ugrarūpaṃ bhayānakaṃ evaṃ devarūpaṃ kṛtvā; tadrūpaṃ kautukaṃ viṣṇurūpaṃ caturbhujaṃ vā kārayet.*
> 
> **(कल्किनः प्रतिष्ठा)**
> अस्य प्रतिष्ठाविशेषं वक्ष्ये — पूर्वोक्त-यागशाला-मध्ये शय्यावेदिं परितः पञ्चाग्नीन् पौण्डरीकाग्निं च सङ्कल्प्य; पौण्डरीके प्रधानाग्नौ हौत्रं प्रशंस्य, 'कल्किनं विष्णुं कामरूपं संहारात्मकम्' इति आवाहनादीनि कृत्वा; 'धूर्णावहन्ता' (मुद्रितपाठे घूर्नोवहन्ता, वै॰ म॰ प्र॰ ७) इति मन्त्रेण अष्टोत्तरशतं हुत्वा; अन्यत् सर्वं समानं कुर्यात्।
> ***(kalkinah pratiṣṭhā)***
> *asya pratiṣṭhāviśeṣaṃ vakṣye — pūrvokta-yāgāśālā-madhye śayyāvediṃ paritaḥ pañcāgnīn pouṇḍarīkāgniṃ ca saṅkalpya; pouṇḍarīke pradhānāgnou houtraṃ praśaṃsya, 'kalkinaṃ viṣṇuṃ kāmarūpaṃ saṃhārātmakam' iti āvāhanādīni kṛtvā; 'dhūrṇāvahantā' (vai. ma. pra. 7) iti mantreṇa aṣṭottaraśataṃ hutvā; anyat sarvaṃ samānaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> नित्यार्चनायां पूर्वोक्त-अर्चाक्रमेण मूर्तिमन्त्रं संयोज्य अर्चयेत्। अवताराणां पूर्ववत् त्रेताग्निविधानेन एकाग्निविधानेन वा अग्नीन् सङ्कल्प्य; तत्-प्रधानाग्नौ हौत्रशंसनादीनि कारयेद् इति एके ॥
> *nityārcanāyāṃ pūrvokta-arcākrameaṇa mūrtimantraṃ saṃyojya arcayet. avatārāṇāṃ pūrvavat tretāgnividhānena ekāgnividhānena vā agnīn saṅkalpya; tat-pradhānāgnou houtraśaṃsanādīni kārayed iti eke.*
> 
> **(आदिमूर्तेः लक्षणं प्रतिष्ठा च)**
> अथ आदिमूर्तिविधिं वक्ष्ये — अनन्तोत्सङ्गे समासीनं दक्षिणं प्रसार्य वामम् आकुञ्च्य, दक्षिणहस्तम् अनन्तोत्सङ्गे न्यस्य, वामं वामजानूर्ध्वे प्रसार्य चतुर्बाहुं शङ्खचक्रधरं फणाभिः सप्तभिः पञ्चभिः वा छत्रमौलिं प्रवालाभं सर्वाभरणभूषितं कृत्वा; तद्-दक्षिणे भृगुं वामे मार्कण्डेयम् एकजानुक्रमेण आसादयित्वा, दक्षिणे ब्रह्माणं वामे शङ्करं च कृत्वा; तद्रूपं कौतुकं च कृत्वा प्रतिष्ठोक्तक्रमेण प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत्।
> ***(ādimūrteḥ laxaṇaṃ pratiṣṭhā ca)***
> *atha ādimūrtividhiṃ vakṣye — anantotsaṅge samāsīnaṃ daxiṇaṃ prasārya vāmām ākuñcya, daxiṇahastam anantotsaṅge nyasya, vāmaṃ vāmajānūrdhve prasārya caturbāhuṃ śaṅkhacakradharaṃ phaṇābhiḥ saptabhiḥ pañcabhiḥ vā chatramauliṃ pravālābhaṃ sarvābharaṇabhūṣitaṃ kṛtvā; tad-daxiṇe bhṛguṃ vāme mārkaṇḍeyam ekajānu-krameṇa āsādayitvā, daxiṇe brahmāṇaṃ vāme śaṅkaraṃ ca kṛtvā; tadrūpaṃ kautukaṃ ca kṛtvā pratiṣṭhoktakremeṇa pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> 'आदिमूर्तिम् अमितमहिमानम् अत्यन्ताद्भुतं सर्वाधारम्' इति आदिमूर्तेः; नागराजस्य पूर्ववद् आवाहनादीनि कारयेत्; अन्यत् सर्वं पूर्ववद् आचरेत् ॥
> *'ādimūrtim amitamahimānam atyantādbhutaṃ sarvādhāram' iti ādimūrteḥ; nāgarājasya pūrvavad āvāhanādīni kārayet; anyat sarvaṃ pūrvavad ācaret.*
> 
> **(अनन्तशयानस्य विधिः)**
> अनन्त-भोग-शयने प्रतिष्ठा चेत् अनन्तशयनादि-पञ्चमूर्तिमन्त्रैः आवाहनादीनि कारयेत्। पुष्पन्यासकाले आसीनम् एव स्मरेत्। सुसम्प्रबुद्धं देवेशं जाग्रद्भावेन संयुक्तं हरिं प्रणवेन उत्थाप्य; पादपार्श्वे पञ्चायुधान् च तत्-तत्-मूर्तिमन्त्रैः समर्चयेत्।
> ***(anantaśayanasya vidhiḥ)***
> *ananta-bhoga-śayane pratiṣṭhā cet anantaśayanādi-pañcamūrtimantraiḥ āvāhanādīni kārayet. puṣpanyāsakāle āsīnam eva smaret. susamprabuddhaṃ deaveśaṃ jāgradbhāveana saṃyuktaṃ hariṃ praṇavena utthāpya; pādapārśve pañcāyudhān ca tat-tat-mūrtimantraih samarcayet.*
> 
> अनन्तस्य फणापार्श्वे 'अनन्तं नागराजं सहस्रशीर्षं शेषं विष्णुसखम्' इति मूर्तिमन्त्रैः अर्चनं पुष्पन्यासं कुर्यात्।
> *anantasya phaṇāpārśve 'anantaṃ nāgarājaṃ sahasraśīrṣaṃ śeṣaṃ viṣṇusakham' iti mūrtimantraih arcanaṃ puṣpanyāsaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> कौतुकं विष्णु-आदि-पञ्चमूर्तिमन्त्रैः पूर्ववद् अर्चयेत्। उद्वासनकाले मन्त्रेण देवेशं शाययेत्। भोगशयने द्वाराभ्यन्तरे जयां विजयां च समर्चयेत्; अन्यत् सर्वं पूर्ववद् आचरेद् इति आह मरीचिः ॥
> *kautukaṃ viṣṇu-ādi-pañcamūrtimantraih pūrvavad arcayet. udvāsanakāle mantreṇa deaveśaṃ śāyayet. bhogaśayane dvārābhyantare jayāṃ vijayāṃ ca samarcayet; anyat sarvaṃ pūrvavad ācared iti āha marīciḥ.*

#### English Translation
*   **Iconography of Kṛṣṇa:** Now, the description of Kṛṣṇa: he is made in the *madhyama-daśatāla* (medium ten-span, 120 finger-units) system, has two arms, is dark-complexioned, wears red garments, is adorned with all ornaments, and has his hair tied up (*udbaddha-kuntala*). He holds a herding or play staff (*krīḍā-yaṣṭi*) in His right hand, and His left elbow is raised. His right hand is in the *kaṭaka* gesture at the level of His chest or navel. The plumb-line measurements and remaining body parts are in accordance with the proportions of Rāma.

*   **Rukmiṇī and Satyabhāmā:** On His right, Goddess Rukmiṇī is placed, golden-hued, with her hair bound in a bun or braids (*dhammilla-kuntala*), her right arm extended, and holding a lotus in her left hand. On His left, Goddess Satyabhāmā is placed, dark-complexioned, with her hair bound, holding a blue lotus in her right hand, and her left arm extended, adorned with all ornaments. On the left side, Garuḍa is depicted in a worshipful posture. Alternatively, the Lord's right hand can hold the play-staff, and His left hand can hold the conch, and the processional icon (*kautuka-bera*) must be made as the four-armed Viṣṇu.

*   **Child Kṛṣṇa (*Navanīta-nṛtta*):** For the dancing child Kṛṣṇa holding butter (*Navanīta-nṛtta*), He stands with His left leg bent and His right leg raised and turned upwards. His right hand is in the gesture of protection (*abhaya*) holding butter, and His left arm is extended outwards. He is unclad (naked), adorned with all ornaments, representing the butter-dance child form.

*   **Kāliyā-mardana:** Alternatively, He can be shown standing on the hoods of the serpent Kāliyā, with His right hand in the *patāka* gesture, and His left hand holding the tail of the serpent while dancing. The forms of Kṛṣṇa are innumerable; therefore, he can carve them according to his own preference.

*   **Consecration of Kṛṣṇa:** I shall explain the specific consecration: setting up the sacrificial hall as before, with the bedding altar and the five fires around it, and also the Pauṇḍarīka fire. Offering the chanter's praise at the primary Pauṇḍarīka fire: invoke reciting "Kṛṣṇaṃ puṇyaṃ nārāyaṇaṃ tridashādhipam" for Kṛṣṇa; "Rukmiṇīṃ sudatīṃ devīṃ ramām" for Rukmiṇī; "Satyabhāmāṃ satīṃ sannatīṃ kṣāmām" for Satyabhāmā; and invoke Garuḍa as before. For the child Kṛṣṇa form, invoke reciting "Navanītanāṭa kṛṣṇaṃ devakīsutaṃ bālarūpam". For the Kāliyā-mardana form, invoke reciting "Kāliyamardanaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ gopālaṃ vaṭapatraśāyinam". For other forms, combine their specific names and perform the invocation. Offer one hundred and eight oblations with the mantra "Satyaḥ satyajñaḥ". All other rites are performed as before. Worship the stationary deity with eight services, and the processional icon combining the form-mantras with the sixteen services. Perform the bathing and festivals as before. If Rukmiṇī and Satyabhāmā are consecrated separately, perform the rites like those for Śrī and Bhū.

*   **Iconography of Kalkin:** Kalkin is made in the *madhyama-daśatāla* system, has the face of a horse (*aśvākāra-mukha*) and a human body, is four-armed, holds the conch, disc, sword, and shield, has a fierce and terrifying appearance, or is in the form of a deity. The processional icon must be made in the four-armed Viṣṇu form.

*   **Consecration of Kalkin:** I shall explain the specific consecration of Kalkin: setting up the sacrificial hall, the bedding altar, the five fires, and the Pauṇḍarīka fire around it. Offer the chanter's praise at the primary Pauṇḍarīka fire: invoke reciting "Kalkinaṃ viṣṇuṃ kāmarūpaṃ saṃhārātmakam", and offer one hundred and eight oblations with the "Dhūrṇāvahantā" mantra. All other rites are identical. Worship in the daily worship with the specific form-mantras. Some say that for the incarnations, the fires can be set up according to the Tretāgni or Ekāgni method.

*   **Iconography of Ādimūrti:** Now, the rules for Ādimūrti: He is seated on the coils of Ādiśeṣa, with His right leg extended and His left leg bent. His right hand rests on the serpent coils, and His left hand is placed on His left knee. He is four-armed, holds the conch and disc, shaded by the seven or five hoods of the serpent as an umbrella, is coral-red-complexioned (*pravālābha*), and is adorned with all ornaments. On His right is sage Bhṛgu, and on His left is sage Mārkaṇḍeya, both seated on one knee. On His right is Brahmā, and on His left is Śaṅkara (Śiva). The processional icon must be made in the same form. Consecrate them according to the rules of consecration. Invoke the Lord reciting "Ādimūrtiṃ amitamahimānaṃ atyantādbhutaṃ sarvādhāram", and invoke the serpent king as before. Perform all other rites as before.

*   **Iconography of Anantaśayana:** If the consecration is for the Lord reclining on the serpent bed (*Anantaśayana*), perform the invocation with the five form-mantras starting with Anantaśayana. During the offering of flowers, he should meditate on the Lord as seated. He should wake up the Lord reciting the Praṇava, and worship the five weapons at His feet with their specific mantras. Beside the hoods of the serpent, worship reciting "Anantaṃ nāgarājaṃ sahasraśīrṣaṃ śeṣaṃ viṣṇusakham" and offer flowers. Worship the processional icon with the five mantras starting with Viṣṇu. At the time of dismissal (*udvāsana*), place the Lord to sleep with the specific mantra. Worship Jaya and Vijayā at the doorway. All other rites are performed as before—so says Marīci.
