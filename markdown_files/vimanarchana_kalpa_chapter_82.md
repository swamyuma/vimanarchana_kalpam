# Vimanarchana Kalpa of Sage Marīci (Vaikhānasa Āgama)
## Chapter 82: Sandhyopāsana-mantra-ṛṣi-chandas-devatā-vidhiḥ (Rules for Seers, Meters, and Deities of the Twilight Worship Mantras)

This document contains the Sanskrit text, IAST transliteration, and English translation of the 82nd Chapter (*Dvyāśītitemaḥ Paṭalaḥ*) of the **Vimānārcanākalpa** (attributed to Sage Marīci, representing the Vaikhānasa school of Vaishnavism).

This chapter details the specific seers (*ṛṣi*), meters (*chandas*), and presiding deities (*devatā*) for the core mantras utilized during the daily twilight prayers (*sandhyopāsana* / *sandhyāvandana*). It lists the assignments for mantras of water purification, the Gayatri, the Vyahritis, pranayama, the solar hymns (Mitra, Sūrya), and expiatory/cleansing prayers.

---

### Section 1: Seers, Meters, and Deities of the Primary Sandhya Mantras

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ द्व्यशीतितमः पटलः**
> **अथ सन्ध्योपासन-मन्त्राणाम् ऋषि-च्छन्दः-अधिदैवतानि व्याख्यास्यामः**
> ***atha dvyāśītitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***atha sandhyopāsana-mantrāṇām ṛṣi-cchandas-adhidevatāni vyākhyāsyāmaḥ***
> 
> ‘अग्निश्च सूर्यश्च’ (or ‘सूर्यो मा मन्युश्च’) इति एतयोः अग्निः सूर्यः च ऋषी, छन्दः (गायत्री) अधिदेवता च; ‘तेजोऽसि’ इत्येतस्य ऋषिः प्रजापतिः, छन्दो गायत्री, सविता देवता। ‘अग्निश्च’ इति एतस्य अग्निः ऋषिः, छन्दो गायत्री, अग्निः देवता। ‘आपो हि ष्ठा मयोभुवः’ इत्येतस्य सिन्धुद्वीपः ऋषिः, छन्दो गायत्री, आपो देवताः। गायत्र्याः विश्वामित्रः ऋषिः, छन्दो गायत्री, सविता देवता। प्रणवस्य ऋषिः प्रजापतिः, छन्दो गायत्री, ब्रह्मा देवता। भूरादि-व्याहृतीनाम् अग्नि-भृगु-कुत्स-सोम-वसिष्ठ-गौतम-
> *‘agniśca sūryaśca’ (or ‘sūryo mā manyuśca’) iti etayoḥ agniḥ sūryaśca ṛṣī, chandas (gāyatrī) adhidevatā ca; ‘tejo'si’ ityetasya ṛṣiḥ prajāpatiḥ, chando gāyatrī, savitā devatā | ‘agniśca’ iti etasya agniḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando gāyatrī, agniḥ devatā | ‘āpo hi ṣṭhā mayobhuvaḥ’ ityetasya sindhudvīpaḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando gāyatrī, āpo devatāḥ | gāyatriyāḥ viśvāmitraḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando gāyatrī, savitā devatā | praṇavasya ṛṣiḥ prajāpatiḥ, chando gāyatrī, brahmā devatā | bhūrādi-vyāhṛtīnām agni-bhṛgu-kutsa-soma-vasiṣṭha-gautama-*

#### English Translation
*   **The Subject:** Now, we shall explain the seers, meters, and presiding deities of the twilight worship mantras (*atha sandhyopāsana-mantrāṇām ṛṣi-cchandas-adhidevatāni vyākhyāsyāmaḥ*).

*   **Atonement Mantras:** For the mantras starting with "agniśca" and "sūryaśca" (or "sūryo mā manyuśca"), the seers are Agni and Sūrya respectively, the meter is Gāyatrī, and they are also the deities. For the mantra "tejo'si", the seer is Prajāpatī, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deity is Savitṛ. For the mantra "agniśca", the seer is Agni, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deity is Agni.

*   **Purifying Water and Gayatri:** For "āpo hi ṣṭhā mayobhuvaḥ", the seer is Sindhudvīpa, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deities are the Waters (*āpo devatāḥ*). For the Gāyatrī mantra, the seer is Viśvāmitra, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deity is Savitṛ. For the Pranava (Om), the seer is Prajāpatī, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deity is Brahmā.

*   **The Vyahritis:** For the seven *vyāhṛtis* starting with *Bhūḥ*: the seers are Agni, Bhṛgu, Kutsa, Soma, Vasiṣṭha, Gautama, and...

### Section 2: Vyahriti Seers, Pranayama, and Solar Hymns

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> कश्यप-अङ्गिरसः इति ऋषयः; गायत्री-उष्णिग्-अनुष्टुब्-बृहती-पङ्क्ति-त्रिष्टुब्-जगत्यः छन्दांसि; अग्नि-वायु-अर्क-वागीश-वरुण-इन्द्र-विश्वेदेवाः इति देवताः। गायत्र्याः पूर्ववत्। ‘ओमापो ज्योती रसोऽमृतं ब्रह्म भूर्भुवःस्वरोम्’ इति गायत्री-शिरसः ऋषिः ब्रह्मा, छन्दोऽनुष्टुप्, परमात्मा देवता। प्राणसंयमनं कृत्वा, सव्याहृतिकां सप्रणवां गायत्रीम् अन्ते सहिरस्कां त्रिरुच्चार्य जपेत्।
> *kaśyapa-aṅgirasaḥ iti ṛṣayaḥ; gāyatrī-uṣṇig-anuṣṭub-bṛhatī-paṅkti-triṣṭub-jagatyāḥ chandāṃsi; agni-vāyu-arka-vāgīśa-varuṇa-indra-viśvedevāḥ iti devatāḥ | gāyatriyāḥ pūrvavat | ‘omāpo jyotī raso'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūrbhuvahsvarom’ iti gāyatrī-śirasaḥ ṛṣiḥ brahmā, chando'nuṣṭup, paramātmā devatā | prāṇasaṃyamanaṃ kṛtvā, savyāhṛtikāṃ sapraṇavāṃ gāyatrīm ante sahiraskāṃ triruccārya japet ||*
> 
> सप्राणायामस्य (or सप्राणायाम-तर्पणस्य) ऋषिः प्रजापतिः, छन्दो गायत्री, परमात्मा अधिदेवता। ‘आयातु’ इति एतस्य वामदेवः ऋषिः, छन्दोऽनुष्टुप्, गायत्री देवता। सावित्र्याः विश्वामित्रः ऋषिः, छन्दो गायत्री, सविता देवता। ‘उत्तमे शिखरे’ इत्यस्य वामदेवः ऋषिः, छन्दोऽनुष्टुप्, गायत्री देवता। ‘मित्रस्य चर्षणीधृतः’ इत्यस्य विश्वामित्रः ऋषिः, छन्दो गायत्री; आद्यानां त्रिष्टुप्, अन्यस्य छन्दो बृहती (or जगती), रविः (or सूर्यः) देवता।
> *saprāṇāyāmasya (or saprāṇāyāma-tarpaṇasya) ṛṣiḥ prajāpatiḥ, chando gāyatrī, paramātmā adhidevatā | ‘āyātu’ iti etasya vāmadevaḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando'nuṣṭup, gāyatrī devatā | sāvitriyāḥ viśvāmitraḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando gāyatrī, savitā devatā | ‘uttame śikhare’ ityasya vāmadevaḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando'nuṣṭup, gāyatrī devatā | ‘mitrasya carṣaṇīdhṛtaḥ’ ityasya viśvāmitraḥ ṛṣiḥ, chando gāyatrī; ādyānāṃ triṣṭup, anyasya chando bṛhatī (or jagatī), raviḥ (or sūryaḥ) devatā ||*
> 
> ‘उद्वयं तमसस्परि’ इत्येषां हिरण्यस्तूपः ऋषिः; प्रथमादीनां यथाक्रमेण अनुष्टुप्-त्रिष्टुप्-गायत्री-जगती-उष्णिग्-ककुभः इति छन्दांसि, रविः देवता। ‘यच्चिद्धि ते’ इत्यादि शुनःशेपः ऋषिः, गायत्री-पङ्क्ति-अनुष्टुभः छन्दांसि। ‘कदा चन स्तरीरसि’ इति ‘इमं मे वरुण’ इति च, ब्रह्मा (or कण्वः) ऋषिः, छन्दो गायत्री। ‘तत्त्वा यामि’ ‘त्वं नो अग्ने’ इत्यादि त्रयाणां छन्दो गायत्री (or त्रिष्टुप्), वरुणो देवता इति। अन्येषां मन्त्राणां सामान्य-ऋष्यादीन् स्मृत्वा उच्चार्य कारयेत् ॥
> *‘udvayaṃ tamasaspaṣi’ ityeṣāṃ hiraṇyastūpaḥ ṛṣiḥ; prathamādīnāṃ yathākrameṇa anuṣṭup-triṣṭup-gāyatrī-jagatī-uṣnig-kakubhaḥ iti chandāṃsi, raviḥ devatā | ‘yacciddhi te’ ityādi śunaḥśepaḥ ṛṣiḥ, gāyatrī-paṅkti-anuṣṭubhaḥ chandāṃsi | ‘kadā cana starīrasi’ iti ‘imaṃ me varuṇa’ iti ca, brahmā (or kaṇvaḥ) ṛṣiḥ, chando gāyatrī | ‘tattvā yāmi’ ‘tvaṃ no agne’ ityādi trayāṇāṃ chando gāyatrī (or triṣṭup), varuṇo devatā iti | anyeṣāṃ mantrāṇāṃ sāmānya-ṛṣyādīn smṛtvā uccārya kārayet ||*

#### English Translation
*   **The Vyahriti Seers & Meters:** ...Kashyapa and Angiras are the seers (*kaśyapa-aṅgirasaḥ iti ṛṣayaḥ*). For the seven Vyahritis, the meters in order are Gāyatrī, Uṣṇih, Anuṣṭubh, Bṛhatī, Paṅkti, Triṣṭubh, and Jagatī; the presiding deities are Agni, Vāyu, Arka (Sun), Vāgīśa (Brahaspati), Varuṇa, Indra, and the Viśvedevas.

*   **The Gayatri Head (Śiras):** For the Gāyatrī mantra, the parameters are as before. For the Gāyatrī-head (*gāyatrī-śiras*) "omāpo jyotī raso'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūrbhuvahsvarom", the seer is Brahmā, the meter is Anuṣṭubh, and the deity is Paramātman. Having performed breath control (*prāṇasaṃyamanaṃ kṛtvā*), one should mutter the Gayatri accompanied by the Vyahritis, the Pranava, and ending with the Gayatri-shiras, three times (*savyāhṛtikāṃ sapraṇavāṃ gāyatrīm ante sahiraskāṃ triruccārya japet*).

*   **Pranayama and Invocation:** For the pranayama, the seer is Prajāpatī, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deity is Paramātman. For the invocation "āyātu", the seer is Vāmadeva, the meter is Anuṣṭubh, and the deity is Gāyatrī. For the Savitrī prayer, the seer is Viśvāmitra, the meter is Gāyatrī, and the deity is Savitṛ. For "uttame śikhare", the seer is Vāmadeva, the meter is Anuṣṭubh, and the deity is Gāyatrī.

*   **Solar and Expiation Mantras:** For "mitrasya carṣaṇīdhṛtaḥ", the seer is Viśvāmitra, the meter is Gāyatrī; for the first verses, the meter is Triṣṭubh, for the others Bṛhatī/Jagatī, and the deity is Ravi (the Sun). For the solar verses starting with "udvayaṃ tamasas pari", the seer is Hiraṇyastūpa; the meters for the first etc. in order are Anuṣṭubh, Triṣṭubh, Gāyatrī, Jagatī, Uṣṇih, and Kakubh, and the deity is Ravi.

*   **Varuṇa Hymns:** For "yacciddhi te", the seer is Śunaḥśepa, the meters are Gāyatrī, Paṅkti, and Anuṣṭubh. For "kadā cana starīrasi" and "imaṃ me varuṇa", the seer is Brahmā (or Kaṇva), the meter is Gāyatrī. For "tat tvā yāmi" and "tvaṃ no agne", the meter is Gāyatrī/Triṣṭubh, and the deity is Varuṇa. For other mantras, one should perform the ritual by remembering the general seers, meters, and deities (*sāmānya-ṛṣyādīn smṛtvā*) and pronouncing them.

## Glossary of Key Terms

1.  **Sandhyopāsana (सन्ध्योपासन):** The twilight worship or Sandhyavandana, performed thrice daily (sunrise, noon, sunset) by twice-born initiates.
2.  **Gāyatrī-śiras (गायत्रीशिरः):** The "head" of the Gayatri mantra ("omāpo jyotī raso'mṛtaṃ..."), recited at the end of pranayama.
3.  **Vyahṛti (व्याहृति):** The sacred utterances representing the cosmic planes (Bhūḥ, Bhuvaḥ, Svaḥ, Mahaḥ, Janaḥ, Tapaḥ, Satyam).
4.  **Prāṇasaṃyamana (प्राणसंयमन):** Breath control or pranayama, performed with the mental recitation of the Gayatri and Vyahritis to purify the body.
5.  **Savitar (सविता / सवितृ):** The solar deity representing the stimulus of creation, who is the primary deity of the Gayatri mantra.
6.  **Sindhudvīpa (सिन्धुद्वीप):** The Vedic seer associated with the water-purification hymns (*āpohiṣṭhā*).
7.  **Vāmadeva (वामदेव):** The Vedic seer associated with the Gayatri invocation (*āyātu varadā devī*).
8.  **Hiraṇyastūpa (हिरण्यस्तूप):** The Vedic seer associated with the solar hymns (*udvayaṃ tamasas pari*).
9.  **Śunaḥśepa (शुनःशेप):** The Vedic seer associated with Varuṇa hymns of forgiveness (*yacciddhi te*).
10. **Ravi (रवि):** The Sun, who is the physical representation of the Supreme Puruṣa and the focal deity of noon twilight worship.
