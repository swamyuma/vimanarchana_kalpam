# Chapter 67: Expiations for Ablution Ceremony (Snapana-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules and Expiatory Rites for Omissions, Deficiencies, and Errors in the Ablution (Snapana) Rituals

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ सप्तषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **(स्नपनप्रायश्चित्त-विधिः)**
> ***atha saptasastitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***(snapanaprāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)***
> 
> अथ स्नपन-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिं वक्ष्ये —
> *atha snapana-prāyaścitta-vidhiṃ vakṣye —*
> 
> **(स्नपन-त्रैविध्यम्)**
> स्नपनं च त्रिविधं — नित्यं, नैमित्तिकं, काम्यं च इति। अयनद्वये तथा विषुवे च स्नपनं यत्, तत् नित्यम्; चन्द्र-सूर्ययोः राहुग्रस्ते वा (मुद्रितपाठे चंद्रसूर्ये राहुग्रस्तेवा) यत् स्नपनं, तत् नैमित्तिकम्; शेषेषु यत् स्नपनं, तत् काम्यम्।
> ***(snapana-traividhyam)***
> *snapanaṃ ca trividhaṃ — nityaṃ, naimittikaṃ, kāmyaṃ ca iti. ayanadvaye tathā viṣuve ca snapanaṃ yat, tat nityam; candra-sūryayoḥ rāhugraste vā yat snapanaṃ, tat naimittikam; śeṣeṣu yat snapanaṃ, tat kāmyam.*
> 
> नित्यस्नपने हीने वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, वारुणं च दशकृत्वो हुत्वा पुनः स्नपनं कारयेत्।
> *nityasnapane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, vāruṇaṃ ca daśakṛtvo hutvā punaḥ snapanaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> ग्रहणकाले देवस्य स्नपने हीने महत्तरो दोषो भवति; पूर्ववद् प्रायश्चित्तं हुत्वा पश्चात् प्रयत्नेन स्नपनं कारयेत्।
> *grahaṇakāle devasya snapane hīne mahattaro doṣo bhavati; pūrvavad prāyaścittaṃ hutvā paścāt prayatnena snapanaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> काम्येषु स्नपनेषु हीनेषु पूर्ववद् प्रायश्चित्तं हुत्वा शुद्धोदकैर्वा स्नापयेत्।
> *kāmyeṣu snapaneṣu hīneṣu pūrvavad prāyaścittaṃ hutvā śuddhoadakairvā snāpayet.*
> 
> आलयाभिमुखे उत्तरे वा स्नपनालयम् उत्तमं (मुद्रितपाठे स्नपनालय मुत्तमं), ऐशान्यां मध्यमं, दक्षिण-पश्चिमयोः अधमं (मुद्रितपाठे दक्षिणपश्चिमयो रधमं) ।
> *ālayābhimuxe uttare vā snapanālayam uttamaṃ, aiśānyāṃ madhyamaṃ, daxiṇa-paścimayoḥ adhamaṃ.*
> 
> आग्नेयादिषु कोणेषु स्नपनं चेत्, वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, दिग्दैवत्य-मन्त्रांश्च (मुद्रितपाठे दिग्देवत्यमंत्रांश्च) हुत्वा स्नपनं कारयेत्।
> *āgneayādiṣu koṇeṣu snapanaṃ cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, digdaivatya-mantrāṃśca hutvā snapanaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> सन्ध्याकाले नैमित्तिके स्नपने प्राप्ते तत् नैमित्तिकं समाप्य पश्चात् नित्यपूजां समाचरेत्।
> *sandhyākāle naimittike snapane prāpte tat naimittikaṃ samāpya paścāt nityapūjāṃ samācaret.*
> 
> **(प्रतिसरबन्धाद्यभावे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> पूर्वरात्रौ प्रतिसरबन्धनं (मुद्रितपाठे प्रतिसरबंधनं), शयनं च विहीनं चेत्, वैष्णवं (मुद्रितपाठे वैष्णवे), श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, सौदर्शनं च हुत्वा, तत्काले शयनाधिवासं हित्वा प्रतिसरबन्धनम् एव कारयेत्।
> ***(pratisarabandhādyabhāve prāyaścittam)***
> *pūrvarātrou pratisarabandhanaṃ, śayanaṃ ca vihīnaṃ cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, soudarśanaṃ ca hutvā, tatkāle śayanādhivāsaṃ hitvā pratisarabandhanam eva kārayet.*
> 
> एकबेरे कौतुके च शयनं विना प्रतिसरबन्धनं सद्य एव कुर्यात्।
> *ekabere kautuke ca śayanaṃ vinā pratisarabandhanaṃ sadya eva kuryāt.*
> 
> अङ्कुरार्पणे (मुद्रितपाठे अंकुरार्पणे) कृते स्नपने हीने वैष्णवं, सौम्यं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, वैष्णवं च (मुद्रितपाठे वैष्णं च) हुत्वा स्नपनं कारयेत्। अङ्कुरार्पणं विना स्नपनं कारयेत् इति केचित्।
> *aṅkurārpaṇe kṛte snapane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, soumyaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā snapanaṃ kārayet. aṅkurārpaṇaṃ vinā snapanaṃ kārayet iti kecit.*
> 
> श्वभ्रे पङ्क्तौ वा (मुद्रितपाठे पंङ्क्तौवा) प्रमाणहीने आधिके च वैष्णवं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं (मुद्रितपाठे श्रीभूमि(भू) देवत्यं), पङ्क्तीशमन्त्रं (मुद्रितपाठे पंङ्तीशमन्त्रं) च जुहुयात्।
> *śvabhre paṅktou vā pramāṇahīne ādhike ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrībhūmidaivataṃ, paṅktīśamantraṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> विष्वक्सेनार्चने हीने वैष्णवं, वैष्वक्सेनं च जुहुयात् (मुद्रितपाठे जुहुयत्)।
> *viṣvakṣenārcane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, vaiṣvakṣenaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> पङ्क्तीशस्य अर्चने (मुद्रितपाठे पंङ्तीशस्याऽर्चने) हीने तन्मन्त्रं, जयादींश्च जुहुयात्।
> *paṅktīśasya arcane hīne tanmantraṃ, jayādīṃśca juhuyāt.*
> 
> इन्द्रादीनाम् अर्चने हीने तत्-तत्-मन्त्रं वैष्णवं च जुहुयात्।
> *indrādīnām arcane hīne tat-tat-mantraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> कलशेषु प्रमाणहीनेषु अधिकेषु च वैष्णवं, वायव्यं, आग्नेयं च जुहुयात्।
> *kalāśeṣu pramāṇahīneṣu adhikeṣu ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, vāyavyaṃ, āgneyaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> मृत्सु हीनासु वैष्णवं, महीदेवत्यं; दर्भेषु (मुद्रितपाठे पर्वतेषु) हीनेषु, तत्-तत्-वर्णहीनेषु प्रमाणहीनेषु च वैष्णवं, आग्नेयं च; धान्येषु हीनेषु वैष्णवं, वायव्यं च; अङ्कुरेषु हीनेषु गारुडं व्याहृतीश्च; अष्टमङ्गलेषु (मुद्रितपाठे अष्टमंगलेषु) हीनेषु तत्-तत्-वर्णहीनेषु प्रमाणहीने च वैष्णवं, ऐन्द्रं च जुहुयात्।
> *mṛtsu hīnāsu vaiṣṇavaṃ, mahīdevatyaṃ; darbhēṣu hīneṣu, tat-tat-varṇahīneṣu pramāṇahīneṣu ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, āgneyaṃ ca; dhānyeṣu hīneṣu vaiṣṇavaṃ, vāyavyaṃ ca; aṅkureṣu hīneṣu gāruḍaṃ vyāhṛtīśca; aṣṭamaṅgaleṣu hīneṣu tat-tat-varṇahīneṣu pramāṇahīne ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, aindraṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> पञ्चगव्ये योगक्रियाहीने मन्त्रहीने च वैष्णवं, रौद्रं च जुहुयात्।
> *pañcagavye yogakriyāhīne mantrahīne ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, raudraṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> पञ्चगव्यादि-द्वादश-प्रधान-द्रव्येषु उक्तप्रमाणहीनेषु जलेन पूरयित्वा तत्-तत्-अधिदैवतमन्त्रं वैष्णवं च जुहुयात्।
> *pañcagavyādi-dvādaśa-pradhāna-drayeṣu uktapramāṇahīneṣu jaleana pūrayitvā tat-tat-adhidaivatamantraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> द्रव्याणां सङ्करे वैपरीत्ये तत्-तत्-अधिदैवार्चने हीने मन्त्रसङ्कुले (मुद्रितपाठे मन्त्रसंकुले) हीने च नित्याग्नौ अष्टोत्तरशतं वैष्णवं यजेत्।
> *drayāṇāṃ saṅkare vaiparītye tat-tat-adhidaivārcane hīne mantrasaṅkule hīne ca nityāgnou aṣṭottaraśataṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ yajet.*
> 
> पुण्यपुष्पाद्युक्तद्रव्यालाभे यथालाभं सङ्गृह्य तत्-तत्-अधिदेवतामन्त्रं वैष्णवं च जुहुयात्।
> *puṇyapuṣpādyuktadrayālābhe yathālābhaṃ saṅgṛhya tat-tat-adhideavatāmantraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> साधिते कलशे भिन्ने (मुद्रितपाठे (भिन्ने) हीने) अन्यं कलशम् आदाय (मुद्रितपाठे अन्यंकलशमादाय) तथैव आपूर्य (मुद्रितपाठे तथैवाऽऽपूर्य) अभ्यर्च्य वैष्णवं दशशो जुहुयात्।
> *sādhite kalāśe bhinne hīne anyaṃ kalāśam ādāya tathaiva āpūrya abhyarcya vaiṣṇavaṃ daśaśo juhuyāt.*
> 
> विपर्यासोद्धृत-द्रव्येण स्नापयेत् चेत्, वैष्णवं तत्-तत्-द्रव्याधिपदैवत्यं च हुत्वा तद् द्रव्यं संशोध्य शुद्धोदकैः स्नापयेत्।
> *viparyāsōddhṛta-drayeṇa snāpayet cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ tat-tat-drayādhipadaivatyāṃ ca hutvā tad drayaṃ saṃṣodhya śuddhoadakaiḥ snāpayet.*
> 
> कलशन्यासे कृते श्व-काकादि-अस्पृश्य-स्पर्शने सति अन्यं कलशम् आदाय पूर्ववद् संसाध्य शान्तिं वैष्णवं च जुहुयात्।
> *kalāśanyāse kṛte śva-kākādi-aspṛśya-sparṣaṇe sati anyaṃ kalāśam ādāya pūrvavad saṃsādhya śāntiṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> द्रव्येषु कृमिकीटादि-पतने सति तद् त्यक्त्वा अन्यद् द्रव्यं (मुद्रितपाठे तच्चक्तवाअन्यद्द्रव्यं) सङ्गृह्य वैष्णवं तत्-तत्-द्रव्याधिपदैवत्यं च जुहुयात्।
> *drayeṣu kṛmikīṭādi-patane sati tad tyaktvā anyad drayaṃ saṅgṛhya vaiṣṇavaṃ tat-tat-drayādhipadaivatyāṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> रत्नानाम् अलाभे वैष्णवं हुत्वा सुवर्णं निक्षिपेत् (मुद्रितपाठे निक्षिपेत)।
> *ratnānām alābhe vaiṣṇavaṃ hutvā suvarṇaṃ nixipet.*
> 
> द्वादशवस्त्रालाभे वैष्णवं, श्रीदैवत्यं च हुत्वा, अष्टचत्वारिंशता (मुद्रितपाठे अष्ट चत्वारिषा) आच्छाद्य स्नपनं कारयेत्।
> *dvādaśavastrālābhe vaiṣṇavaṃ, śrīdaivatyāṃ ca hutvā, aṣṭacatvāriṃśatā ācchādya snapanaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> यथोक्तस्नपने येनकेनचिद् असंभृते सति विंशतिकलशादि-पञ्चकलशान्तैः कलशैः (मुद्रितपाठे कलशान्तैःकलशैः) स्नपयेत्।
> *yathōktasnapane yeanakeanacid asaṃbhṛte sati viṃśatikalāśādi-pañcakalāśāntaiah kalāśaiah snapayeet.*
> 
> अथवा शुद्धस्नपनविधिना स्नापयेत् इति एके (मुद्रितपाठे स्नापयेदित्येके) ॥
> *athavā śuddhasnapanavidhinā snāpayet iti eke.*

#### English Translation
*   **Threefold Classification of Ablution:** Ablution (*snapana*) is of three kinds: daily (*nitya*), occasional (*naimittika*), and optional/desire-based (*kāmya*). The daily bath is that performed at the two solstices (*ayana*) and two equinoxes (*viṣuva*); the occasional bath is that performed during solar or lunar eclipses when Rahu seizes the luminaries; the optional bath is that performed on all other occasions according to desires.

*   **Omission of Daily Ablution:** If the daily bath is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, *Puruṣa-sūkta*, and *Vāruṇa* oblations ten times each, and perform the bath again.

*   **Omission During Eclipses:** If the bath of the deity is omitted during an eclipse, a severe fault arises; offer the expiatory oblations as before, and perform the bath with great effort.

*   **Omission of Optional Ablution:** If optional baths are omitted, offer the expiatory oblations as before, or simply bathe the deity with pure water.

*   **Designated Locations for Ablution:** The best ablution-hall (*snapana-ālaya*) is in front of or to the north of the temple; the middle is in the northeast; the worst is in the south or west.

*   **Ablution in Forbidden Corners:** If the ablution is performed in the corners starting with southeast, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Viṣṇu-sūkta*, and *Puruṣa-sūkta* oblations, and the mantras of the directions, and then perform the bath.

*   **Occasional Bath During Twilight:** If the time for the occasional bath arrives during twilight, complete the occasional bath first, and then perform the daily worship.

*   **Omission of Wrist-Band and Reclining Rites:** If the wrist-band/protective thread (*pratisara-bandhana*) and the reclining (*śayana*) rites are omitted on the previous night, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *Saudarśana* (to Sudarśana) oblations, and perform the wrist-band ceremony immediately, omitting the reclining adhivāsana.

*   **Worship of Single Icons:** For a single icon (*eka-bera*) and the processional icon (*kautuka*), perform the wrist-band ceremony immediately without the reclining rites.

*   **Omission of Bath After Sprouting:** If after the sprouting ceremony (*aṅkurārpaṇa*) is completed, the ablution is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Saumya*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *Vaiṣṇava* oblations, and perform the bath. Some say that one may perform the bath even without the sprouting ceremony.

*   **Defective Foundation Pit:** If the pit (*śvabhra*) or the row (*paṅkti*) is deficient or excessive in dimensions, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Śrī-Bhūmi-daivata*, and *Paṅktīśa* oblations.

*   **Omission of Visvaksena Worship:** If the worship of Viṣvaksena is omitted, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Vaiṣvaksena* oblations.

*   **Omission of Line Deities Worship:** If the worship of the line deities (*paṅktīśa*) is omitted, offer their respective mantras and the *Jaya-ādi* oblations.

*   **Omission of Indra and Other Directional Deities:** If the worship of Indra and other directional deities is omitted, offer their respective mantras and the *Vaiṣṇava oblation.

*   **Defective Ablution Pots:** If the pots (*kalaśas*) are deficient or excessive in dimensions, offer the *Vaiṣṇava*, *Vāyavya*, and *Āgneya* oblations.

*   **Omission of Sacred Soils, Grasses, and Grains:** If the sacred soils (*mṛt*) are deficient, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Mahī-daivata* oblations; if the sacred grasses (*darbha*) are missing, or if they are deficient in color or dimensions, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Āgneya* oblations; if the grains are deficient, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Vāyavya* oblations; if the sprouts are deficient, offer the *Gāruḍa* oblation and the *vyāhṛtis*; if the eight auspicious items (*aṣṭa-maṅgala*) are deficient in color or dimensions, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Aindra* oblations.

*   **Errors in Panchagavya Preparation:** If the preparation or mantras for the five cow-products (*pañcagavya*) are deficient, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Raudra* oblations.

*   **Deficiencies in Principal Liquids:** If the twelve principal liquids (*dvādaśa-pradhāna-dravya* starting with *pañcagavya*) are short of their specified quantities, fill them up with water, and offer oblations to their respective presiding deities and the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation.

*   **Mixing and Reversal of Materials:** If the materials are mixed, reversed, if the worship of their presiding deities is omitted, or if there is a confusion of mantras, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation one hundred and eight times in the daily fire.

*   **Missing Worship Ingredients:** In the absence of holy flowers and other specified ingredients, collect whatever is available, and offer their respective presiding deities' mantras and the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation.

*   **Broken Ablution Pots:** If a prepared pot breaks or is missing, take another pot, fill it in the same manner, worship it, and offer the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation ten times.

*   **Calamities During Ablution:** If the bath is performed with contrarily gathered materials, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and the presiding deities' oblations for those materials, purify the materials, and bathe the deity with pure water.

*   **Defilement of Pots:** If after the pots are arranged, they are touched by dogs, crows, or other untouchables, take another pot, prepare it as before, perform the peace ceremony (*śānti*), and offer the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation.

*   **Insects in Bath Liquids:** If insects, worms, etc. fall into the liquids, discard them, collect fresh liquids, and offer the *Vaiṣṇava and the presiding deities' oblations for those liquids.

*   **Missing Gems and Garments:** In the absence of gems, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* oblation and deposit gold. In the absence of twelve garments, offer the *Vaiṣṇava* and *Śrī-daivata* oblations, cover the pots with forty-eight threads/cloths, and perform the bath.

*   **Inability to Gather All Materials:** If some of the specified materials for the bath are unavailable, perform the bath with twenty pots or five pots of water, etc. Or, according to some, perform the bath according to the rules of *śuddha-snapana* (pure/simple bath).
