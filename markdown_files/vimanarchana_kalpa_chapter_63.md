# Chapter 63: Renovation and Re-consecration of the Temple (Karṣaṇādi-punaḥsaṃskāra-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules for Temple Restoration, Image Reconstruction, and Re-consecration

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ त्रिषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **कर्षणादि-पुनस्संस्कार-विधिः -**
> ***atha triṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***karṣaṇādi-punassaṃskāra-vidhiḥ -***
> 
> **(कर्षणादि-पुनस्संस्कारः)**
> अथ कर्षणादि पुनस्संस्कारविधिं वक्ष्ये — तत्र आलये (मुद्रितपाठे तत्राऽऽलये) तृण-गुल्म-लतादीन् संशोध्य; शिलाप्रक्षेपणं, इष्टकाप्रक्षेपणं, दारुप्रक्षेपणं, सुधावर्णलेपनं च कृत्वा; आलयस्य उत्तरे वास्तुहोमं हुत्वा; पुण्याहान्ते पर्यग्निं कृत्वा; पञ्चगव्यैः समभ्युक्ष्य (मुद्रितपाठे पञ्चगव्यै स्समभ्युक्ष्य); कर्षणाार्थम् आलयाभिमुखे (मुद्रितपाठे कर्षणार्थमाल्याऽभिमुखे) व्रीहिभिः स्थण्डिलं कृत्वा; देवेशं, विष्वक्सेनम्, अनपायिनं च अभ्यर्च्य; हविः निवेद्य; सुवर्णेन हलं कृत्वा; आचार्यो हस्तेन गर्भागारादि सर्वत्र विष्णोः अनुवाकाद्यैः (मुद्रितपाठे विष्णोर्नुकाद्यैः) कर्षयित्वा यथोक्तानि बीजानि भारम् अर्धं वा हृत्वा अभ्युक्ष्य (मुद्रितपाठे हृत्वाऽभ्युक्ष्य); तस्मिन् सोमम् अभ्यर्च्य; विष्णुगायत्र्या अभिमन्त्र्य, 'सोमं राजानम्' (मुद्रितपाठे सोमंराजान) इति मन्त्रेण सर्वत्र वापनं कृत्वा; तस्य उपरि दूर्वादि-तृणभारान् आस्तीर्य; तान् गोदानसूक्तम् उच्चार्य गोक्षणेभ्यो (गोभ्यो) निवेद्य; आलयं संशोध्य; ब्रह्मादीनां पञ्चानां, सावित्रादीनाम् अष्टानां, ईशादीनां द्वात्रिंशद्-देवानां, नागादीनां पञ्चविंशति-देवानां (मुद्रितपाठे पञ्चविंशद्देवानां), रत्यादीनां चतसृणां च द्रोणैः द्रोणार्धैः वा तण्डुलैः एकपात्रे चरुं पक्त्वा; सघृतं बलिम् आहृत्य; पूर्वम् उदकं, पुष्पं, बलिं, उदकं च तद्-तद्-नाम्ना दत्त्वा; आलयं संशोध्य; आद्येष्टकार्थम् औपासनाग्निकुण्डं (मुद्रितपाठे मौपासनाग्निकुण्डं) कृत्वा; अग्निं परिस्तीर्य; वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, दिग्दैवतं च हुत्वा; चतुर्वेद-आदि-मन्त्रान् देवं ध्यायन् जप्त्वा; आद्येष्टकास्थानं संस्पृश्य अन्तःपरिषेकं कृत्वा; अग्निं परिस्तीर्य; गर्भविन्यासार्थं वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीसूक्तं, महीसूक्तं, दिग्दैवतं च हुत्वा; 'नागराजाय स्वाहा, सर्वरत्नेभ्यः स्वाहा, सर्वधातुभ्यः स्वाहा, सर्वबीजेभ्यः स्वाहा, सर्वलोहेभ्यः स्वाहा, नदीभ्यः स्वाहा, पातालेभ्यः स्वाहा, नागेभ्यः स्वाहा, दिग्गजेभ्यः स्वाहा' इति हुत्वा; अभ्यन्तरं द्वार-दक्षिण-स्तम्भाद् दक्षिणे मेदिनीम् अभ्यर्च्य, पुरुषसूक्तं मेदिन्यादीन् जप्त्वा; तत्-तत्-स्थानं संस्पृश्य अन्तःपरिषेकं कृत्वा परिषिच्य; मूर्ध्नेष्टकार्थं (मुद्रितपाठे अंत्येष्टकार्थं) वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, विमानपाल-दैवतं च हुत्वा; विमानस्य उपरिष्ठात् स्थूपिकीलाद् अधस्तात् चतुर्वेद-आदि-मन्त्रान् विष्णुसूक्तं च जप्त्वा अभिमृशेत्।
> ***(karṣaṇādi-punassaṃskāraḥ)***
> *atha karṣaṇādi punassaṃskāravidhiṃ vakṣye — tatra ālaye tṛṇa-gulma-latādīn saṃśodhya; śilāpraxeapaṇaṃ, iṣṭakāpraxeapaṇaṃ, dārupraxeapaṇaṃ, sudhāvarṇaleapanaṃ ca kṛtvā; ālayasya uttare vāstuhomaṃ hutvā; puṇyāhānter paryagniṃ kṛtvā; pañcagavyaih samabhyuxya; karṣaṇārtham ālayābhimuxe vrīhibhiḥ sthaṇḍilaṃ kṛtvā; deaveśaṃ, viṣvakseaṇam, anapāyinaṃ ca abhyarcya; haviḥ nivedya; suvarṇeana halaṃ kṛtvā; ācāryo hasteana garbhāgārādi sarvatra viṣṇoh anuvākādyaih karṣayitvā yathoktāni bījāni bhāram ardhaṃ vā hṛtvā abhyuxya; tasmin somam abhyarcya; viṣṇugāyatryā abhimantrya, 'somaṃ rājānam' iti mantreaṇa sarvatra vāpanaṃ kṛtvā; tasya upari dūrvādi-tṛṇabhārān āstīrya; tān godānasūktam uccārya gōkṣaṇebhyo nivedya; alayaṃ saṃṣodhya; brahmādīnāṃ pañcānāṃ, sāvitrādīnām aṣṭānāṃ, īśādīnāṃ dvātriṃśad-devānāṃ, nāgādīnāṃ pañcaviṃśati-devānāṃ, ratyādīnāṃ catasṛṇāṃ ca droṇaih droṇārdhaih vā taṇḍulaih ekapātre caruṃ paktvā; saghṛtaṃ balim āhṛtya; pūrvam udakaṃ, puṣpaṃ, baliṃ, udakaṃ ca tad-tad-nāmnā dattva; alayaṃ saṃṣodhya; ādyeṣṭakārtham aupāsanāgnikuṇḍaṃ kṛtvā; agniṃ paristīrya; vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, digdaivataṃ ca hutvā; caturveda-adi-mantrān deavaṃ dhyāyan japtvā; ādyeṣṭakāsthānaṃ saṃspṛśya antaḥpariṣekaṃ kṛtvā; agniṃ paristīrya; garbhavinyāsārthaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrīsūktaṃ, mahīsūktaṃ, digdaivataṃ ca hutvā; 'nāgarājāya svāhā, sarvaratnebhyaḥ svāhā, sarvadhātubhyaḥ svāhā, sarvabījebhyaḥ svāhā, sarvalōhebhyaḥ svāhā, nadībhyaḥ svāhā, pātālebhyaḥ svāhā, nāgebhyaḥ svāhā, diggajebhyaḥ svāhā' iti hutvā; abhyantaraṃ dvāra-daxiṇa-stambhād daxiṇe medinīm abhyarcya, puruṣasūktaṃ medinyādīn japtvā; tat-tat-sthānaṃ saṃspṛśya antaḥpariṣekaṃ kṛtvā pariṣicya; mūrdhneṣṭakārthaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, vimānapāla-daivataṃ ca hutvā; vimānanya upariṣṭhāt sthūpikīlād adhastāt caturveda-adi-mantrān viṣṇusūktaṃ ca japtvā abhimṛśet.*
> 
> ध्रुवबेरं मानहीनं चेत्, अग्निं परिस्तीर्य; ध्रुवस्थापनोक्तवद् होमं हुत्वा; पूर्ववद् रत्नन्यास-मन्त्रान् स्थापनसूक्तं च जप्त्वा संस्थाप्य, नवीकरणं कृत्वा कौतुकादिभिः सह प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत् ॥
> *dhruvaberaṃ mānahīnaṃ cet, agniṃ paristīrya; dhruvavasthāpanōktavad homaṃ hutvā; pūrvavad ratnanyāsa-mantrān sthāpanasūktaṃ ca japtvā saṃsthāpya, navīkaraṇaṃ kṛtvā kautukādibhiḥ saha pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> **(बेरस्य नाशे चलनावस्थायां च प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> ध्रुवे नष्टे पूर्वद्रव्येण उत्कृष्टद्रव्येण वा बेरं सलक्षणं कृत्वा प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत् ॥
> ***(berasya nāśe calanāvasthāyāṃ ca prāyaścittam)***
> *dhruve naṣṭe pūrvadravyeṇa utkṛṣṭadravyeṇa vā beraṃ salaxaṇaṃ kṛtvā pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> नर-वृष-गजाद्यैः उत्पाटितं बेरम् अहीन-अङ्गं चेत् बेरशुद्ध्यर्थं जलाधिवासं कृत्वा; तत्-स्थाने रत्नन्यासं कृत्वा पुनःप्रतिष्ठां कारयेत्।
> *nara-vṛṣa-gajādyaiah utpāṭitaṃ beraṃ ahīna-aṅgaṃ cet beraśuddhyarthaṃ jalādhivāsaṃ kṛtvā; tat-sthāne ratnanyāsaṃ kṛtvā punaḥpratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> अन्य-आलयात् अपहृतं (मुद्रितपाठे अपहृत(मात्रं)) निष्पन्नक्रियं शिलाबेरं यदि स्यात्, तद् भूमौ पिधाय (निखन्य); विधिना आहृत्य; शेषक्रियां कृत्वा विधिना स्थापयेत्।
> *anya-ālayāt apahṛtaṃ niṣpannakriyaṃ śilāberaṃ yadi syāt, tad bhūmou pidhāya; vidhinā āhṛtya; śeṣakriyāṃ kṛtvā vidhinā sthāpayet.*
> 
> शिल्पिना शिलाग्रहणं कृत्वा सलक्षणं कृतं बेरं स्थापितम् इच्छेत् चेत्, विधिना आहृत्य प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत्।
> *śilpinā śilāgrahaṇaṃ kṛtvā salaxaṇaṃ kṛtaṃ beraṃ sthāpitam icchet cet, vidhinā āhṛtya pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> यथाविधि यथास्थानं स्थापितं निर्दोषबेरं न चालयेत्। तद् अज्ञानात् चालितं चेत्, राज्ञो राज्यस्य च अशुभाय नाशाय भवति। तद्-दोषशमनार्थं पञ्चाग्नौ महाशान्तिं हुत्वा; विप्रशतं भोजयित्वा; सुवर्णदानं गोदानं च कृत्वा पुनःप्रतिष्ठां कारयेत्।
> *yathāvidhi yathāsthānaṃ sthāpitaṃ nirdoṣaberaṃ na cālayet. tad ajñānāt cālitaṃ cet, rājño rājyasya ca aśubhāya nāśāya bhavati. tad-doṣaśamanārthaṃ pañcāgnou mahāśāntiṃ hutvā; vipraśataṃ bhojayitvā; suvarṇadānaṃ godānaṃ ca kṛtvā punaḥpratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet.*
> 
> आचार्याद्यैः अविधिज्ञैः स्थापितं बेरं क्षिप्रं चालयित्वा; यथाविधि विधिज्ञैः पुनः स्थापयेत्।
> *ācāryādyaiah avidhijñaiah sthāpitaṃ beraṃ kṣipraṃ cālayitvā; yathāvidhi vidhijñaiah punaḥ sthāpayet.*
> 
> विधिज्ञैः स्थापितं बेरं चालितं चेत्; शान्तिं हुत्वा; वैष्णवान् सम्पूज्य यथास्थानं यथाविधि स्थापयेत्।
> *vidhijñaiah sthāpitaṃ beraṃ cālitaṃ cet; śāntiṃ hutvā; vaiṣṇavān sampūjya yathāsthānaṃ yathāvidhi sthāpayet.*
> 
> विमानं ध्रुवं च, नदी-तटाक-समुद्र-सङ्बाध-महावातादिना येन केनचित् चालितं चेत्, तद् बेरं भूमौ पिधाय; तत्-स्थाने पूर्ववद् विमानं कृत्वा ध्रुवबेरम् अचलं स्थापयेत्।
> *vimānaṃ dhruvaṃ ca, nadī-taṭāka-samudra-saṅbādha-mahāvātādinā yeana keanacit cālitaṃ cet, tad beraṃ bhūmou pidhāya; tat-sthāne pūrvavad vimānaṃ kṛtvā dhruvaberaṃ acalaṃ sthāpayet.*
> 
> अथवा तत्-समीपे ग्रामादौ सति तत्र विविक्ते देशे मनोरमे तथैव विमानम् उत्कृष्टं वा कृत्वा; तद् बेरं सपरिषत्कम् आहृत्य संस्थाप्य विधिना अर्चयेत्।
> *athavā tat-samīpe grāmādou sati tatra vivikte deaśe manorame tathāiva vimānama utkṛṣṭaṃ vā kṛtvā; tad beraṃ sapariṣatkam āhṛtya saṃsthāpya vidhinā arcayet.*
> 
> ग्रामादीनां हीने तस्मात् शतदण्डाद् ऊर्ध्वं विस्तीर्णे देशे पूर्ववद् विमानं कृत्वा तद् देवं स्थापयेत्।
> *grāmādīnāṃ hīne tasmāt śatadaṇḍād ūrdhvaṃ vistīrṇe deaśe pūrvavad vimānaṃ kṛtvā tad deavaṃ sthāpayet.*
> 
> राजा राष्ट्रान्तरं जित्वा बेरम् आहृत्य स्थापितुं यदि इच्छेत्, स्वराष्ट्र मनोरमे देशे ग्रामविन्यासं कृत्वा; तद्-वास्तु-विभाग-आलये बेरं विधिना संस्थाप्य अर्चनादीनि कारयेत् ॥
> *rājā rāṣṭrāntaraṃ jitva beraṃ āhṛtya sthāpitaṃ yadi icchet, svarāṣṭre manorame deaśe grāmavinyāsaṃ kṛtvā; tad-vāstu-vibhāga-ālaye beraṃ vidhinā saṃsthāpya arcanādīni kārayet.*
> 
> अपौरुषेयस्य (मुद्रितपाठे अपीरुषस्य) देवावासस्य समीपे पौरुषं विमानं कर्तुम् इच्छेत् चेत्, तस्मिन् प्राकार-अभ्यन्तरे तद्-बाह्ये वा, भूशुद्धिं विना प्रासादं प्रतिमां च कृत्वा प्रतिष्ठाप्य अर्चयेत्।
> *apauruṣeyasya devāvāsasya samīpe pauruṣaṃ vimānaṃ kartum icchet cet, tasmin prākāra-abhyantare tad-bāhye vā, bhūśuddhiṃ vinā prāsādaṃ pratimāṃ ca kṛtvā pratiṣṭhāpya arcayet.*
> 
> तस्य मूलस्थान-अर्चन-समफलं (मुद्रितपाठे मूलस्थानाऽर्चनसमफल) मचिराद् एव सिध्यति इति आह मरीचिः ॥
> *tasya mūlasthāna-arcana-samaphalaṃ macirād eva sidhyati iti āha marīciḥ.*

#### English Translation
*   **Renovation of the Temple Premises:** Now, the rules for renovation starting with plowing (*Karṣaṇa*): clearing the grass, shrubs, and creepers around the temple; performing masonry work, placing stones, bricks, and wood, plastering and white-washing. To the north of the temple, offer the Vāstu-homa, perform the *puṇyāha* purification, circumambulate with fire, and sprinkle the area with *pañcagavya*. In front of the temple, build a grain pedestal, worship the Lord, Viṣvaksena, and Anapāyin, offer cooked food (*havis*), and ornament a gold plow. The priest should plow the sanctuary and all areas with his hand while reciting the Viṣṇu-anuvākas. Having brought half the seeds, sprinkle them with water, worship Soma, consecrate them with the Viṣṇu-gāyatrī, and sow them in all areas reciting "Somam rājānam". Spread dūrvā grass and straw over them, recite the Go-dāna-sūkta, and present them to the cows.

*   **Offerings to the Grid Deities:** Clean the temple. Offer cooked rice (*caru*) cooked in one vessel using one, half, or quarter *droṇa* of rice to the five deities starting with Brahmā, the eight starting with Sāvitra, the thirty-two starting with Īśa, the twenty-five starting with Nāga, and the four starting with Rati. Present boundaries (*bali*) with ghee, offering water, flowers, and boundary-offerings while reciting their names. Clean the temple.

*   **Foundation and Womb Deposits for Restoration:** Kindle the Aupāsana fire-pit for the foundation stone (*ādyeṣṭikā*), circumambulate the fire, and offer oblations with the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, and Digdevatā mantras. Reciting the four Vedas while meditating on the Lord, touch the foundation stone, and perform the internal *pariṣecana*. Circumambulate the fire. For the womb-deposit (*garbha-nyāsa*), offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, Śrī-sūkta, Mahī-sūkta, and Digdevatā oblations. Offer specific oblations: "Nāgarājāya svāhā", "Sarvaratnebhyah svāhā", "Sarvadhātubhyah svāhā", "Sarvabījebhyah svāhā", "Sarvalohebhyah svāhā", "Nadībhyah svāhā", "Pātālebhyah svāhā", "Nāgebhyah svāhā", and "Diggajebhyah svāhā". On the right of the southern doorpost inside the temple, worship the Earth goddess (*Medinī*), chant the Puruṣa-sūkta and Medinī-mantra, touch the location, and perform the *pariṣecana*. For the top-stone (*mūrdhneṣṭikā*), offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, and Vimānapāla-devatā oblations. On top of the temple, below the peg of the finial (*sthūpi-kīla*), chant the four Vedas and the Viṣṇu-sūkta, and touch the finial.

*   **Deficiencies in Deity Proportions:** If the stationary deity is incorrect in proportions (*māna-hīna*), circumambulate the fire, offer oblations as specified for installation, chant the gem-deposit mantras and the installation sūkta, establish the deity, perform the renovation, and consecrate the deity along with the processional icons.

*   **Destruction of the Deity:** If the stationary deity is destroyed, carve a new icon according to the rules, using the same material or a superior material, and perform the consecration. If the icon is damaged by humans, bulls, or elephants, perform the water-reclining for purification, deposit gems at the site, and perform the consecration.

*   **Stolen and Sculptor's Icons:** If a stone icon completed and worshiped in another temple is stolen, bury it in the ground, bring it out according to the rules, perform the remaining rites, and install it. If the sculptor carves an icon after performing the stone-quarrying rites and wishes to install it, bring it according to the rules and perform the consecration.

*   **Moving the Deity:** A defectless icon installed according to the rules in its proper place must not be moved. If it is moved due to ignorance, it leads to misfortune and destruction of the king and the state. To pacify that sin, perform the great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*) in the five fires, feed one hundred Brahmins, donate gold and cows, and perform the consecration again. If the icon was installed by unqualified priests who do not know the rules, remove it quickly and have it installed by qualified priests who know the rules. If an icon installed by qualified priests is moved, perform the peace ceremony, worship the Vaiṣṇavas, and place it back in its proper place according to the rules.

*   **Natural Calamities:** If the temple or the deity is moved by river floods, lake erosion, sea storms, or cyclones, bury the icon in the ground, rebuild the temple at that site as before, and install the stationary deity firmly. Alternatively, if there is a village nearby, build a superior temple in a secluded and beautiful place, bring the deity along with His retinue (*saparivāra*), install Him, and worship Him. If the village is abandoned, build a temple in a spacious place at a distance of more than one hundred *daṇḍas* (bow-lengths), and install the deity.

*   **Spoils of War:** If the king conquers another country and wishes to bring and install their deity, he should layout a village in a beautiful area in his own country, build a temple in the designated वास्तु section, install the deity according to the rules, and perform the worship.

*   **Worship at Ancient Sites:** If one wishes to build a human/new temple near a self-manifested or ancient temple, he can build a temple and a representation inside or outside its walls without the need for site purification (*bhū-śuddhi*), and install and worship it. The worship of that deity yields the same fruit as worshiping at the original ancient site—so says Marīci.
