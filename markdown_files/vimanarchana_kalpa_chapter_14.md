# Vimanarchana Kalpa of Sage Marīci (Vaikhānasa Āgama)
## Chapter 14: Navavidha-Parivāra-Kramaḥ (The Nine Grades of Retinue Shrines and Priestly Establishments)

This document contains the Sanskrit text, IAST transliteration, and English translation of the 14th Chapter (*Caturdaśaḥ Paṭalaḥ*) of the **Vimānārcanākalpa** (attributed to Sage Marīci, representing the Vaikhānasa school of Vaishnavism).

This chapter defines the nine hierarchical grades of retinue deity configurations (*navavidha-parivāra*), ranging from the basic 5-deity setup up to the elaborate 82-deity configuration. It also details the nine grades of priestly and attendant appointments (*arcaka-paricāraka*) and the rules for land endowments (*bhūmyādi-dāna*) to ensure the continuity of worship.

---

### Section 1: The Nine Classes of Retinue Deities (Navavidha-parivāra-vidhiḥ)

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ चतुर्दशः पटलः**
> **परिवाराणां नवभेदविधिः**
> ***atha caturdaśaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***parivārāṇāṃ navabhedavidhiḥ***
> 
> अथ परिवाराणां नवभेदविधिं वक्ष्ये - आलयान्तर्गताः देवाः, द्वारदेवाः, द्वारपालाश्च सर्वत्र समानाः उच्यन्ते।
> *atha parivārāṇāṃ navabhedavidhiṃ vakṣye - ālayāntargatāḥ devāḥ, dvāradevāḥ, dvārapālāś ca sarvatra samānāḥ ucyante.*
> 
> 1.  **अधमाधमम् (पञ्च परिवाराः):** विष्वक्सेन-गरुड-श्रीभूत-न्यक्ष-भूतपीठानि; एते पञ्च परिवाराः।
> 2.  **अधममध्यमम् (षोडश परिवाराः):** एतैः सार्धम् इन्द्रादि-दिग्देवाः, भास्कर-प्रमुखे चक्र-शङ्खौ च; एते षोडश परिवाराः।
> 3.  **अधमोत्तमम् (एकविंशति परिवाराः):** एतैः सहिताः विवस्वन्-मित्र-क्षत्तारः, अभिमुखे ध्वज-यूथाधिपौ च; एते एकविंशति देवाः।
> 4.  **मध्यमाधमम् (द्वात्रिंशत् परिवाराः):** एतैः सहिताः सिद्धि-श्री-हविष्पालक-स्कन्द-विघ्नेश-सप्तमातृ-पुरुष-ज्येष्ठा-भार्गव-ब्रह्म-चन्द्राश्च; एते द्वात्रिंशद् देवाः।
> 5.  **मध्यममध्यमम् (द्विचत्वारिंशत् परिवाराः):** एतैः सह तृतीयावरणे भास्कर-अङ्गारक-स्कन्द-दुर्गा-सप्तरोहिणी-मन्द-सप्तमातृ-बुध-बृहस्पति-पुरुषाश्च; एते द्विचत्वारिंशद् देवाः।
> 6.  **मध्यमोत्तमम् (द्विपञ्चाशत् परिवाराः):** एतैः सह ज्येष्ठा-शुक्र-गङ्गा-सप्तर्षि-ब्रह्म-चन्द्र-रुद्र-अश्वि-कपिल-पञ्चभूत-सरस्वत्यश्च; एते द्विपञ्चाशद् देवाः।
> 7.  **उत्तमाधमम् (द्विषष्टि देवाः):** एतैः सह चतुर्थावरणे पृथिवी-धात्री-सुरप्रिया-पुण्या-व्याहृति-दशभव-मनु-हरि-शार्ङ्गाश्च; एते द्विषष्टि देवाः।
> 8.  **उत्तममध्यमम् (द्विसप्तति देवाः):** एतैः सह एकादशरुद्र-द्वादशादित्याः; एते द्विसप्तति देवाः।
> 9.  **उत्तमोत्तमम् (द्व्यशीति देवाः):** एतैः सह बलिरक्षक-वसु-जयाः दश; एते द्व्यशीति देवाः।
> *1.  **adhamādhamam (pañca parivārāḥ):** viṣvaksena-garuḍa-śrībūta-nyakṣa-bhūtapīṭhāni; ete pañca parivārāḥ.*
> *2.  **adhamamadhyamam (ṣoḍaśa parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ sārdham indrādi-digdevāḥ, bhāskara-pramukhe cakra-śaṅkhau ca; ete ṣoḍaśa parivārāḥ.*
> *3.  **adhamottamam (ekaviṃśati parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ sahitāḥ vivasvan-mitra-kṣattāraḥ, abhimukhe dhvaja-yūthādhipau ca; ete ekaviṃśati devāḥ.*
> *4.  **madhyamādhamam (dvātriṃśat parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ sahitāḥ siddhi-śrī-havişpālaka-skanda-vighneśa-saptamātṛ-puruṣa-jyeṣṭhā-bhārgava-brahma-candrāś ca; ete dvātriṃśad devāḥ.*
> *5.  **madhyamamadhyamam (dvicatvāriṃśat parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ saha tṛtīyāvaraṇe bhāskara-aṅgāraka-skanda-durgā-saptarohiṇī-manda-saptamātṛ-budha-bṛhaspati-puruṣāś ca; ete dvicatvāriṃśad devāḥ.*
> *6.  **madhyamottamam (dvipañcāśat parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ saha jyeṣṭhā-śukra-gaṅgā-saptarṣi-brahma-candra-rudra-aśvi-kapila-pañcabhūta-sarasvatyaś ca; ete dvipañcāśad devāḥ.*
> *7.  **uttamādhamam (dviṣaṣṭi parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ saha caturthāvaraṇe pṛthivī-dhātrī-surapriyā-puṇyā-vyāhṛti-daśabhava-manu-hari-schārṅgāś ca; ete dviṣaṣṭi devāḥ.*
> *8.  **uttamamadhyamam (dvisaptati parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ saha ekādaśarudra-dvādaśādityāḥ; ete dvisaptati devāḥ.*
> *9.  **uttamottamam (dvyaśīti parivārāḥ):** etaiḥ saha balirakṣaka-vasu-jayāḥ daśa; ete dvyaśīti devāḥ.*
> 
> अभिमुखे (प्रतिमुखे) अनपायिनः; द्वारे धात्रादीन्, प्राकार-द्वारेषु द्वारपालकान् यथोक्ते देशे सम्यग् अर्चयेत्।
> *abhimukhe anapāyinaḥ; dvāre dhātrādīn, prākāradvāreṣu dvārapālakān yathokte deśe samyag arcayet.*

#### English Translation
*   **The Nine Classes of Retinue Deities (*Navavidha-parivāra-vidhiḥ*):**
    *   Now, I shall describe the rules for the nine grades of retinue deities (*navavidha-parivāra*).
    *   The deities residing within the temple, the portal deities, and the door guardians are described as common to all setups.
    *   **1. Adhamādhama (Inferior-Inferior - 5 deities):** Settle **Viṣvaksena**, **Garuḍa**, **Śrībūta**, **Nyakṣa**, and the offering altar (**Bhūtapīṭha**).
    *   **2. Adhamamadhyama (Inferior-Middling - 16 deities):** Settle the above five plus the eight directional deities starting with **Indra**, **Bhāskara** (Sun), **Cakra**, and **Śaṅkha**.
    *   **3. Adhamottama (Inferior-Superior - 21 deities):** Settle the above sixteen plus **Vivasvat**, **Mitra**, **Kṣattā**, and facing the temple: **Dhvaja** (flagstaff) and **Yūthādhipa**.
    *   **4. Madhyamādhama (Middling-Inferior - 32 deities):** Settle the above twenty-one plus **Siddhi**, **Śrī**, **Havirpālaka**, **Skanda**, **Vighneśa**, the **Seven Mothers** (*Saptamātṛkās*), **Puruṣa**, **Jyeṣṭhā**, **Bhārgava**, **Brahmā**, and **Candra**.
    *   **5. Madhyamamadhyama (Middling-Middling - 42 deities):** Settle the above thirty-two, and in the third enclosure: **Bhāskara**, **Aṅgāraka**, **Skanda**, **Durgā**, the **Seven Rohiṇīs**, **Manda** (Śani), the **Seven Mothers**, **Budha**, **Bṛhaspati**, and **Puruṣa**.
    *   **6. Madhyamottama (Middling-Superior - 52 deities):** Settle the above forty-two plus **Jyeṣṭhā**, **Śukra**, **Gaṅgā**, the **Seven Sages** (*Saptarṣis*), **Brahmā**, **Candra**, **Rudra**, the **Aśvins**, **Kapila**, the **five elements** (*pañca-bhūta*), and **Sarasvatī**.
    *   **7. Uttamādhama (Superior-Inferior - 62 deities):** Settle the above fifty-two, and in the fourth enclosure: **Pṛthivī**, **Dhātrī**, **Surapriyā**, **Puṇyā**, **Vyāhṛti**, **Daśabhava**, **Manu**, **Hari**, and **Śārṅga**.
    *   **8. Uttamamadhyama (Superior-Middling - 72 deities):** Settle the above sixty-two plus the **eleven Rudras** and the **twelve Ādityas**.
    *   **9. Uttamottama (Superior-Superior - 82 deities):** Settle the above seventy-two plus **Balirakṣaka**, the **eight Vasus**, and **Jaya**.
    *   Worship the everlasting deities (*anapāyinaḥ*) facing the temple, **Dhātā and others** at the main entrance, and the door-guardians at the gates of the enclosure walls in their respective designated spots properly.

### Section 2: Priest and Attendant Specifications (Arcaka-paricāraka-vibhāgaḥ)

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> नवविध-अर्चक-परिचारकान् वक्ष्ये -
> *navavidha-arcaka-paricārakān vakṣye -*
> 
> 1.  **अधमाधमम्:** मन्त्रकल्पवित् शौचाचार-संयुक्तः सुपुष्टाङ्गः ऋत्विगुक्त-गुणोपेतः एकः अर्चकः, द्वौ परिचारकौ च।
> 2.  **अधममध्यमम्:** द्वौ अर्चकौ, चत्वारः परिचारकाश्च।
> 3.  **अधमोत्तमम्:** त्रयः अर्चकाः, नव परिचारकाश्च।
> 4.  **मध्यमाधमम्:** चत्वारः अर्चकाः, षोडश परिचारकाश्च।
> 5.  **मध्यममध्यमम्:** षड् अर्चकाः, पञ्चविंशति परिचारकाश्च।
> 6.  **मध्यमोत्तमम्:** अष्टौ अर्चकाः, चत्वारिंशत् परिचारकाश्च।
> 7.  **उत्तमाधमम्:** द्वादश अर्चकाः, पञ्चाशत् परिचारकाश्च।
> 8.  **उत्तममध्यमम्:** षोडश अर्चकाः, चतुष्षष्टि परिचारकाश्च।
> 9.  **उत्तमोत्तमम्:** विंशति अर्चकाः, अशीति परिचारकाश्च।
> *1.  **adhamādhamam:** mantrakalpavit śaucācāra-saṃyuktaḥ supuṣṭāṅgaḥ ṛtvigukta-guṇopetaḥ ekaḥ arcakaḥ, dvau paricārakau ca.*
> *2.  **adhamamadhyamam:** dvau arcakau, catvāraḥ paricārakāś ca.*
> *3.  **adhamottamam:** trayaḥ arcakāḥ, nava paricārakāś ca.*
> *4.  **madhyamādhamam:** catvāraḥ arcakāḥ, ṣoḍaśa paricārakāś ca.*
> *5.  **madhyamamadhyamam:** ṣaḍ arcakāḥ, pañcaviṃśati paricārakāś ca.*
> *6.  **madhyamottamam:** aṣṭau arcakāḥ, catvāriṃśat paricārakāś ca.*
> *7.  **uttamādhamam:** dvādaśa arcakāḥ, pañcāśat paricārakāś ca.*
> *8.  **uttamamadhyamam:** ṣoḍaśa arcakāḥ, catuṣṣaṣṭi paricārakāś ca.*
> *9.  **uttamottamam:** viṃśati arcakāḥ, aśīti paricārakāś ca.*
> 
> देवेशस्य प्रतिसन्ध्यम् एकैकः पुरुषः; तथा बेरं प्रति पूजार्थम् अर्चकाः परिचारकाश्च बहवो भवन्ति।
> *deveśasya pratisandhyam ekaikaḥ puruṣaḥ; tathā beraṃ prati pūjārtham arcakāḥ paricārakāś ca bahovo bhavanti.*

#### English Translation
*   **Priest and Attendant Specifications (*Arcaka-paricāraka-vibhāgaḥ*):**
    *   Now, I shall describe the nine categories of priests and attendants (*arcaka-paricāraka*):
    *   **1. Adhamādhama:** 1 priest, who is well-versed in *mantra-kalpa*, pure in conduct, robust of limb, and endowed with the qualities of an officiating priest (*ṛtvij*), and 2 assistants.
    *   **2. Adhamamadhyama:** 2 priests and 4 assistants.
    *   **3. Adhamottama:** 3 priests and 9 assistants.
    *   **4. Madhyamādhama:** 4 priests and 16 assistants.
    *   **5. Madhyamamadhyama:** 6 priests and 25 assistants.
    *   **6. Madhyamottama:** 8 priests and 40 assistants.
    *   **7. Uttamādhama:** 12 priests and 50 assistants.
    *   **8. Uttamamadhyama:** 16 priests and 64 assistants.
    *   **9. Uttamottama:** 20 priests and 80 assistants.
    *   For the Lord of Gods, there must be at least one person for each juncture of the day (*pratisandhyam*). Furthermore, for each installed icon (*bera*), there should be many priests and attendants dedicated to its worship.

### Section 3: Land and Endowment Gifts to Priests and Assistants (Ācāryādīnāṃ bhūmyādi-dānam)

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> नित्य-नैमित्तिकार्थम् आचार्य-अर्चक-आदीनां जीवनार्थं, अन्य-विभव-वृद्ध्यर्थं (अन्य-दिव्य-विभावार्यार्थं) च अत्यन्त-पुष्कलान् भूमि-भोगान् आचार्यस्य हस्ते देव-सन्निधौ यजमानः सोदकं दद्यात्। सर्वम् आचार्य-नियोगेन कारयेत्।
> *nitya-naimittikārtham ācārya-arcaka-ādīnāṃ jīvanārthaṃ, anya-vibhava-vṛddhyarthaṃ ca atyanta-puṣkalān bhūmibhogān ācāryasya haste devasannidhau yajamānaḥ sodakaṃ dadyāt. sarvam ācārya-niyogena kārayet.*
> 
> आचार्य-अर्चक-आदयः क्लेश-युक्ताश्चेद्, जनाः सर्वे सर्वदा क्लेश-युक्ताः भवेयुः; राज्ञः राष्ट्रस्य च महद् भयं, ग्राम-यजमानयोः नाशश्च भवेत्। तस्मात् सर्व-प्रयत्नेन आचार्य-अर्चक-आदीनां जीवनार्थं भूमिभोगं सुसम्पन्नं कल्पयेत्।
> *ācārya-arcaka-ādayaḥ kleśayuktāś ced, janāḥ sarve sarvadā kleśayuktāḥ bhaveyuḥ; rājñaḥ rāṣṭrasya ca mahad bhayaṃ, grāmayajamānayor nāśas ca bhavet. tasmāt sarvaprayatnena ācārya-arcaka-ādīnāṃ jīvanārthaṃ bhūmibhogaṃ susampannaṃ kalpayet.*
> 
> आचार्य-अर्चक-परिचारक-आदीनाम् अभावे तत्-पुत्रादीन्; तदभावे तन्-नियुक्तान् कारयेत्। अन्यथा महत्तरो दोषो भवति इति विज्ञायते॥
> *ācārya-arcaka-paricāraka-ādīnām abhāve tatputrādīn; tadabhāve tanniyuktān kārayet. anyathā mahattaro doṣo bhavati iti vigñāyate.*

#### English Translation
*   **Land and Endowment Gifts to Priests and Assistants (*Ācāryādīnāṃ bhūmyādidānam*):**
    *   For the performance of daily (*nitya*) and occasional (*naimittika*) rituals, for the sustenance of the teacher, priests, and attendants, and for the overall progress of the temple, the patron (*yajamāna*) should solemnly offer very abundant land endowments (*bhūmi-bhogān*) with water (*sodakam*) into the hands of the teacher in the presence of the deity.
    *   Everything must be carried out in accordance with the direction of the teacher (*ācārya-niyogena*).
    *   If the teacher, priests, and others are in distress, all the people in the land will suffer distress at all times. Settle a great fear upon the king and the state, and ruin upon the village and the patron. Therefore, with all effort, establish well-endowed land holdings for the livelihood of the teacher, priests, and attendants.
    *   In the absence of the teacher, priests, or attendants, their sons should take up the service; in their absence, appoint those selected by them (*tanniyuktān*). Otherwise, a very serious defect arises, as is well known.
