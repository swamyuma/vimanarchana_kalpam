# Chapter 62: Expiations for Omission of Stationary Deity Installation (Dhruvabera-sthāna-hīnādau prāyaścittam)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules and Expiatory Rites for Delays and Deficiencies in the Installation of the Stationary Deity

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ द्विषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **ध्रुवबेरस्थानहीनादि-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिः -**
> ***atha dviṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***dhruvaberasthānahīnādi-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ -***
> 
> **(ध्रुवबेरस्थापने हीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> समाप्ते विमाने मासाद् ऊर्ध्वं ध्रुवबेरस्थापने हीने वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, रौद्रं, ऐन्द्रं, आग्नेयं, वारुणं, बार्हस्पत्यं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च, शान्तिं हुत्वा; वैष्णवान् सम्पूज्य, आचार्याय दक्षिणां दत्त्वा, ब्राह्मणान् भोजयित्वा आरभेत ॥
> ***(dhruvaberasthāpane hīne prāyaścittam)***
> *samāpte vimāne māsād ūrdhvaṃ dhruvaberasthāpane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, raudraṃ, aindraṃ, āgneyaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, bārhaspatyaṃ, śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ ca, śāntiṃ hutvā; vaiṣṇavān samupūjya, ācāryāya daxiṇāṃ dattva, brāhmaṇān bhojayitvā ārabhet.*
> 
> मासद्वयातीते द्विगुणम्।
> *māsadvayātīte dviguṇam.*
> 
> त्रिमासे अतीते त्रिगुणम्।
> *trimāse atīte triguṇam.*
> 
> एवं संवत्सरान्तं वर्धयेत्। संवत्सरे अतीते तु महत्तरो दोषो भवति।
> *evaṃ saṃvatsarāntaṃ vardhayet. saṃvatsare atīte tu mahattaro doṣo bhavati.*
> 
> **(जलाधिवासात् पूर्वम् अङ्गहानौ प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> जलाधिवासात् पूर्वं ध्रुवबेरस्य अङ्गहानिश्चेत् (मुद्रितपाठे ध्रुवबेरस्यां गहानिश्चेत्), वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, 'दद्भ्यः स्वाहा' इति अङ्गहोमं जुहुयात्।
> ***(jalādhivāsāt pūrvam aṅgahānou prāyaścittam)***
> *jalādhivāsāt pūrvaṃ dhruvaberasya aṅgahāniścet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ, 'dadbhyah svāhā' iti aṅgahomaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> आचार्याय दक्षिणां दत्त्वा, सन्धानयोग्यं चेत् सन्धानं कुर्यात्, इतरथा अपरं (नवीनं) बेरं समाहरेत्।
> *ācāryāya daxiṇāṃ dattva, sandhānayogyaṃ cet sandhānaṃ kuryāt, itarathā aparaṃ beraṃ samāharet.*
> 
> **(अविधिना कृते बेरे प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> शिलाग्रहणक्रियां विना बेरं सलक्षणं शिल्पिना कृतं चेत्, वैष्णव-शान्तिं (मुद्रितपाठे वैष्ण-वशांतं) हुत्वा, तद् बेरं भूमौ पिधाय (निखन्य), वास्तुहोमं हुत्वा, पर्यग्नि-पञ्चगव्याभ्यां संशोध्य, तद् बेरं परितः पूर्ववद् बलिं दत्त्वा, देवान् समाराध्य, पश्चात् समन्त्रकं सङ्गृह्य विधिना स्थापयेत्।
> ***(avidhinā kṛte bere prāyaścittam)***
> *śilāgrahaṇakriyāṃ vinā beraṃ salaxaṇaṃ śilpinā kṛtaṃ cet, vaiṣṇava-śāntiṃ hutvā, tad beraṃ bhūmou pidhāya, vāstuhomaṃ hutvā, paryagni-pañcagavyābhyāṃ saṃśodhya, tad beraṃ paritaḥ pūrvavad baliṃ dattva, devān samārādhya, paścāt samantrakaṃ saṅgṛhya vidhinā sthāpayet.*
> 
> **(जलाधिवासात् परम् अङ्गहानौ प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> जलाधिवासात् पश्चात् स्थापनात् पूर्वं (मुद्रितपाठे स्थापनावत्पूर्वं) देव-देव्यादीनाम् अङ्गहानिश्चेत्, वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीसूक्तं, महीसूक्तं, ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, मुन्योर्मन्त्रं च हुत्वा; तद् अङ्गहीनं सन्धानं कृत्वा, अयुक्तं चेत् विधिवत् त्यक्त्वा, पुनर्बेरं समाहृत्य स्थापयेत्।
> ***(jalādhivāsāt param aṅgahānou prāyaścittam)***
> *jalādhivāsāt paścāt sthāpanāt pūrvaṃ deva-devyādīnām aṅgahāniścet, vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrīsūktaṃ, mahīsūktaṃ, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, munyormantraṃ ca hutvā; tad aṅgahīnaṃ sandhānaṃ kṛtvā, ayuktaṃ cet vidhivat tyaktvā, punarberaṃ samāhṛtya sthāpayet.*
> 
> **(प्रतिष्ठायाः परं संस्कारहीने प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> स्थापिते महाबेरे मासाद् ऊर्ध्वं संस्कारहीने वा, अब्जाग्नौ (सभ्याग्नौ) वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, ब्राह्मं, रौद्रं, पारिषदं हुत्वा आरभेत।
> ***(pratiṣṭhāyāḥ paraṃ saṃskārahīne prāyaścittam)***
> *sthāpite mahābere māsād ūrdhvaṃ saṃskārahīne vā, abjāgnou vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ, brāhmaṃ, raudraṃ, pāriṣadaṃ hutvā ārabhet.*
> 
> मासद्वये अतीते द्विगुणं, मासत्रये त्रिगुणं, एवं संवत्सरान्तं वर्धयेत्।
> *māsadvaye atīte dviguṇaṃ, māsatraye triguṇaṃ, evaṃ saṃvatsarāntaṃ vardhayet.*
> 
> संवत्सरान्तं पुनः स्थापयेत् (पुनःप्रतिष्ठां कुर्यात्)।
> *saṃvatsarāntaṃ punaḥ sthāpayet.*
> 
> द्वादशे वर्षे अतीते विमानस्य कर्षणादि-पुनस्संस्कारं, बेरस्य शिला-शूल-ग्रहणं, अन्यत् सर्वं च कृत्वा पुनः प्रतिष्ठां कारयेत् इति आह मरीचिः ॥
> *dvādaśe varṣe atīte vimānanya karṣaṇādi-punassaṃskāraṃ, berasya śilā-śūla-grahaṇaṃ, anyat sarvaṃ ca kṛtvā punaḥ pratiṣṭhāṃ kārayet iti āha marīciḥ.*

#### English Translation
*   **Delay in Stationary Deity Installation:** If after the temple is completed, a month passes without the installation of the stationary deity (*dhruva-bera*), offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Raudra, Aindra, Āgneya, Vāruṇa, Bārhaspatya, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and perform the peace ceremony (*śānti-homa*). Worship the Vaiṣṇavas, give donations to the priest (*ācārya*), feed the Brahmins, and then begin the installation.

*   **Increasing Penalties for Delay:** If two months pass, double the offerings; if three months pass, triple the offerings. Thus, increase the offerings up to one year. If a year passes, a severe fault arises.

*   **Damage to Limb before Water-Reclining:** If before the water-reclining (*jalādhivāsa*), a limb of the stationary deity is damaged (*aṅga-hāni*), offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and the limb-offering (*aṅga-homa*) reciting "Dadbhyah svāhā". Give donations to the priest. If it is repairable, repair it; otherwise, bring another icon.

*   **Icon Carved Without Quarrying Rites:** If the sculptor carves an icon without the proper stone-quarrying rituals (*śilā-grahaṇa*), offer the Vaiṣṇava peace oblations, bury the icon in the ground, perform the Vāstu-homa, purify the site with fire and *pañcagavya*, offer boundary oblations around the buried icon, worship the gods, and then gather materials with mantras and install a new icon according to the rules.

*   **Limb Damage after Water-Reclining:** If after the water-reclining but before the installation, a limb of the Lord or the Goddesses is damaged, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, Śrīsūkta, Mahīsūkta, Brāhma, Raudra, and Sage-mantra oblations. Repair the damaged limb. If it is unfit, discard it according to the rules, bring another icon, and install it.

*   **Neglect of Installed Deity:** Once the main deity (*mahā-bera*) is installed, if a month passes without daily worship or purification rites, kindle the Sabhya fire (*abjāgni*), offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā, Brāhma, Raudra, and Pāriṣada oblations, and restart the worship. If two months pass, double the offerings; if three months, triple; increase up to one year. After a year, perform the installation again. If twelve years pass without worship, perform the plowing, quarrying, wooden core installation, and complete re-consecration of the temple and the icon—so says Marīci.
