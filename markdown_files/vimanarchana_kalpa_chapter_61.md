# Chapter 61: Expiatory Rites for Site Examination to Temple Completion (Bhū-parīkṣādi-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ)
### Section 1: Liturgical Rules and Expiatory Ceremonies (Prāyaścitta) from Site Selection to Temple Dedication

#### Sanskrit (Devanagari) & Transliteration (IAST)
> **अथ एकषष्टितमः पटलः**
> **भूपरीक्षादि-प्रायश्चित्त-विधिः -**
> ***atha ekaṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ***
> ***bhūparīkṣādi-prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ -***
> 
> **(भूपरीक्षादौ प्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> अथ भूपरीक्षादि प्रायश्चित्तविधिं व्याख्यास्यामः — न्यूनातिरिक्त-व्याघातेषु प्रायश्चित्तं न कुर्याच्चेत्, विनाशाय भवति। तस्मात् प्रायश्चित्तं समाचरेत्।
> ***(bhūparīkṣādou prāyaścittam)***
> *atha bhūparīkṣādi prāyaścittavidhiṃ vyākhyāsyāmah — nyūnātirikta-vyāghāteṣu prāyaścittaṃ na kuryāccet, vināśāya bhavati. tasmāt prāyaścittaṃ samāceret.*
> 
> प्रायः इति शब्देन दोष-संपातानां, चित्तम् इति तस्य प्रतिषेधः; तस्मात् प्रायश्चित्तम् इति उच्यते।
> *prāyah iti śabdena doṣa-saṃpātānāṃ, cittam iti tasya pratiṣedhah; tasmāt prāyaścittam iti ucyate.*
> 
> भूपरीक्षादि क्रियां यस्मिन् अहनि कारयेत्, तस्मिन् अहनि तदर्थं गमनकाले विघ्नश्चेत्, वैष्णवं वैघ्न्यं च जप्त्वा (मुद्रितपाठे जत्वा); दुर्वाक्ये श्रुते ब्राह्मं सारस्वतं च जप्त्वा; देवं ध्यायान् गच्छेत्।
> *bhūparīkṣādi kriyāṃ yasmin ahani kārayet, tasmin ahani tadarthaṃ gamanakāle vighnaścet, vaiṣṇavaṃ vaighnyaṃ ca japtvā; durvākye śrute brāhmaṃ sārasvataṃ ca japtvā; devaṃ dhyāyān gacchet.*
> 
> तत्काल-कलह-रुधिरस्राव-अग्निदाहेषु वैष्णवं, सौम्यं, वैष्वक्सेनं (मुद्रितपाठे वैष्णक्सेनं), सौदर्शनं, गारुडं च लौकिकाग्नौ हुत्वा, पुनरारभेत।
> *tatkāla-kalaha-rudhirasrāva-agnidāheṣu vaiṣṇavaṃ, soumyaṃ, vaiṣvakseaṇaṃ, soudarśanaṃ, gāruḍaṃ ca loukikāgnou hutvā, punarārabhet.*
> 
> भूपरीक्षादिकाले तत्-पांसुक्षये पूर्णाहुतिं (मुद्रितपाठे पूर्णाहृतिं) मिन्दाहुतिं च जुहुयात्। तत्-तद्-अभिमन्त्रक्रमेण आहुतं चेत्, तस्मिन् शुद्धे देशे च औपासनाग्निं आधाय; वैष्णवं व्याहृत्यन्तं दशांशो जुहुयात्।
> *bhūparīkṣādikāle tat-pāṃsuxaye pūrṇāhutiṃ mindāhutiṃ ca juhuyāt. tat-tad-abhimantrakrameṇa āhutaṃ cet, tasmin śuddhe deaśe ca aupāsanāgniṃ ādhāya; vaiṣṇavaṃ vyāhṛtyantaṃ daśāṃśo juhuyāt.*
> 
> कर्षणकाले वृषभे अङ्गहीने रौद्रं, ब्राह्मं; हलादीनाम् उक्तवृक्षालाभे प्रमाणहीने च वैष्णवं, सौम्यं, आग्नेयं, प्राजापत्यं च; रज्जुच्छेदे वारुणं; सीतायां श्रीदैवत्यं वात्रे ज्येष्ठामन्त्रं, वृषे वायव्यं, अक्षशङ्खे वासव्यं (मुद्रितपाठे अक्षिष्यांवासवं) युगे शेषमन्त्रं, प्रतोदे याम्यं, व्याहृतीपर्यन्तं जुहुयात्।
> *karṣaṇakāle vṛṣabhe aṅgahīne raudraṃ, brāhmaṃ; halādīnām uktavṛxālābhe pramāṇahīne ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, soumyaṃ, āgneyaṃ, prājāpatyaṃ ca; rajjucchēde vāruṇaṃ; sītāyāṃ śrīdaivatyāṃ vātre jyeṣṭhāmantraṃ, vṛṣe vāyavyaṃ, axaśaṅkhe vāsavyaṃ yuge śeṣamantraṃ, pratode yāmyaṃ, vyāhṛtīparyantaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> **(कर्षणकाले शुद्धिः)**
> कर्षणकाले कपाल-अस्थि-तुष-केश-वल्मीक-पङ्क-सङ्करादि-दर्शने तद् व्यपोह्य; तस्मिन् पञ्चगव्येन अभ्युक्ष्य; सौम्यम् आग्नेयं वैष्णवं च जुहुयात्।
> ***(karṣaṇakāle śuddhiḥ)***
> *karṣaṇakāle kapāla-asthi-tuṣa-keaśa-valmīka-paṅka-saṅkarādi-darśane tad vyapōhya; tasmin pañcagavyena abhyuxya; soumyam āgneyaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> विष्वक्सेन-अनपायिनोः (मुद्रितपाठे विष्वक्सेनानपयिनोः) अर्चनाहीने वैष्णवं तद्-दैवत्यं (मुद्रितपाठे तद्देवल्यं) च हुत्वा पुनरर्चयेत्।
> *viṣvakseaṇa-anapāyinoh arcanāhīne vaiṣṇavaṃ tad-daivatyāṃ ca hutvā punararcayet.*
> 
> बीजावापने हीने वैष्णवं, सौम्यं, वायव्यं हुत्वा; बीजदानं कुर्यात्।
> *bījāvāpane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, soumyaṃ, vāyavyaṃ hutvā; bījadānaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> गोगण-निवेदने हीने वैष्णवं, वैष्वक्सेनं, गारुडं, सौदर्शनं च हुत्वा; पलालभारान् तृणानि वा आस्तीर्य गोगणेभ्यो निवेदयेत्।
> *gogora-nivedane hīne vaiṣṇavaṃ, vaiṣvakseaṇaṃ, gāruḍaṃ, soudarśanaṃ ca hutvā; palālābhārān tṛṇāni vā āstīrya gogorēbhyo nivedayet.*
> 
> पददेवताबली हीने विपरीते वा तत्-पददेवतामन्त्रपूर्वं वैष्णवं ब्राह्मं, वासवं, याम्यं, वारुणं, कौबेरं च हुत्वा आाराध्य; तथैव बलिमर्चयेत्।
> *padadevatābalī hīne viparīte vā tat-padadevatāmantrapūrvaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ brāhmaṃ, vāsavaṃ, yāmyaṃ, vāruṇaṃ, kauberaṃ ca hutvā ārādhya; tathāiva balimarcayet.*
> 
> ब्रह्मपद्यावटक्रियायां हीनायां कार्यम् आरब्धं; वैष्णवं ब्राह्मं वारुणं महीदैवत्यं हुत्वा आरभेत।
> *brahmapadyāvaṭakriyāyāṃ hīnāyāṃ kāryam ārabdhaṃ; vaiṣṇavaṃ brāhmaṃ vāruṇaṃ mahīdaivatyāṃ hutvā ārabhet.*
> 
> शकुनपरिच्छेदहीने दिग्दैवत्यं आदित्यं वैष्णवं च हुत्वा, दिक्-परिच्छेदं कुर्यात्।
> *śakunapariccedahīne digdaivatyāṃ ādityaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā, dik-pariccedaṃ kuryāt.*
> 
> **(खननादौ शुद्धिः)**
> विमानार्थं भूखननकाले शल्य-लोष्ट-पाषाणादि-दर्शने वैष्णवं ब्राह्मं पञ्चभूमिदैवत्यं व्याहृतीः (मुद्रितपाठे व्याहृत्यन्तैश्च) च जुहुयात्।
> ***(khananādou śuddhiḥ)***
> *vimānārthaṃ bhūkhananakāle śalya-loṣṭa-pāṣāṇādi-darśane vaiṣṇavaṃ brāhmaṃ pañcabhūmidaivatyāṃ vyāhṛtīḥ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> आद्येष्टिका-शिलायाः वा प्रमाणे हीने अधिके च वैष्णवं विष्णुसूक्तं च जुहुयात्।
> *ādyeṣṭikā-śilāyāḥ vā pramāṇe hīne adhike ca vaiṣṇavaṃ viṣṇusūktaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> तासाम् अग्रविपर्यासे अधोमुखे च दिग्दैवत्यं वैष्णवं च हुत्वा, देवानाम् आदिमं त्रीन् जपेत्।
> *tāsām agraviparyāse adhomuṣe ca digdaivatyāṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ ca hutvā, devānām ādimaṃ trīn japet.*
> 
> तस्मिन् रत्नन्यासे विहीने विष्णुसूक्तं जुहुयात्।
> *tasmin ratnanyāse vihīne viṣṇusūktaṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> गर्भन्यासे विहीने वैष्णवं विष्णुसूक्तं श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा; विधिना गर्भन्यासं कारयेत्।
> *garbhanyāse vihīne vaiṣṇavaṃ viṣṇusūktaṃ śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ ca hutvā; vidhinā garbhanyāsaṃ kārayet.*
> 
> गर्भभाजने प्रमाणहीने अधिके च सौरं, सौम्यं, आग्नेयं च यजेत्।
> *garbhabhājane pramāṇahīne adhike ca souraṃ, soumyaṃ, āgneyaṃ ca yajet.*
> 
> द्रव्याणां सङ्करे स्थानविपर्यासे च वैष्णवं तत्-तत्-स्थानाधिदैवत्यं च जुहुयात्।
> *dravyāṇāṃ saṅkare sthānaviparyāse ca vaiṣṇavaṃ tat-tat-sthānādhidaivatyāṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> अनुक्ते देशे न्यस्तं चेत्, वैष्णवं विष्णुसूक्तं पुरुषसूक्तं श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा, तत्-तत्-स्थाने निवेशयेत्।
> *anukte deaśe nyastaṃ cet, vaiṣṇavaṃ viṣṇusūktaṃ puruṣasūktaṃ śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ ca hutvā, tat-tat-sthāne niveśayet.*
> 
> मूर्ध्नेष्टिका-शिला-प्रमाणहीने (मुद्रितपाठे मूर्धेटकाशिलाप्रमाणेहीने) अधिके च आद्येष्टिकावत् प्रायश्चित्तं समाचरेत्।
> *mūrdhneṣṭikā-śilā-pramāṇahīne adhike ca ādyeṣṭikāvat prāyaścittaṃ samāceret.*
> 
> स्थूपिकीले नभस्तारे (मुद्रितपाठे स्थूपिकीलेनभातारे) च प्रमाणहीने उक्तवृक्षालाभे च वैष्णवं, सौम्यं, आग्नेयं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा; विधिना कारयेत्।
> *sthūpikīle nabhastāre ca pramāṇahīne uktavṛxālābhe ca vaiṣṇavaṃ, soumyaṃ, āgneyaṃ, śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ ca hutvā; vidhinā kārayet.*
> 
> **(समाप्तविमाने शुद्धिः)**
> विमाने समाप्ते मासाद् ऊर्ध्वं कलशक्रिया (मुद्रितपाठे कलभक्रिया) सुधावर्ण-अनुलेपनेषु हीनेषु वैष्णवं, विष्णुसूक्तं, पुरुषसूक्तं, श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा आरभेत। आग्नेयं भूमिदैवत्यं च हुत्वा; विधिना कारयेत्।
> ***(samāptavimāne śuddhiḥ)***
> *vimāne samāpte māsād ūrdhvaṃ kalaśakriyā sudhāvarṇa-anuleapaneṣu hīneṣu vaiṣṇavaṃ, viṣṇusūktaṃ, puruṣasūktaṃ, śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ ca hutvā ārabhet. āgneyaṃ bhūmidaivatyāṃ ca hutvā; vidhinā kārayet.*
> 
> आलयस्य कवाटे हीने धात्रादि-षड्दैवत्यं वैष्णवं श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं च जुहुयात्।
> *ālayasya kavāṭe hīne dhātrādi-ṣaḍdaivatyāṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> प्राकार-गोपुरादिषु हीनेषु महत्तरो दोषो भवेत् — वंशहानिः शत्रुवृद्धिश्च भवेत्; विष्वक्सेने हीने कुलोत्सादनं; भूतपीठे हीने धन-धान्य-आयुष्य-हानिः; कूप-आराम-महानस-सपुष्पसञ्चय-स्नानोदक-पानीयस्थान-स्नपनआलय-आस्थानमण्डपादिषु (मुद्रितपाठे मंटपादिषु) हीनेषु तत् सर्वं निष्फलं भवति। तस्मात् सर्वप्रयत्नेन कारयेत्। वैष्णवं विष्णुसूक्तं पुरुषसूक्तं श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं तत्-तद्-अधिदैवत्यं जुहुयात् ॥
> *prākāra-gopurādiṣu hīneṣu mahattaro doṣo bhavet — vaṃśahānih śatruvṛddhiśca bhavet; viṣvakseaṇe hīne kulotsādanaṃ; bhūtapīṭhe hīne dhana-dhānya-āyuṣya-hānih; kūpa-ārāma-mahānasa-sapuṣpasañcaya-snānōdaka-pānīyasthāna-snapanālayā-āsthānamaṇḍapādiṣu hīneṣu tat sarvaṃ niṣfalaṃ bhavati. tasmāt sarvaprayatnena kārayet. vaiṣṇavaṃ viṣṇusūktaṃ puruṣasūktaṃ śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ tat-tad-adhidaivatyāṃ juhuyāt.*
> 
> **(अनुभूप्रायश्चित्तम्)**
> प्रथमेष्टकादि-स्थूपिकील-विन्यासान्तम् अनुक्तप्रायश्चित्तं वक्ष्ये — तद्-आलयात् पुरतः दक्षिणे वा पञ्चाग्नौ वैष्णवं विष्णुसूक्तं पुरुषसूक्तं श्रीभूमिदैवत्यं, स्थूप्याद्युपाननान्तरङ्गे नामभिः अङ्गहोमं 'दद्भ्यः स्वाहा' दीन्, ब्राह्मं रौद्रं वैष्णवं आग्नेयं दुर्गासूक्तं प्राजापत्यं च जुहुयात्।
> ***(anubhūprāyaścittam)***
> *prathemēṣṭakādi-sthūpikīla-vinyāsāntam anuktaprāyaścittaṃ vaxye — tad-ālayāt puratah daxiṇe vā pañcāgnou vaiṣṇavaṃ viṣṇusūktaṃ puruṣasūktaṃ śrībhūmidaivatyāṃ, sthūpyādyupānanāntaraṅge nāmabhiḥ aṅgahomaṃ 'dadbhyah svāhā' dīn, brāhmaṃ raudraṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ āgneyaṃ durgāsūktaṃ prājāpatyaṃ ca juhuyāt.*
> 
> समाप्ते विमाने अज्ञानाद् अर्थलोभाद् वा ध्रुवबेरं विना कौतुकं स्थापितं चेत्, तद् आभिचारिकं भवति; ग्रामस्य यजमानस्य राज्ञो राष्ट्रस्य च विनाशो भवति। तस्मात् महाशान्तिं त्र्यहं हुत्वा, वैष्णवान् सम्पूज्य, ब्राह्मणान् भोजयित्वा, 'क्षमस्व' इति देवं प्रार्थ्य, बालालये प्रतिष्ठाप्य, ध्रुवबेरं ध्रुवार्चाबेरं वा स्थापयेत् ॥
> *samāpte vimāne ajñānād arthalobhād vā dhruvaberaṃ vinā kautukaṃ sthāpitaṃ cet, tad ābhicārikaṃ bhavati; grāmasya yajamānasya rājño rāṣṭrasya ca vināśo bhavati. tasmāt mahāśāntiṃ tryahaṃ hutvā, vaiṣṇavān sampūjya, brāhmaṇān bhojayitvā, 'xamasva' iti devaṃ prārthya, bālālaye pratiṣṭhāpya, dhruvaberaṃ dhruvārcāberaṃ vā saṃsthāpayet.*

#### English Translation
*   **Definition of Prāyaścitta (Expiation):** Now, we shall explain the rules for expiatory rites starting with the site examination (*Bhū-parīkṣā*): if one does not perform expiations for deficiencies, excesses, or obstacles, it leads to destruction. Therefore, one must perform expiatory rites. The word *prāya* denotes errors/sins, and *citta* denotes their containment/prevention; hence it is called *prāyaścitta* (expiation).

*   **Obstacles during Travel or Site Selection:** On the day when the site examination is to be performed, if obstacles arise during the journey to the site, chant the Vaiṣṇava and Vaighnya (obstacle-removing) mantras. If bad words are heard, chant the Brāhma and Sārasvata mantras, and proceed while meditating on the Lord. If at that time quarrels, blood-flow, or fire outbreaks occur, offer oblations in the secular/common fire with the Vaiṣṇava, Saumya, Vaiṣvaksena, Saudarsana, and Gāruḍa mantras, and then restart the work.

*   **Errors during Site Selection and Plowing:** If during the site examination, dust is depleted, offer the *pūrṇāhuti* (complete offering) and *mindāhuti* (remedial offering). In that purified site, having kindled the Aupāsana fire according to the specific mantras, offer Vaiṣṇava and Vyāhṛti oblations, one-tenth of the normal amount. If during plowing, the bull is injured, offer the Raudra and Brāhma oblations. If the specified wood for the plow and other tools is unavailable or incorrect in size, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Saumya, Āgneya, and Prājāpatya oblations. If the rope breaks, offer the Vāruṇa oblation. For the furrow (*sītā*), offer the Śrī-mātra and Jyeṣṭhā-mantra oblations. For the bull, offer the Vāyavya oblation. For the axle, offer the Vāsava oblation. For the yoke, offer the Śeṣa-mantra. For the goad (*pratoda*), offer the Yāmya oblation, concluding with the Vyāhṛti.

*   **Impurity and Ritual Omissions:** If during plowing, skulls, bones, husks, hair, anthills, or mud impurities are seen, remove them, sprinkle the site with *pañcagavya* (five cow products), and offer the Saumya, Āgneya, and Vaiṣṇava oblations. If the worship of Viṣvaksena and Anapāyin is omitted, offer the Vaiṣṇava and their specific deity-mantras, and worship them again. If the sowing of seeds (*bījāvāpa*) is deficient, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Saumya, and Vāyavya oblations, and perform the seed-offering. If the offering of cows is omitted, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Vaiṣvaksena, Gāruḍa, and Saudarśana oblations, spread straw or grass, and offer them to the cows.

*   **Omissions in Altar and Boundary Rites:** If the offerings to the padadevatās (deities of the grid) are omitted or performed in reverse order, offer oblations with the specific deity-mantras preceded by the Vaiṣṇava, Brāhma, Vāsava, Yāmya, Vāruṇa, and Kaubera mantras, worship them, and offer the boundary oblations (*bali*). If the pit-digging ritual for the Brahmā-pada is deficient, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Brāhma, Vāruṇa, and Mahī-devatā oblations, and start the work again. If the determination of omens is omitted, offer the Digdevatā, Āditya, and Vaiṣṇava oblations, and perform the boundary determination.

*   **Errors in Excavation and Foundation Stones:** If during excavation for the temple foundation, bones, pebbles, or stones are found, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Brāhma, Pañcabhūmi-devatā, and Vyāhṛti oblations. If the foundation stone (*ādyeṣṭikā*) or stone slab (*śilā*) is incorrect in measurement (too small or too large), offer the Vaiṣṇava and Viṣṇu-sūkta oblations. If their orientation is reversed or they are placed upside down, offer the Digdevatā and Vaiṣṇava oblations, and chant the three primeval mantras of the gods. If the deposit of gems (*ratna-nyāsa*) is omitted, offer the Viṣṇu-sūkta. If the deposit of the womb (*garbha-nyāsa*) is omitted, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and perform the garbha-nyāsa according to the rules. If the womb-vessel (*garbha-bhājana*) is incorrect in size (too small or too large), worship with the Saura, Saumya, and Āgneya mantras. If materials are mixed or locations are swapped, offer the Vaiṣṇava and the specific deity-mantras of those locations. If an icon is placed in an unspecified location, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and place it in the correct location.

*   **Errors in Final Construction Rites:** If the top-stone (*mūrdhneṣṭikā*) is incorrect in size, perform the expiation as for the foundation stone (*ādyeṣṭikā*). If the peg of the finial (*sthūpi-kīla*) is incorrect in size or the specified wood is unavailable, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Saumya, Āgneya, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and perform the installation according to the rules. If the temple is completed, but after a month, the plastering, coloring, or painting are deficient, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and begin the work. Offer the Āgneya and Bhūmi-devatā oblations, and perform the work. If the temple door is deficient, offer oblations to the six deities starting with Dhātṛ, and the Vaiṣṇava and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations.

*   **Consequences of Neglecting Minor Structures:** If the walls (*prākāra*), gateways (*gopura*), and other structures are deficient, severe faults arise: destruction of the lineage and increase of enemies. If the worship of Viṣvaksena is omitted, the family is destroyed. If the pedestal of spirits (*bhūta-pīṭha*) is deficient, wealth, grain, and lifespan are lost. If the well, garden, kitchen, flower-garden, bathing-place, water-tank, bathing-hall, or assembly-hall are deficient, all results become fruitless. Therefore, one must build them with all efforts. Offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā, and their specific deity-mantras.

*   **General Expiatory Rites (*Anubhū-prāyaścitta*):** I shall now describe the general expiations for any unspecified errors from the foundation stone (*prathameṣṭakā*) to the finial-peg (*sthūpi-kīla*): to the front or right of the temple, in the five fires, offer the Vaiṣṇava, Viṣṇu-sūkta, Puruṣa-sūkta, and Śrī-Bhūmi-devatā oblations. Offer limb-offerings (*aṅga-homa*) for the finial (*sthūpi*) and other parts with the "Dadbhyah svāhā" and other mantras, and the Brāhma, Raudra, Vaiṣṇava, Āgneya, Durgā-sūkta, and Prājāpatya oblations.

*   **Severe Fault of Missing the Stationary Deity:** If after the temple is completed, due to ignorance or greed for wealth, the processional icon (*kautuka*) is installed without the stationary deity (*dhruva-bera*), it becomes black magic (*abhicārika*). It leads to the destruction of the village, the patron (*yajamāna*), the king, and the nation. Therefore, perform the great peace ceremony (*mahā-śānti*) for three days, worship the Vaiṣṇavas, feed the Brahmins, pray to the Lord saying "forgive me", and install Him in a temporary temple (*bālālaye*), and then establish the stationary deity or the stationary worship icon.
